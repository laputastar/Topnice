Hi, we are New Horizons Toy, the creative team behind Zombie Cube.

Zombie Cube is our original cube-shaped designer toy IP from Hong Kong. It was designed with a clean geometric form and a simple purpose: to become a physical canvas for art, emotion, and visual storytelling.

For this collaboration, we are working withTimothy Goodman, a New York-based artist whose hand-drawn words and lines have appeared across walls, products, packaging, clothing, and cultural spaces.

The idea behindZombie Cube × Timothy Goodmanis to transform Timothy’s expressive visual language into a physical collectible object.

Just as an illustration can become a print, a mural can become part of a city, and a drawing can become a piece of culture, we believe Timothy’s hand-drawn world can become a 4-inch designer toy — something you can hold, display, and collect.

The figure turns Zombie Cube’s cube head, body, arms, and legs into a three-dimensional surface for artwork. The result is not simply a toy with graphics on it, but a collectible object built around the meeting of form and drawing.

We are planning this release as a special artist collaboration edition, with dedicated packaging and collectible presentation. Kickstarter allows us to introduce it first to people who understand the value of art toys, artist editions, and limited creative objects.

By backing this project, you help us bring a hand-drawn visual world into physical form.

As a heartfelt thank-you to our backers, we've set up two milestones:

When we reach our first milestone — half of our funding goal — every backer who has joined us before that moment will unlock a special mystery gift.

When we reach full funding, every single backer (no matter when they joined) will unlock an exclusive Timothy Goodman co-branded phone strap.

No extra cost. No complicated steps.Just real gifts, unlocked by you — because you brought art to life with us.

Thank you for your support and for being here with us.
