Why We Created HUBi32

Support HUBi32 Today

Imagine a device that combines the power of anESP32-S3 development board, the intelligence of aUSB analyzer, the convenience ofHID automation, the creativity ofmultimedia, and the aesthetics of afuturistic cyberpunk desk companion.

That device isHUBi32.

HUBi32 was created for makers, hackers, embedded developers, robotics enthusiasts, IoT creators, students, and technology lovers who want more than a traditional development board. We wanted to build something that not only helps you create innovative projects but also becomes an interactive part of your workspace.

With real-time USB power monitoring, intelligent USB control, RGB lighting, motion sensing, multimedia capabilities, HID automation, and a fully featured web dashboard, HUBi32 transforms everyday embedded development into an immersive experience.

Powered by the powerful ESP32-S3-WROOM-1 MCU, HUBi32 combines intelligent USB control, real-time power analytics, RGB lighting, motion sensing, multimedia support, HID automation, and a futuristic web dashboard into one compact and expandable platform.

Whether you're building automation systems, robotics projects, multimedia interfaces, cyberpunk desk setups, or advanced IoT applications, HUBi32 delivers a truly unique interactive experience.

From the moment HUBi32 powers on, glowing RGB effects, futuristic TFT animations, real-time USB diagnostics, and interactive motion sensing bring your desk setup to life.

Designed with both functionality and aesthetics in mind, HUBi32 transforms embedded development into an immersive cyberpunk experience.
