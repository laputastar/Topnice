A Cluttered, Uninspired Desk

Is your desk cluttered with chargers, cables, and stands? Power strips overloaded, monitors stuck in one orientation, and ambient lighting requiring extra wiring.

In today’s world, our desks are command centers for productivity and creativity.

Yet, many struggle with desk clutter: tangled cables, limited charging ports, and rigid setups. Traditional arms are just metal poles; they don't solve the "spaghetti wire" mess or provide the ambiance you need.

Are you tired of compromising your setup?

Meet KOVADEX X1

KOVADEX integrates 100W fast charging, full-aluminum dual arms, and 80+ RGB mood lights into one device – one power cord, zero drilling, giving you a clean and productive workspace.

Support is just the foundation –flexibilityis where real performance lives. We use aviation‑grade aluminum alloy, with each arm supporting monitors up to 36 inches. Both arms rotate independently 360°, letting you switch between landscape and portrait in just one second – no need to lift your monitor or re‑route cables. Left screen landscape for coding, right portrait for documents. Landscape for efficient work, portrait for total immersion in gaming – rotate however you like.

The KOVADEX X1 dual‑arm system is built for creators, gamers, and professionals. Each arm features a high‑performance internal spring mechanism for effortless tilting and rotating, delivering a smooth “zero‑gravity” feel that makes adjustments almost weightless.

Crafted entirely from premium aluminum alloy, the KOVADEX X1 Dual Arm Set’s base and stand carry a satisfying weight of5.88 kg. This isn’t just about durability—it’s about the confidence that comes with true stability. Once placed on your desk, it anchors itself firmly, eliminating any wobble or uncertainty, so you can focus entirely on your work.

100W Power Station: The Heart of Your Desk

Stop searching for chargers all over the place, and stop stressing over limited outlets.

KOVADEX X1 features a built-in100Wpower delivery system with 3 USB-C and 1 USB-A ports integrated directly into the base.

Whether it’sfast-charging your MacBook Pro, powering your iPad, keeping your smartphone at 100%, or supplying energy to your gaming gear, controllers, and desktop accessories, everything you needis within arm’s reach. One cable to the wall, four devices on your desk.

80+ RGB Modes: Set the Vibe for Every Moment

Your desk setup should reflect your personality. With over80+ preset RGB lighting modes, the KOVADEX X1 transforms your workspace into a dynamic masterpiece.

From calming "Deep Sea" blues for focused work to high-energy "Cyberpunk" pulses for late-night gaming sessions, you can switch the atmosphere with a single touch.

It’s not just a monitor arm; it’s a statement piece. No extra light strips to buy, no additional power cables to plug in – it’s ready the moment you turn it on.

Magnetic Smart Storage: Clutter-Free, Mind-Free

Clear the clutter, clear your mind. KOVADEX X1 base features two strategically placed magnetic brackets on the sides.

Safely snap your smartphone, game controller, or headphones onto the base to keep your desk surface pristine. These organizers ensure your essential gear is always organized, visible, and ready for action without taking up precious desk real estate.

* Bonus!The two magnetic areas are part of our modular design, so you can easily swap out our included stand and use other compatible accessories.

Invisible Cable Management

Hide the mess. Built-in cable channels within the arms keep your power and display wires completely invisible for a clean, professional look.
