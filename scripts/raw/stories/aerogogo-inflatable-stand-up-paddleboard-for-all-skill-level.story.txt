What's in the Box

Meet Aerogogo AquaVector

Aerogogo introduces AquaVector, an inflatable stand-up paddleboard designed to remove the hassle from every trip on the water.

Powered by our exclusive ultra-mini automatic SUP pump, AquaVector inflates itself quickly and stops automatically at the perfect pressure—no manual pumping, no guesswork, no wasted energy.

Its extra-large 350 cm board provides outstanding stability, helping beginners find their balance with confidence while giving experienced paddlers a smoother, more comfortable ride

Aerogogo AquaVector Inflatable SUP Unbox

Great Balance and Stability

Bigger Board. Better Stability.

Most inflatable SUPs are only 300–330 cm long. Ours extends up to 350 cm, giving you a larger, more stable platform on the water.

The extra length improves tracking, balance, and overall comfort, making it easier for beginners and more enjoyable for families, pets, and gear.

More space. More stability. More confidence on every ride.

Built Tough with Double-Layer PVC

AquaVector is constructed with premium double-layer PVC for exceptional rigidity and long-lasting durability.

Built to handle 15 PSI, the reinforced structure minimizes flex under your feet, providing a firmer, more stable platform for better balance and smoother paddling.

The rugged construction also delivers outstanding air retention and enhanced puncture resistance, making it ready for everything from calm lakes to more demanding river adventures.

Auto Inflate & Auto Stop

Inflate Your SUP at the Push of a Button

Meet the GIGA PUMP 138—our ultra-mini automatic SUP pump that takes the hard work out of setup.

Connect it, set your target pressure, and press start. Your AquaVector inflates to 12 PSI in as little as 10 minutes, then automatically stops when it's ready.

No manual pumping. No guesswork. Just grab your paddle and hit the water.

Designed for More

Thoughtfully Designed for Every Adventure

Every detail of AquaVector is designed to make your time on the water more enjoyable.

Along with the essentials—including a 6-point bungee storage system, comfortable grab handle, and 3-fin setup for improved tracking—AquaVector also features a built-in camera mount to capture every adventure and a fishing rod holder for anglers who want to cast off with confidence.

Whether you're exploring, filming, or fishing, AquaVector is ready for more than just paddling.

Build Your Ultimate Outdoor Setup

AquaVector is just the beginning. Pair it with Aerogogo's outdoor essentials to create the perfect setup for every adventure—from the water to your basecamp.

Our ultra-mini automatic SUP pump inflates your paddle board with the push of a button, so you're ready to hit the water in minutes.

Keep your gear dry on every trip, then use it as a vacuum storage bag to save space when packing for your next adventure.

Transform your paddleboard into a comfortable sit-on-top kayak for longer, more relaxing rides.

Rinse off after swimming, wash away sand and mud, or clean your paddle board before packing it away.

Your adventure doesn't end when you leave the water.

Pair AquaVector with our inflatable Dome Air Tent to create the ultimate lakeside basecamp. Inflate it with the GIGA PUMP 138 in minutes, then unwind, enjoy the sunset, share meals with friends, or camp under the stars.
