Risks and Challenges

Every new project comes with challenges, and we believe in being transparent about them.

The good news is that the Athlete Mindset & Motivation PowerDeck™ is not just an idea—we've already completed the product design, content development, and prototype stage. Kickstarter funding will help us move into production, fulfillment, and broader distribution.

The primary risks involve manufacturing timelines, supply chain delays, shipping costs, and fulfillment logistics. While these challenges can affect almost any physical product, we've intentionally designed the PowerDeck to be simple to manufacture and easy to ship. Unlike complex technology products, the PowerDeck contains no electronics, software development, or specialized components that could create significant production risk.

Our team also brings decades of experience in product development, publishing, business leadership, coaching, and customer fulfillment. Through Play Strong®, we've successfully sold and delivered products to customers in all 50 states and more than 15 countries, serving thousands of athletes, coaches, and sports families over the past two decades.

To reduce risk, we are:

• Working with established printing and production partners

• Building realistic production and delivery timelines

• Maintaining regular communication with backers throughout the process

• Keeping the product intentionally simple and focused

• Using Kickstarter funds primarily for production, inventory, packaging, and fulfillment

As this is our first Kickstarter campaign, we know there will be things to learn along the way. That's one reason we've intentionally set a modest funding goal. Our focus is not on creating the biggest Kickstarter campaign possible—it's on delivering an exceptional product, building trust with our supporters, and laying the foundation for a long-term mission to empower young athletes.

Most importantly, we are deeply committed to fulfilling every reward and delivering on the promises we make to our backers.

Thank you for helping us bring the Athlete Mindset & Motivation PowerDeck™ to life.

YOUTH SPORTS: AN AMAZING OPPORTUNITY

There has never been a more exciting time to be a young athlete.

Today's young athletes have access to better coaching, better facilities, better equipment, and more opportunities than ever before. Millions of kids are discovering the joy of sports, while coaches and parents are investing countless hours to help them grow and succeed.

At its best, youth sports is about so much more than wins and losses.

It's where young people learn confidence, perseverance, teamwork, leadership, resilience, and character. It's where lifelong friendships are formed, families make lasting memories, and coaches have the privilege of shaping lives—not just athletes.

We believe youth sports is one of the greatest opportunities to help young people become stronger, more confident, and better prepared for life.

THE HIDDEN CHALLENGE

While today's young athletes have more opportunities than ever before, many are also facing more pressure than ever before.

Young athletes are navigating self-doubt, fear of failure, comparison, performance anxiety, social media, unrealistic expectations, and the pressure to always perform. Too often, these invisible opponents become harder to overcome than the competition itself.

Most young athletes spend countless hours training their bodies—practicing skills, building strength, and improving performance.

But very few are ever taught how to train their minds.

Mental toughness, confidence, resilience, focus, leadership, and a positive mindset are not traits athletes are simply born with. Like athletic skills, they can be developed through consistent practice.

We believe the inner game deserves the same intentional training as the outer game.

That's why we created theAthlete Mindset & Motivation PowerDeck™—a simple daily mindset training system that helps young athletes build confidence, strengthen character, develop resilience, and become the best version of themselves, both on and off the field.

What if every young athlete had a positive mindset coach in their pocket?
