Risks and challenges
This is Adoraboard's first group buy. I've been transparent about that from the start — and I want to be equally transparent here.

Manufacturing: I have an established relationship with my keycap manufacturer and a confirmed cost per unit. That said, production timelines can shift. If there are delays, I will communicate them early and often — no radio silence.

Shipping: All orders ship from Washington State. Shipping costs are collected separately after the campaign via survey, so you'll know exactly what you're paying before you're charged.

Built & Ready tier: Each keyboard is assembled and tested by hand, one at a time. This tier is capped at 10 units to make sure every single one gets the attention it deserves.
If something goes wrong: Defective or missing keycaps will be replaced. I keep buffer stock for exactly this reason. If there's ever a situation I can't resolve with a replacement, I will issue a refund.

I'm a one-person studio and I care deeply about this set. Your trust means everything to this first run — and to every run after it.

Opening:Most keyboards are built for speed. Botanical Whispers was built for something quieter — the feeling of morning light through a window, a cup of tea going cold, the particular peace of having somewhere soft to put your hands while you think.

Every key in this set carries a detail. A pressed sprig on Tab. A verse tucked into Shift. The words "Botanical Whispers" hidden on E, where most people's fingers rest longest. These aren't decorations. They're small discoveries — the kind you find after months of typing, when the light catches at just the right angle.

About Adoraboard:Adoraboard is a one-person studio making keyboards for people who want their desk to feel like a place they actually want to be. This is our first group buy. Everything here was designed, prototyped, and photographed by hand.

Environmental commitments

Long-lasting design

PBT keycaps don't shine, fade, or wear. Hot-swappable switches mean you can always mod later — this is the only keyboard you'll ever need.

Reusability and recyclability

Keycaps and switches are fully swappable across keyboards. Nothing gets thrown away when you want something new — just swap.

Sustainable Distribution

Small batch, made to order. We only produce what's funded — zero overproduction, zero waste sitting in a warehouse; orders are consolidated and shipped in bulk via sea freight rather than individual air shipments — reducing emissions and cost per unit.
