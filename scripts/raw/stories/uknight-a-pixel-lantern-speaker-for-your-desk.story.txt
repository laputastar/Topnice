Project Timeline

Most desktop speakers are just black boxes that play sound.

UKNIGHT was built to feel alive.

Part retro gadget, part ambient lantern, part pixel-art desk companion — UKNIGHT transforms your workspace, bedside table, gaming setup, or camping corner into something with personality. The glowing twin lanterns pulse beside your music, the pixel display quietly keeps time, and the top platform turns your favorite minifigure into the “pilot” of your own tiny atmosphere machine.

This isn’t just a Bluetooth speaker.

It’s the centerpiece your desk has been missing.

A Speaker Designed Like a Sci-Fi Artifact

UKNIGHT doesn’t look like traditional audio gear because it wasn’t designed like one.

Inspired by retro-futuristic consoles, pixel displays, and miniature exploration vehicles, the entire form factor was crafted to feel collectible before it even powers on. The glowing side lanterns create the silhouette of a tiny rover, while the front pixel display brings back the charm of old-school digital devices — only reimagined for modern setups.

Whether it sits beside your keyboard, under your monitor, or hanging inside a tent at night, UKNIGHT instantly changes the atmosphere around it.

Bluetooth 6.0 That Feels Instant

A desktop speaker should disappear between you and your music.

That’s why UKNIGHT uses Bluetooth 6.0 for faster pairing, lower latency, stronger stability, and lower power consumption. Pairing takes seconds, and once connected, the speaker automatically synchronizes time with your phone so the built-in pixel clock always stays accurate.

With a signal-to-noise ratio above 90dB, audio stays clean and stable whether you’re gaming, working, relaxing, or listening late at night.

A desktop spacecraft with orbital light wings

Inspired by VTOL aircraft and orbital satellites, UKNIGHT’s twin rotating lanterns are designed like miniature light engines. Each module can be adjusted independently, letting you direct light exactly where you need it — across your keyboard, bedside table, display shelf, or campsite.

From focused task lighting to soft ambient glow, UKNIGHT adapts to your space with smooth 10%–100% brightness control and two lighting modes: Constant Light for visibility and Breathing Light for atmosphere.

With up to 200 lumens of illumination, it’s more than a speaker — it’s a portable atmosphere engine for your desk, room, and outdoor nights.

The Pixel Screen Gives It a Soul

Most speakers stay visually silent when nothing is playing.

UKNIGHT keeps living on your desk.

The retro pixel display shows time, animated equalizer visuals, and playful interface elements inspired by classic digital devices. It feels less like a speaker and more like a tiny desktop companion quietly reacting to your environment.

The soft pixel lighting is clear without being aggressive, making it perfect for night use beside monitors, beds, or gaming setups.

When connected to your phone, the clock automatically synchronizes time over Bluetooth, so it’s always accurate without manual adjustment.

Your Desk Toy Becomes Part of the Story

The top-mounted minifigure platform might be the most fun feature on the entire device.

Attach your favorite building-block minifigure and UKNIGHT instantly becomes a customizable display piece. The figure transforms from decoration into a “pilot” for the speaker itself, turning the whole product into a miniature scene straight out of a sci-fi movie.

Every setup becomes personal.

A gaming desk.A creator workspace.A collector shelf.A cozy bedside corner.

UKNIGHT was designed to blend sound, light, and personality together into one object.

Compact Enough to Carry Anywhere
