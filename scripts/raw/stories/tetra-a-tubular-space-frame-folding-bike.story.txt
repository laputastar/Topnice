From Shape to Function

What’s a tetrahedron? The name may sound complicated, but we see these structures all the time in our daily lives.

To put it simply, a tetrahedron is when 4 triangles come together to form a triangular pyramid.

The inherent rigidity of these structures make tetrahedrons a common feature of spaceframes. But we haven’t yet seen these shapes used in folding bike frames… until now.

Tetra is more than just a beautiful bike. This is the future of performanceandconvenience, made by hand with human ingenuity.

Patented Tetra-fold System

A bike that folds once lets you minimize storage space in cramped urban spaces. But a bike that folds beyond the ordinary?

We’ve just opened a brand new realm of possibilities.

First, the rear wheel folds forward, reducing vertical bulk by half.

Then, the bridge folds backward, further reducing the vertical bulk.

For our third fold, the long triangular piece slots into the front, acting as a 3rd hinge. This is part of Analog Machine Works’ patented design innovation.

Finally, the handlebar folds downwards, completing the Tetra-fold process.

Your bike is now folded into a highly compact size for easy transport and storage.

Linkage-shock System, High Quality Suspension

Most foldable bikes use plastic blocks instead of a real shock absorber. But our unique, patented linkage-shock system lets us use a high quality hydraulic shock absorber that intuitively folds with your bike.

Thanks to its high-quality suspension, riding with Tetra is as smooth as ever. Feel for yourself how true quality makes a difference for your compact folding bike.

Tubular Space Frame

To bring our folded-yet-powerful structures to life, the tubular space frame was a natural choice.

At its core, Tetra is a precise hinge arrangement that requires powerful moving parts. The tubular space-frame structure was essential to bringing this vision to life.

Now, after years of refinement, we’ve successfully engineered this complex structure to preserve rigidity, all while achieving a distinctive and elegant form.

You’ve never rode a bike like this before.

The Tetra-fold System™ is our patented design innovation that allows for one additional fold in the bridge of the bike.

The bridge is comprised of 2 opposite-facing tetrahedrons. These pieces come together during the folding process, folding the bridge completely and enabling our 3rd key fold.

Linkage-Shock System ™

Tetra’s shock absorber is linked to the rear wheel, allowing the two parts to fold together in what we call a Linkage-Shock System™. This is what lets us use a much better suspension than conventional folding bikes.

Step 1. Swing the rear wheel down.Step 2. Lower the seatpost.Step 3. Release the two clamp levers and swing the front wheel aside.Step 4. Align the wheels side by side.Step 5. Fold the handlebar down to complete the folding process.
