Key Innovations, In One View.

Eight things that make MemoMind One different — before we show you each one in depth.

These Are Glasses. First.

Most smart glasses look like tech. MemoMind One looks like — and feels like — the glasses you already wear.

- Frames you'd want to wear

- A fit that forgets itself

~175° natural curvature. Flexible hinges. Core components balanced behind the ears. 46.6 g — closer to regular glasses than a gadget.

- Individualized prescription lenses

A Display Worth Wearing.

Dual Micro-LED projectors hold a 2000-nit image five meters in front of you — readable in full sun, and angled so your screen stays yours alone.

Clarity, second to none — and invisible to anyone but you.

- 2000 nits   Bright all day

Dual Micro-LED. Readable in full sunlight.

- 1-5m Away    Adjustable Display

Float from 1-5m in front of you. Adjust height, depth, and brightness to your comfort.

- Minimized Light Leakage

Privacy-focused AR optical design. Downward light, high-transparency grating.

No Camera. No Compromises On Privacy.

Most smart glasses today center around a camera. MemoMind One doesn't have one. That's the headline — but privacy runs deeper than the hardware.

Built-in Harman AudioEFX Speaker.

One Charge. One Full Day.

Designed For Real Life.

AI that works with you. Quietly, yet powerfully.

Ask a question, and answers appear right in your glasses — giving you subtle cues and seamless hands-free support throughout the day.

- AI Translator

Real-time translation in 26 languages. Listen-in mode for passive translation, Dialog mode for back-and-forth.

Record a meeting, a lecture, or a walk. Get a structured AI Summary in seconds. Choose glasses or phone as your audio source to get meeting organized.

- AI Teleprompter

Live subtitles in your line of sight. Switch between AI Prompts and Real-Time Summaries.

Destination in your line of sight. Walking or cycling directions, saved places, clean interface.

Schedule, To-Do, and Suggested — a quiet calendar that knows what today needs.

Catch fleeting ideas the moment they appear. AI clusters them into themes over time, so half-formed thoughts find each other.

- A dashboard that's yours.

- A full app. iOS & Android.
