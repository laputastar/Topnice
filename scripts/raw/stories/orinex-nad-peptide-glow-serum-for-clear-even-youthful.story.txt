Risks and challenges
“Skincare is a regulated category, and there are a few key risks with ORINEX NAD+ Peptide glow Serum. First, I’m working with a U.S. lab and manufacturer, and delays in raw materials, testing, or production could push back delivery timelines. Second, while the formula is being developed with experienced chemists and standard cosmetic safety testing, individual skin types can react differently, so results will vary and some users may experience irritation. Third, bringing a new brand into retailers like Amazon requires strict compliance and documentation; if there are rule changes or extra review, it could slow down how fast we scale.
To reduce these risks, I’m partnering only with established, GMP‑compliant labs, building extra time into the production schedule, and keeping backers updated at every major milestone. If anything changes with timelines or regulations, I will communicate clearly and adjust fulfillment plans to make sure backers either receive their rewards or are offered a fair solution.”

Story / Why ORINEX NAD+

I’m a single mom of three building a skincare brand the hard way: between school drop‑offs, warehouse runs, and late nights studying ingredient decks and Amazon data. When I looked at the top NAD+ and peptide serums on Amazon, I saw the same pattern over and over: pretty packaging, big promises, and formulas that didn’t feel designed for people dealing with visible texture, marks from past breakouts, and uneven‑looking tone while still wanting to age well.

ORINEX NAD+ Peptide glow Serum was born from that gap. I wanted a “grown woman” formula: NAD+‑inspired ingredients to support a healthy‑looking glow, targeted peptides to help skin look firmer and smoother, and brightening cosmetic actives that help improve the appearance of dark spots and post‑blemish marks while respecting the look of the skin’s barrier. Not a 10‑step routine, not a trendy ingredient of the month – one hero serum that fits busy life and helps skin look more even, refined, and well‑rested over time.

I’m using Kickstarter to fund my first full production run of 5,000 units: custom bottles, lab‑grade filling, stability and safety testing appropriate for a cosmetic product, and compliant packaging so ORINEX can also live on Amazon and other major retailers. Backers will directly help cover production, testing, and shipping – and in return, you’ll be the first to get ORINEX NAD+ at launch‑only pricing, plus have a voice in future products in the line.

If you’ve ever felt like the skincare industry talks at you instead of building with you, this project is for you.
