Risks and challenges
Bringing a handcrafted, ecosystem-based product to life comes with unique challenges — but we’ve designed this project with reliability and scalability in mind.

Each terrarium involves a detailed manual crafting process, including sculpting, finishing, and system integration. As demand grows, maintaining consistent quality while scaling production is a key focus. To address this, we’ve already standardized our production workflow and are working with experienced manufacturing partners to ensure stable output and quality control.

Another challenge lies in the integration of the smart misting system. To ensure long-term reliability, we have tested the system across extended usage scenarios, optimizing for durability, consistency, and low noise performance.

Logistics and international shipping are also factors we take seriously. We are developing protective packaging solutions and working with trusted fulfillment partners to minimize the risk of damage during transit.

While unexpected delays can occur, we are committed to transparent communication and will keep all backers updated throughout the production and delivery process.

what's in the box

WildForm is more than a terrarium. It is a living landscape inspired by nature itself — a handcrafted ecosystem terrarium that combines realistic mountain rockwork with an integrated humidity system.

Designed for both reptile habitats and planted displays, WildForm creates a more natural, more immersive, and more convenient environment for your animals and plants.

Unlike ordinary enclosures with visible spray tubes and awkward add-ons, WildForm’s misting nozzle is seamlessly embedded into the mountain structure. The result is a clean, realistic look that feels like part of the landscape, not an accessory attached to it.

Integrated Humidity System

WildForm includes a built-in misting system that helps maintain stable humidity inside the tank.

With scheduled misting, you can reduce the need for daily manual spraying and create a more consistent environment for humidity-loving species and moisture-dependent plants.

Two Misting Modes

WildForm supports both continuous misting and intermittent misting, giving users flexibility to match different animal or plant care routines.

Continuous misting for stronger humidity support

Intermittent misting for natural rain-forest style cycles

Quiet Operation

The misting system is designed for silent or near-silent operation, so it will not disturb your pets, your room, or your sleep. This is especially important for nocturnal reptiles and quiet indoor spaces.

More Natural for Reptiles

Many reptiles respond better to stable humidity, natural-looking terrain, and less frequent disturbance.

WildForm helps create a habitat that feels closer to an animal’s native environment while reducing the need to open the enclosure repeatedly.

Bring the Rainforest Indoors

WildForm is not only for reptiles. It is also ideal for planted ecosystems, tropical moss setups, and humidity-loving terrarium plants.

The misting system helps create a moist microclimate that supports healthy growth and a lush rainforest look.

Hidden Reservoir, Natural Appearance

The integrated water reservoir is cleverly concealed within the handcrafted rockscape, preserving the clean and natural appearance of the habitat.

When it's time to refill, simply lift the hidden cover, add water, and close it again. No exposed tanks, no external containers, and no visible equipment disrupting the beauty of the landscape.

For backers who already own a terrarium, the misting system can also be offered separately as a reward. This gives customers the option to upgrade an existing setup and build their own custom ecosystem.
