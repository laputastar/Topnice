One Cable Any Laptop

Unlike most portable monitors, Fold 7 supports dual connection modes.

A Fold 7 can be connected tothree different computers at most. The specific connection methods are as follows:

Our product is equipped with a USB C-C cable, a USB A-C cable, an HDMI-mini HDMI cable and a power cord.

① USB C-C cable:three screens are lit through the full-function USB C interface with drivers; Only one screen below is lit up without installing a driver through the full-function USB C interface; Light up the above two screens through the non-full-function USB C interface with the driver; You can't light up any screen through a non-full-featured USB C interface without installing a driver.

② USB A-C cable:connected to the TypeC interface on the side of the display through the USB A interface of the notebook computer, to light up the two screens above; You can't light up any screen without installing a driver through USB A interface.

③ HDMI-mini HDMI cable:Connect to the three mini HDMI interfaces on the side of the monitor through the HDMI interface of the laptop, and light up the three screens respectively without installing drivers.

MacBook + iPad Simultaneous Setup

MacBook Connection:Use a USB data-only cable (USB A-C or non-full-function USB C-C) to link your MacBook.Please note that driver installation is mandatory for this connection to activate the display.

iPad Connection:Connect your iPad directly via a USB-C to Mini HDMI cable. This method is fully plug-and-play and requires no additional drivers.
