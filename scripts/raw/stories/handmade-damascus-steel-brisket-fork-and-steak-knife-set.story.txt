[RISKS & CHALLENGES]
Risks and challenges
Producing handmade Damascus knives demands precision, skilled craftsmanship, and rigorous quality control. While we have solid experience in handcrafted knife production, we believe in full transparency with our backers and want to clearly outline potential challenges and how we are prepared to address them.
Production Delays:
Each knife is individually forged, heat-treated, ground, and polished by hand. If demand exceeds projections, production timelines may extend.
Solution: We are proactively securing additional raw materials and expanding our production capacity to manage increased volume without compromising quality.
Raw Material Availability:
Damascus steel relies on specific high-carbon and low-carbon steel combinations. Broader supply chain disruptions could affect availability.
Solution: We are sourcing and reserving materials in advance of campaign completion to minimize potential delays.
Quality Control:
Because every knife is handmade, slight variations in Damascus patterning and natural wood grain may occur. These variations are part of true craftsmanship and make each piece unique.
Solution: Every knife will undergo strict inspection and performance testing before shipment to ensure it meets our standards for durability, sharpness, and functionality.
Delivery Timeline:
Global shipping conditions can occasionally cause unforeseen delays.
Solution: We are building buffer time into our fulfillment schedule and will maintain consistent communication with backers throughout the process.
Our Commitment:
We are committed to honesty, transparency, and consistent updates from production to delivery. If challenges arise, we will inform backers promptly and provide clear action plans.
Our goal is not simply to deliver a knife set — it is to build long-term trust and lasting relationships with a community that values craftsmanship and integrity.
Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

The Ultimate Damascus Brisket & Steak Set

Every great barbecue tells a story. It begins with patience, skill, and quality ingredients, but the experience is only complete with the right tools. That is why we created the Handmade Damascus Steel Brisket & Steak Set—a premium collection designed for barbecue enthusiasts, home chefs, outdoor adventurers, and anyone who appreciates exceptional craftsmanship.This complete set includes a hand-forged Damascus steel brisket knife, matching brisket fork, premium steak knives, and a handcrafted leather roll kit. Together, they create the perfect combination of preparation, serving, and dining tools for your next feast.

Our journey started with a simple goal: to create a set that performs as beautifully as it looks. Too often, carving knives lack character, steak knives lack quality, and storage solutions are an afterthought. We wanted to build something different—a complete set that combines performance, durability, and timeless craftsmanship.At the heart of every piece is genuine Damascus steel. Each blade is forged from multiple layers of steel, creating the distinctive flowing patterns that have made Damascus steel famous around the world. No two blades are exactly alike, making every set unique. Beyond its striking appearance, Damascus steel offers excellent edge retention, strength, and cutting performance.

The brisket knife is designed to effortlessly slice through smoked brisket, roasts, turkey, ham, and other large cuts of meat. Its long, razor-sharp blade allows for clean, precise cuts that preserve flavor and presentation. Whether you're preparing competition-style barbecue or a family dinner, this knife is built to deliver professional results.The matching brisket fork provides stability and control while carving. Its strong construction securely holds meat in place, making slicing easier, safer, and more accurate. Together, the brisket knife and fork form the perfect carving team.Once the carving is complete, the experience continues at the table with our handcrafted Damascus steel steak knives. Designed to glide through steaks with ease, these knives allow you to enjoy every bite without tearing or crushing the meat.
