Risks and challenges
As with any hardware product, we are actively managing potential risks and challenges to ensure the best possible experience for backers. The compact wearable design requires precise manufacturing to ensure durability, stable connectivity, and consistent quality across all units. We are working closely with our production partners and maintaining strict quality control throughout the entire manufacturing process.

In addition, production timelines may be affected by component availability, tooling progress, quality inspections, and logistics arrangements. We are actively coordinating with our supply chain to minimize potential delays and keep the overall schedule as stable as possible.

The device uses a Type-C connection and is currently being tested across a wide range of smartphones and devices to ensure broad compatibility. We will provide clear compatibility guidance before shipment.

Finally, international fulfillment may be influenced by customs clearance procedures, local regulations, and logistics conditions in different regions. We are working with experienced fulfillment partners and will continue to provide regular updates to backers throughout the process to ensure transparency and smooth delivery.
