What is Yeti Ready?
Yeti Ready is a survival and emergency preparedness brand built for people who refuse to take the apocalypse too seriously.
Every Yeti Ready bug-out bag comes fully loaded with real survival gear — a water filtration straw, emergency bivvy, first aid kit, MRE, Apoca-lipstick, and Squatch Spray — because being prepared shouldn't mean being boring.
Why we built this
Most emergency preparedness gear looks like it was designed by someone who hates fun. Scary. Overwhelming. Zero personality.
We believe being ready for an emergency should feel empowering, not terrifying. So we built a bag that takes survival seriously — but doesn't take itself seriously.
Why back us on Indiegogo?
Early backers get the lowest price these bags will ever be offered. This is your chance to get in at the ground floor of a brand that's doing something different in the preparedness space.
The apocalypse is coming. Might as well be ready — and have fun doing it.
