Risks and challenges
ZHIRO is currently in the final stages of development.
While the core structure and dimensions have been validated, some elements are still being refined.

These may include:
– Final surface finishing
– Hardware adjustments
– Material variations

Manufacturing timelines may also be affected by:
– Supplier scheduling
– Material sourcing
– Quality control requirements

Because certain steps — including polishing, edge finishing, and surface treatment — involve manual refinement, slight variations between units may occur.

This can include:
– Minor differences in finish or edge texture
– Subtle surface marks visible under specific lighting
– Natural variation in acid-etched patterns and material tone

These are considered part of the manufacturing character of the product, not functional defects.

We are working closely with our manufacturing partners to ensure consistency and precision across all units.

If any delays or changes occur, we will communicate them clearly and promptly.
