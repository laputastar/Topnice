What's in the box?

You know the feeling.

The pain: When you're working on something tiny and incredibly precise – like a watch movement, a dial with hairline indices, or a circuit board packed with microscopic components – you can't see enough. You reach for a magnifying loupe or a headband magnifier. But now your eye is pressed against a lens, your depth perception is gone, and you keep bumping the tool into the part you're trying to see. You're still working half-blind.

What if your screwdriver had eyes?

Because the HT01 connects directly to the HT01 App on your phone, tablet, or computer – you don't need a magnifying loupe anymore. The live HD view streams to your screen, letting you see every screw, gear, and circuit trace in crystal-clear, zoomable detail. No headband digging into your temples. No eye strain from squinting through a tiny lens. You simply look at your large, bright screen while your hands work freely.
