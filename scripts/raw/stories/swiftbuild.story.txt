Risks and Challenges
While we are confident in Swiftbuild Pro, bringing a hardware product to life involves inherent risks. Our primary focus is on manufacturing consistency, vacuum system reliability, and long-term durability.

To mitigate these risks, we have already:

Prototyping: Completed multiple stages of functional prototypes to ensure the vacuum pressure meets our high standards.

IP Protection: Applied for key technical patents to protect our unique vacuum architecture.

Sourcing: Pre-selected high-quality components for the high-torque motor and chemical-resistant bottles.

THE BIRTH OF Swiftbuild Pro

Every resin print ends the same way: an incredible model, followed by a job nobody wants. Lifting a resin vat with both hands. Tilting it carefully, hoping nothing spills as you pour the remaining resin back into the bottle. Drips on the bench, on your gloves, and resin left behind in the corners of the vat. Then comes the IPA rinse, every single time.

We tried syringes, funnels, and careful pouring. Nothing was fast, and nothing was truly clean. Resin handling had quietly become the worst part of a hobby we loved.

Introducing Swiftbuild Pro

So we built the tool we wished existed. Swiftbuild Pro is a cordless, handheld pump that draws leftover resin straight from the vat back into the bottle — clean, contamination-free, in under 90 seconds.

Recover Resin in Under 90 Seconds

Finishing a print shouldn't mean a mess. Swiftbuild Pro draws leftover resin straight out of the vat and back into the bottle in under 90 seconds — fast, clean, and contamination-free. No more lifting the vat, no more careful pouring.

One Hand. One Touch

Place the low-profile nozzle into the vat and press the touch control — the built-in pump does the rest. Electronic directional control lets you recover resin one-handed, while your other hand stays free.

A Nozzle That Reaches Every Corner

The low-profile angled tip sits close to the film surface and slips into the tight corners standard pouring always misses. It even lifts small spills and residue off your bench — so you recover more resin and waste less.

Zero Drips, Spotless Bench

A one-way valve inside the nozzle stops resin from flowing back or dripping between transfers. Lift the nozzle away and the flow cuts off instantly — your workspace stays clean, every time.

Recover and Clean — All in One

Once the resin's back in the bottle, run IPA through the same system to flush both the vat and the bottle in a single cycle. One tool handles recovery and cleanup, cutting a whole step out of your workflow.

Swap Bottles in Seconds

The detachable 500ml storage bottles are chemically resistant and built for fast swaps. Change colors or switch resins in seconds — no need to clean the whole system between every change.

Separated Design, Built to Last

The pump body is completely isolated from the resin path, so it never gets gummed up. The modular, resin-free design keeps maintenance simple and the tool reliable, print after print.
