Risks and challenges
At KBDcraft, we believe in radical transparency. Building a modular, brick-built input system like the SAHA presents unique challenges, and we want you to know exactly how we manage them:

Global Logistics
Shipping modular components globally involves navigating complex supply chains. Factors such as international port congestion, customs clearance, and fluctuating shipping rates are outside of our direct control. However, we have partnered with experienced third-party logistics (3PL) providers to optimize our fulfillment process. We will provide tracking information and support to ensure your SAHA kit arrives safely at your doorstep.

Supply Chain & Component Availability
The SAHA system relies on several custom-engineered parts. While we have robust inventory planning in place, global electronic and material shortages can occasionally impact lead times. We have already pre-allocated key components for our initial batches to mitigate this risk. In the rare event of a material delay, we will be transparent about the impact and offer clear alternatives or solutions if necessary.

At KBDcraft, we believe in radical transparency. Building a modular, brick-built input system like the SAHA presents unique challenges, and we want you to know exactly how we manage them:

Global Logistics
Shipping modular components globally involves navigating complex supply chains. Factors such as international port congestion, customs clearance, and fluctuating shipping rates are outside of our direct control. However, we have partnered with experienced third-party logistics (3PL) providers to optimize our fulfillment process. We will provide tracking information and support to ensure your SAHA kit arrives safely at your doorstep.

Supply Chain & Component Availability
The SAHA system relies on several custom-engineered parts. While we have robust inventory planning in place, global electronic and material shortages can occasionally impact lead times. We have already pre-allocated key components for our initial batches to mitigate this risk. In the rare event of a material delay, we will be transparent about the impact and offer clear alternatives or solutions if necessary.

Long-lasting design

As our SAHA system utilizes high-tolerance, brick-built structures, maintaining consistent manufacturing precision is our top priority. While our previous batches have set a high standard, scaling up for a new product line requires rigorous quality control. We have secured long-term partnerships with our specialized manufacturers to ensure every modular component meets our exact specifications. If any unforeseen production delays occur, we commit to proactive communication and detailed updates.
