Blean
Promoting Sustainable and Safe Menstrual Care
Menstruation is a natural part of life that is experienced by a quarter of the global population.
Most people rely on
disposable menstrual products
, but these come with a high environmental cost. In the United States alone, an estimated 19 billion pads and tampons are discarded every year. Many of these products also contain chemicals and synthetic materials that may raise concerns for users’ health and well-being.
Reusable menstrual products, such as
menstrual cups
, offer a more sustainable alternative. Although they are not yet equally accessible to all communities, they can provide substantial environmental benefits, as a single menstrual cup can last between 4 and 6 years. In addition, menstrual cups are typically made from medical-grade silicone, making them a safe and body-friendly option for menstrual care.
A menstrual cup is a flexible, reusable device that is inserted into the vagina to collect menstrual blood. While menstrual cups have proven to be a safe and effective alternative for managing menstruation, they are still used by only a minority of the population. To better understand why, we conducted both
qualitative and quantitative research
with menstrual cup users and menstrual health experts.
Through this research, we identified a widely overlooked problem:
cleaning menstrual cups in public restrooms can quickly become stressful, inconvenient, and unhygienic.
In fact, this challenge is one of the leading reasons for discontinued use and creates a significant barrier for potential new users. Many individuals carry water bottles into restroom stalls or intentionally delay emptying their cups to avoid cleaning them in public, choices that can be uncomfortable and impractical, especially for people with heavy flows.
This is why we decided to develop
Blean
!
Blean allows users to rinse their menstrual cup or disc entirely inside a restroom stall, providing a private, hygienic, and discreet way to manage their period outside the home. Its integrated drainage system and thoughtfully designed cleaning process help eliminate concerns about touching contaminated surfaces or accidentally dropping the cup into the toilet, while allowing users to complete the process comfortably and at their own pace.
By simplifying on-the-go menstrual care,
Blean makes reusable menstrual products easier to integrate into everyday life.
Users can travel, work long shifts, attend school, exercise, or spend time in unfamiliar places without needing to plan their day around restroom access or privacy limitations. The result is greater autonomy, comfort, and confidence during menstruation,
empowering individuals to maintain their routines without compromise.
How does Blean work?
Before entering the restroom stall, fill Blean with water. Once inside, remove your menstrual cup or disc, place it into Blean, and shake for an initial rinse. Then, use the built-in clean water reservoir for a second rinse, leaving your cup or disc ready to be safely and hygienically reinserted. Finally, collapse Blean, pack it away, and continue your day with confidence.
