PLOTARA is a compact CNC plotter designed to make digital fabrication simpler and more accessible.

With its fully 3D-printable structure, beltless mechanical system, magnetic pen-changing mechanism, and plug-and-play electronics, PLOTARA provides an approachable platform for makers, students, educators, and creative workshops.

It is designed to be simple enough for beginners while remaining modular and expandable for advanced users.

✏️ Magnetic Tool Changer

PLOTARA is more than just a plotter.

The optional magnetic multi-pen tool changer allows automatic pen switching during a single drawing session — without additional servos, motors, or complex mechanical systems.

The magnetic pen holder grabs and releases tools automatically while still allowing users to swap pens manually with ease.

Simple enough for beginners, yet modular and expandable for future experimentation and upgrades.

🧩 Modular Pen Station

The tool-changing station is built from small modular units.

Each pen holder can be printed separately and connected side-by-side, allowing users to expand the system only as much as needed — similar to building blocks.

💻 Software & Workflow

PLOTARA works with a variety of free and open-source software tools, allowing users to get started without purchasing additional software licenses.

No proprietary software or subscriptions are required. Simply create or import your artwork, convert it when needed, and send it to the machine.

Detailed setup guides, recommended software lists, and step-by-step tutorials will be provided as part of the project documentation. Users will be able to quickly learn how to prepare files, upload designs, and start drawing with confidence.

By building on widely used open-source tools, PLOTARA remains flexible, accessible, and easy to integrate into creative, educational, and maker workflows.
