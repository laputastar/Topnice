Environmental commitments

Imagine packing everything you want – and still having room to spare. The Seekr Air Pack is the smartest way to travel: an innovative vacuum bag that shrinks your clothes by up to 70%, freeing space for more outfits, the extra pair of shoes, the gifts on the way home – the things that actually make the trip. No more choosing what to leave behind. No more sitting on your suitcase to force it shut. Pack, press, go.

Less air. More room. Same suitcase. Pack your clothes, close the bag, press the button. The Seekr Air Pack pack pulls the air out and shrinks down – freeing up to70%of the space your clothes used to waste. What didn't fit before, fits now.

The magic behind the compression – our built-in pump. No extra device. No kneeling on your bag. Every other vacuum bag needs muscle or a separate pump. The Seekr Air Pack doesn't. The pump livesinside the bag– rechargeable over USB-C, always with you, nothing to lose. One charge = up to20 pump-outs. Empty to full in15 minutes.

Environmental commitments

Long-lasting design

Our project was designed to be durable by choosing a reusable product over a single-use one. Traditional vacuum bags tear and get thrown away – the Seekr Air Pack is made from rugged, tear-resistant TPU and built for daily use over many years. Its built-in, USB-C rechargeable pump replaces disposable alternatives and runs without throwaway batteries. We back every Air Pack with a 2-year warranty because we trust how long it lasts. One bag that holds up for years replaces dozens of cheap, disposable ones – for us, that's the single biggest lever to cut waste.
