The Problem: We've Been Scrolling for Decades

The mouse wheel hasn't changed for 30 years.

We’re still flicking tiny wheels to move through massive amounts of content: documents, spreadsheets, code, timelines, and more.

It’s slow. It’s repetitive. And it’s hard to control, even with infinite scroll wheels.

All those micro-movements waste time and putting constant strain on your fingers.

Scrolling should feel better than this.

The Solution: A New Way to Scroll

Instead of repeatedly flicking a wheel up and down, the Rotary Mouse introduces a continuous rotary motion.

You don’t flick. You rotate with tactile clicks while exercising your finger.

Place your finger on the wheel and turn it like a dial. As you rotate, the screen moves smoothly and precisely, giving you direct, real-time control over speed and direction.

Scroll slowly for precision. Spin faster to move quickly. Stop exactly where you want.
