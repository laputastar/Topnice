Risks and challenges
We’ve spent the past years building strong relationships with trusted manufacturing partners and refining every detail of the project before launch. Prototypes, materials, packaging, and production planning have already been carefully prepared to reduce potential risks as much as possible.

Designed for adventure, versatility, and everyday wear, each watch combines refined design with the spirit of exploration.

After months of development and countless prototypes, we’re proud to introduce the first Ouro Watches collection on Kickstarter.

At the heart of theAmazônia Seriesbeats theMiyota 9039, a premium Japanese automatic movement trusted by watch enthusiasts and independent brands worldwide.

Renowned for its reliability, accuracy, and slim profile, the 9039 features a smooth sweeping seconds hand and approximately42 hours of power reserve, delivering dependable performance day after day.

Designed and manufactured in Japan, this movement combines proven engineering with long-term durability, making it an ideal choice for a watch built to be worn and enjoyed for years to come.

Hi, we’re Brian and David — two Brazilian brothers and the founders of Ouro Watches.

For years, we worked behind the scenes creating products, designs and 3D visuals for other companies and brands. No matter the project, we always treated the work as if it were our own, often going far beyond what was expected.

Over time, we realized we wanted to dedicate that same passion and energy into building something truly ours.

As watch design gradually became part of our daily work, everything started to feel natural. Watches brought together everything we love most: design, detail, functionality and storytelling.

That’s how Ouro Watches was born.

“Ouro” is our family name, inherited from our Amazonian mother. To us, it represents value, intention
