At trade shows, in cafés during overseas business trips, or in busy airport lounges, background noise can quickly become a problem.

In places filled with conversations and environmental noise, it can be harder to hear the other person clearly — and translation accuracy can suffer as a result.

In important meetings and business discussions, poor voice pickup and recognition errors can create unnecessary stress.

Voxera is designed to deliver stable call quality and reliable voice recognition even in noisy environments.

With a 4-microphone setup — 2 omnidirectional microphones and 2 directional microphones — it can better separate the speaker’s voice from surrounding noise.

Advanced noise reduction algorithms help suppress unwanted background noise and extract clearer speech, improving both translation accuracy and call quality at the same time.This creates a smoother, more natural communication experience, even in demanding environments.

Voxera also uses Bluetooth 6.2 technology to ensure a stable connection with low latency.It helps minimize audio dropouts and connection instability during translation, so conversations can stay seamless and reliable.

Voxera combines beamforming technology with AI-based noise reduction algorithms.

By pairing optimized microphone pickup design with advanced voice processing, it helps suppress surrounding noise while enhancing human speech.
