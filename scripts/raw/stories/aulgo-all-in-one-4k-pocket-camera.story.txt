Potential Risks & Mitigation Plans
1. Manufacturing & Supply Chain Risks
Risk
Supplier limitations or material shortages may cause delays in tooling and mass production.
Mitigation
We have secured two backup suppliers for core parts and signed a fixed schedule agreement with our main manufacturer. Regular progress checks will be conducted throughout production to identify and resolve problems early.
2. Technical Performance Risks
Risk
Minor performance inconsistencies may appear in mass-produced units during daily use.
Mitigation
Pre-production prototypes have passed full professional testing to meet all design standards. Strict in-line quality control will be enforced in mass production. Key function tests will be performed per batch, with defective products removed directly to ensure stable quality.
3. Fulfillment & Shipping Risks
Risk
Global logistics pressure and customs clearance issues may lead to international shipping delays.
Mitigation
We work with an experienced global fulfillment partner for crowdfunding campaigns. All pre-shipping preparations will be finished ahead of schedule. Real-time tracking will be offered to all users, and buffer stock will be reserved to handle unexpected delivery issues.
Our team has solid experience in consumer electronics development and production. Comprehensive contingency plans are in place for all foreseeable risks. We will deliver regular progress updates to backers and maintain full transparency to guarantee smooth delivery.
