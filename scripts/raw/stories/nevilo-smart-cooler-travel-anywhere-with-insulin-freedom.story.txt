Meet NEVILO Travel Cooler Case

Powered by advancedupgraded semiconductortechnology, NEVILO eliminates the bulk and noise of traditional compressors to deliver instant,precise cooling from 2–20°Cin aquietandreliable system.

This electronic cooling is combined with Biogel-assisted passive retention in a single cup-shaped travel cooler case that is neither a disposable ice pack nor just an insulated bottle. It is avisible,adjustable,power-readyportablesmart cooler case designed for stable temp-sensitive storage like insulin on the go.

Passive Biogel Cooling— For Transit Best for: flights, commutes, and any situation where you’re on the move.

Before your trip, freeze the included Biogel in your freezer for 10–12 hours. Place the frozen Biogel and your items into the cup, then screw the lid on tightly. In this unpowered state, the vacuum-insulated cup body combined with the Biogel maintains low temperatures for approximately6–8hours.

✅ During this, NEVILO can be safely stored in a backpack or luggage.

Active Cooling Mode— When You’re Stationary Best for: hotel rooms, car rides, office desks, or anywhere you can set it down in open air.

Once the Biogel loses its cooling effect, activate NEVILO’s built-in active cooling mode. NEVILO comes with a built-in 10,000mAh battery that powers the cooling module independently. On a full charge, it maintains your set temperature for approximately6–7hours.

For extended use, we recommend keeping NEVILO plugged in (home outlet, power bank, or car charger) whenever possible. Plugging into external power maximizes your cooling time, providing uninterrupted comfort throughout your journey.

⚠️ Do not place NEVILO inside a closed backpack, bag, or luggagewhile powered on. Blocked vents will cause overheating and may damage the device.

Compact Size, Colossal Capacity

NEVILO Smart Cooler Caseholds up to 7 insulin pens(capacity may vary slightly depending on pen size). Its space-optimized design strikes the perfect balance between compactness and practicality.

With its sleek, lightweight profile, it slips effortlessly into your bag, making daily commutes and travel completely hassle-free.

Visible & Adjustable Temperature Control

Traditional cooler bags leave you guessing.

NEVILO’s real-time LED display shows the current chamber temperature at a glance, so you always know exactly what’s happening inside.

Adjustable Range:Set your target temperature anywherebetween 2°C and 20°C (35.6–68°F).

Precision:The device maintains the set temperature within±1°C.

Pre-cooling Time:When using active cooling, allow15–30 minutesfor the chamber to reach your target temperature before placing items inside.

Celsius / Fahrenheit:Press and hold the mode button for 2 seconds toswitch between °C and °F.
