🔥 Authentic Damascus Craftsmanship

The Reaper is handcrafted fromwelded 1095 high-carbon steel and 15N20 steel, creating true Damascus steel with exceptional performance and character. Each blade isforged using traditional hand toolsandheat temperedto achieve the ideal balance of hardness and toughness.

1095 High-Carbon Steelprovides excellent edge retention and outstanding cutting performance.

15N20 Steeladds toughness, flexibility, and enhanced resistance to chipping.

The Damascus pattern is completely authentic—never engraved, laser etched, or stamped.It is created naturally through the repeated forging and folding of the two steels, making every blade unique with its own one-of-a-kind pattern.

Weight & Balance

⚖️800 g (1.76 lbs) axe headoffers an exceptional balance between striking power and precision. Heavy enough to generate effective splitting force, yet light enough to maintain excellent control and reduce fatigue during extended use. This carefully chosen head weight delivers smooth swing dynamics, improved accuracy, and efficient energy transfer—making it ideal for chopping limbs, processing firewood, and splitting logs with confidence and ease.

🪓21-inch Ashwood handle

crafted for strength, comfort, and durability. Ashwood is prized for its excellent shock absorption and flexibility, helping to reduce vibration and hand fatigue during use. The 21-inch length provides an ideal balance of leverage, control, and maneuverability for both chopping and splitting tasks.
