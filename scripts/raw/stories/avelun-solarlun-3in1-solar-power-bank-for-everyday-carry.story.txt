WHY AVELUN SOLARLUN EXISTS

You packed light. Your battery didn’t.9 out of 10 people have missed a photo, a call, or a trail because their phone died.A regular power bank helps — until it runs out.A traditional solar panel helps — if you are willing to carry the bulk.A small solar power bank looks convenient — but often relies on a tiny solar strip that is too limited for real outdoor charging.

We built Avelun SolarLun so you never have to choose between staying light and staying powered.

MEET AVELUN SOLARLUN

AVELUN SOLARLUNis made of two independent modules that work separately or together.The power bank module stores energy and charges devices anytime.The solar charger module unfolds into a 6-fold flexible solar array and can charge devices directly through Type-C.When snapped together magnetically, the solar panel helps recharge the power bank using sunlight.This gives you more flexibility than a regular power bank, more portability than a traditional solar kit, and more usable solar area than most tiny solar power banks.

ONE SYSTEM. THREE CHARGING MODES.

Most power banks only work until they run out. Most solar chargers only work when the sun is available. SolarLun brings both together in one modular system.

Use stored power anytime. Charge devices directly from sunlight. Snap the battery onto the panel to recover energy outdoors. One compact system gives you more ways to stay powered when outlets are not nearby.

POWER WHEN THE SUN ISN’T AROUND

When sunlight is not available, use SolarLun as a portable power bank. The detachable 7,800mAh battery module gives you reliable backup power for phones, cameras, earbuds, lights, and other everyday devices.

POWER DIRECTLY FROM THE SUN

When sunlight is available, SolarLun works as a standalone solar charger. Unfold the 6-fold solar panel, connect your device through the USB-C output, and use sunlight to power phones, GPS devices, cameras, USB lights, and other small electronics.

This mode is ideal when you want to save the battery module for later, charge during the day, or keep essential devices running while you are outdoors.

SOLAR RECOVERY FOR LONGER DAYS

When your battery module runs low, SolarLun gives you another way to recover power outdoors. Snap the power bank onto the solar panel, place it in stable sunlight, and let the panel help recharge the battery during the day.

No complicated clips. No loose mounting parts. No awkward alignment. Just snap, connect, and recover power when outlets are not around.

THE CORE DIFFERENCE

6-FOLD SOLAR CHARGER

Avelun SolarLun uses a 6-fold flexible solar panel that opens wide when sunlight is available.Folded, it is compact for bag carry.Opened, it becomes a real solar charging surface.The solar panel includes its own Type-C output, so it can directly charge compatible electronic devices in sunlight — even when it is not connected to the power bank module.

18W SOLAR POWER

7,800mAh POWER BANK MODULE

The detachable 7,800mAh battery module gives SolarLun instant backup power when you need it most. Use it like a regular power bank to charge your phone, earbuds, camera accessories, USB lights, or other everyday devices — no sunlight required.

Then, when daylight is available, connect it back to the solar panel and let SolarLun help recover energy from the sun. It is everyday backup power with a way to recharge beyond the wall outlet.

TYPE-C + USB-A OUTPUT

SolarLun is built to work with the devices you already carry. The battery module gives you Type-C and USB-A output for everyday charging, while the solar panel includes its own Type-C port for direct sunlight charging.

Use the battery when you need stored power. Use the solar panel when daylight is available. From phones and cameras to earbuds, lights, GPS devices, and accessories, SolarLun gives your gear more ways to stay powered.
