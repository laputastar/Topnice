Risks and challenges
Like every hardware and content-based project, Kinder-Box also comes with challenges and risks that we want to communicate openly and honestly.

One of the biggest challenges is transforming our current working prototype into a scalable and affordable product for families. Hardware production, manufacturing costs, enclosure optimization and sourcing reliable components are areas that still require significant work and funding.

Another important challenge is content production. Our vision is to regularly release new multilingual stories for children. Creating high-quality stories, translating them into multiple languages and producing audio content takes time, creativity and resources. Our goal is to publish at least one new multilingual story every week while continuously expanding our content library over time.

Because Kinder-Box depends on internet connectivity for accessing updated stories and content, maintaining a stable and simple user experience across different home environments is also an important technical challenge.

As a small family-driven project, we are building Kinder-Box step by step. The advantage is that we already have a fully working prototype that is used daily within our own family. This allows us to continuously improve the platform based on real-world usage and practical experience instead of theoretical concepts.

We also understand the responsibility that comes with building technology for children. Simplicity, safety and reliability are our highest priorities, and we intentionally want to keep Kinder-Box focused and easy to use rather than overloaded with unnecessary features.

With the support of this Kickstarter campaign, we hope to overcome these challenges by improving production, expanding multilingual content and continuing to develop Kinder-Box into a safe and meaningful storytelling platform for families around the world.

Why We Created Kinder-Box

As parents living in Switzerland, we were looking for a simple audio device that our children could use independently.

Most children's devices today are filled with apps, advertisements, videos, notifications and distractions.

We wanted something different.

A safe audio experience focused on stories, imagination and language learning.

So we started writing our own bedtime stories and children's adventures.

At first, these stories were only for our own children.

Later, we began producing multilingual versions of our stories using modern voice technology, allowing children to enjoy the same stories in multiple languages.

What started as a small family project slowly evolved into Kinder-Box.

Children today spend more time than ever in front of screens.

Many devices designed for children include:

Endless scrolling

Video distractions

Complicated menus

Social media integrations

We wanted a simpler solution.

Kinder-Box is a multilingual touchscreen audio box designed specifically for children.

Select stories themselves

Change languages

Listen independently

Explore new adventures safely

No internet browsing.

No social media.

Designed for children. Simple for parents.
