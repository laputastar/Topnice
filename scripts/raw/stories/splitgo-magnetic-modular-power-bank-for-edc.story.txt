We are a small product team focused on creating practical everyday-carry gear for modern life, travel, and outdoor use.

Why do we always need to carry so many separate gadgets? A power bank, a flashlight, a mini fan, a shaver, a lighter, a mist sprayer — each one can be useful, but carrying all of them separately quickly becomes bulky and inconvenient.

So we began exploring a different approach: one compact power source that can work with different functional modules. Instead of designing another regular power bank, we wanted to build a modular system that can adapt to different situations — from daily commuting and short trips to camping, outdoor activities, and emergency moments.

Our team has experience in product design, electronics development, mechanical structure, supply chain coordination, and crowdfunding operations. During the development of SplitGo, we worked closely on the magnetic connection structure, battery module design, module stability, charging performance, and the overall user experience of switching between different functions.

We know that a modular product is not only about adding more accessories. Each module needs to feel useful, easy to attach, and reliable in real situations. That is why we spent time refining the connection method, testing different module combinations, and making sure the product stays compact enough for everyday carry.

See SplitGo in Real Use

For this Kickstarter campaign, our goal is to bring SplitGo to backers who want a more flexible and practical power system — something that can charge your devices, support useful add-ons, and reduce the need to carry multiple small gadgets.

We’re excited to share SplitGo with the Kickstarter community and continue improving it with your support and feedback.

Risks and challenges

SplitGo is a modular electronic product, so the main challenges are magnetic connection stability, contact pin durability, battery safety, production consistency, and module performance.

Before mass production, we will continue testing the magnetic structure, battery protection system, charging performance, and each functional module.

If there are any changes caused by tooling, component supply, certification preparation, or quality control, we will keep backers updated throughout the process.
