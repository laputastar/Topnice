Meet Kerylove YYZG-450

Full Visibility Cooking System

Traditional air fryers keep food hidden behind closed drawers and solid baskets.You can hear the fan running.You can smell the food cooking.But you never really know what's happening inside.Most people end up opening the basket again and again, interrupting the cooking process.

Kerylove YYZG-450 changes that completely.Featuring a transparent glass dome and built-in halogen illumination, it lets you monitor every stage of cooking in real time.From the first sizzle to the final golden finish, every delicious moment stays in sight.

Steam-Assisted Cooking System

One of the biggest compromises with traditional air fryers is moisture loss.The same hot air that creates a crispy crust can also pull moisture out of your food.Fish becomes dry.Chicken loses tenderness.Vegetables quickly lose their freshness.For many people, this simply feels like the unavoidable trade-off of healthier cooking.

We believed there had to be a better way.That's why Kerylove YYZG-450 introduces a steam-assisted cooking system.Simply add water to the drip tray.As 360° hot air circulates around your food, gentle steam helps preserve moisture inside.The result is a balance traditional air fryers struggle to achieve.Crispy outside.Juicy inside.Without sacrificing either.

Perfect for foods that benefit from both texture and moisture.Everyday meals simply taste better.

Elevated Grease Separation System

Kerylove YYZG-450 features a raised perforated basket and a dedicated grease collection tray.As food cooks, excess oil naturally drains away from the cooking surface and collects below.This helps keep food elevated above the grease rather than sitting in it.Enjoy lighter meals with less greasy textures.

4.5L Family-Sized Capacity

Designed for everyday family cooking.The spacious 4.5L capacity (4.8 QT) provides enough room for meals for 2–5people, helping you prepare more food in a single batch and spend less time waiting.

Spend Less Time Cleaning

Kerylove YYZG-450 features a premium ceramic-coated aluminum basket designed to make cleanup significantly easier.The smooth non-stick surface helps reduce food residue buildup and allows grease and debris to be removed more effortlessly.

Effortless Control

One of the most practical features is the ability toadd waterto the drip tray. This enables steam-assisted cooking, helping you achieve juicy results on the inside while keeping the crispy exterior — perfect for steaming vegetables, fish, dumplings, or reheating leftovers without drying them out. It brings multi-functionality (air fry + steam roast) in one compact unit.
