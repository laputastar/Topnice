Risks and challenges
Peeksy is a hardware product, so the main challenges may come from production scheduling, component supply, quality control, software and connection optimization, packaging, and international shipping.

We currently have working samples and are testing the product in real use scenarios. However, before mass production, we still need to finalize the product details, confirm materials, complete pilot production, and run quality checks.

Our goal is to keep the product simple, reliable, and transparent throughout the process.

We Built This Because We Needed It

A crooked selfie. A blank phone back. A desk without a timer. We fixed all three.

One of us spent an entire solo trip taking crooked selfies with her rear camera. Timer. Run. Check. Half out of frame. Again. And again. Eight tries for one usable shot.

"I just want to see what my rear camera sees."

That feature didn't exist. So we built it.

Then we looked at the blank back of our phones and thought — why can't it show our favorite photo? Our mood? A looping video of our dog? So we added that.

Then someone wanted mini games during desk breaks. And a timer. Done.

Mirloop1 became the tiny screen we use every day.

We're a small team of designers and photographers.

We test prototypes at our desks, in coffee shops, on trips. Every feature came from a real moment — not a spreadsheet.

We've partnered with experienced consumer electronics manufacturers. The working prototype is ready.
