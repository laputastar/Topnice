[RISKS & CHALLENGES]
Risks and challenges
Like any physical product launch, the main risks are production timing, final quality checks, shipping timelines, and supplier coordination. We have been manufacturing, developing and testing versions of this product for years through our original disc golf bag stand, so this is not our first time building, producing, and shipping a stand like this.

The Ryzer Go is currently in the final stages of tooling and production preparation. Before full production begins, we will review final samples, confirm fit and function, and make sure the product meets our quality standards. If we find anything that needs improvement, we may take extra time to fix it before shipping.

Shipping timelines can also be affected by manufacturing schedules, freight delays, customs, and fulfillment logistics. We are working with experienced production and fulfillment partners to reduce those risks, and we will keep backers updated throughout the process.

Our goal is to be transparent, move carefully, and deliver a product we are proud of rather than rushing something out before it is ready.

Now you always have someplace to set down your bag.The Ryzer Go is a stand that attaches to almost any bag or backpack, with spring-loaded legs that lift it 13.7 inches off the ground in seconds, instantly elevating your backpack, camera bag, tool bag, or gear bag so it stays clean, dry, and accessible, no matter where your day takes you.

Twist to Deploy

The video above shows the Ryzer Go stand without a bag attached so you can clearly see the twist-to-deploy action. Once attached, the spring-loaded legs snap open with a twist, giving your bag a sturdy upright stand in seconds. When you’re on the go, it folds flat for easy carrying and storage.

Elevates Your Bag 13.7 Inches

Keep your essentials dry, within reach, and off the ground anywhere you need it. At work, while traveling, outdoors, in the garage, or almost anywhere else, your bag usually ends up on the floor. It also means if you need something, you’re probably bending down, kneeling, or digging through your bag at ground level.

The Ryzer Go folds flat in seconds and slides into your daily carry with ease. At just 2.4 lbs, it adds next to nothing to your setup while making everything more accessible.

Depth Size Information

Bag Depth Compatibility

Measure your bag when it's full

Ryzer Go 7" Depth: Recommended for bags 5.5 to 9.5 inches deep

Ryzer Go 9" Depth: Recommended for bags 8.5 to 12 inches deep

Bags between 8.5" and 9.5" deep can work with either size

If your bag measures around 9", choose based on shape: Larger front pockets usually fit better on the 9" depth stand, while sleek backpacks with tighter pockets usually fit better on the 7" stand

Bags deeper than 12" can work, but the leg opening handle may be harder to reach

Disc Golfers select the 9" depth

Bag Style Compatability

Works with backpack or tote style bags

Backpacks need to have a relatevly flat bottom

Bag Width Compatibility

Both models support bags approximately 10 to 16 inches wide

Bags under 10" wide can work, but more of the 12" frame will show

Rigid bottom bags can be wider than 16"

Weight Capacity

Recommended maximum bag weight is 50 lbs

Ryzer Go Weight

The Ryzer weighs ~2.4 lbs

Keep legs whithin the frame when closed and reduce the footprint when the legs are open. They will lower the stand by 3", to about 10.7". The low profile leg add-ons are more TSA and travel friendly since the legs do not extend past the Ryzer Go frame. You can also switch between leg lengths depending on how you'll use it.

Use your Ryzer Go on multiple bags with an Additional Bag Attachment Set. Attach a set to each bag, then simply unclamp the Ryzer Go from one and move it to another in less than a minute.

Includes:4 clamps with pin and clutch, 4 heavy duty clamp mounts, and 2 shoulder strap hangers.

Comes with G-hooks and long straps. Use the G-hooks to attach your Ryzer Go to bags with vertical MOLLE, like some cooler bags, without puncturing the bag. Use the long webbing straps for bags with low D-rings. A separate horizontal MOLLE attachment is currently in development.
