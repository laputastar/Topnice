Risks and challenges
Karmi is currently in the prototype stage, and like any hardware project, there are challenges related to manufacturing, component sourcing, testing, and fulfillment. While we have already developed working prototypes and core software features, unexpected delays can still happen during production and shipping.

To reduce these risks, we are keeping the product focused and intentionally simple in its first version. We are using widely available electronic components, standard USB-C charging, and a compact design that is easier to manufacture and assemble at scale. We are also working closely with manufacturing and fulfillment partners to help ensure quality and reliability throughout production.

Another challenge is balancing creativity with polish. We plan to continue refining the interface, animations, and fortune content during the campaign and production phase while keeping the core experience stable and achievable.

As an independent creative studio, we believe in transparent communication. If any delays or changes happen during development, we will keep backers updated throughout the process. Our goal is to deliver a fun, thoughtful, and well-crafted product that feels worth carrying every day.

Why We Created Karmi

We’ve always been fascinated by fortune tellers, tarot cards, lucky charms, and the small rituals people use to reflect, dream, and connect with others.

Karmi began as an experiment: what if these traditions could be transformed into a playful modern companion you could carry everywhere?

After months of prototyping, design exploration, and testing, we created Karmi — a tiny device that blends nostalgia, technology, and curiosity into something fun to use and share.
