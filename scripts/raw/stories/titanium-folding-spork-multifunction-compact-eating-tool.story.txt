Introducing FieldSpork, a CNC-machined titanium folding spork with a built-in steak knife, fruit pick, bottle opener, and a precise 5ml measuring spoon, and depth measurement scale.

Carry less. Do more. Eat better.

Plastic utensils warp or crack. Stamped sporks bend. Traditional dining sets are bulky and inconvenient to carry.

You shouldn't have to pack multiple utensils just to enjoy a proper meal.

Whether you're camping, traveling, or having lunch at your desk, your eating tools should be just as dependable as the rest of your everyday carry.

From Compact Carry to Full Capability

Small enough to disappear in your pocket. Capable enough to replace your entire utensil kit. From weekday lunches to backcountry meals, FieldSpork is built for wherever life takes you.

Cut, peel, scrape, pick, and eat with a single tool, precision-machined from titanium for exceptional durability. Corrosion-resistant, inert, and food-safe, it's designed to serve for years to come.

No plastic. No moving parts. Nothing to break.

Unfold it in seconds—and you're ready for any meal, anywhere.

The folding design makes FieldSpork incredibly compact to carry, yet quickly unfolds into a full-length utensil whenever you need the extra reach.

Spork + 5ml Measuring Spoon

Not just a spoon. Not just a fork.

A carefully balanced spork that:

Scoops comfortably

Picks precisely

Holds exactly max of 5ml (1 teaspoon) of liquid.

👉 Use it to eat.👉 Use it to measure.👉 Use it with intention.

This is the kind of precision that only CNC machining can achieve, not casting, not stamping. From the first unit, to the 10,000 units, we keep the same consistency with no compromise.

The geometry of the spoon was carefully engineered to provideintuitive portion referencein everyday use. Depending on how it's held, the spoon accommodates approximately4 or 5 ml of liquid, while its flat fill line corresponds to roughly3 gramsof fine powder— adding another layer of functionality to an already versatile tool.

Serrated Steak Knife

The serrated blade on FieldSpork was engineered specifically for dining. Rather than pursuing razor-like sharpness, we carefully refined the geometry of each serration to deliver efficient cutting performance where it matters most. It glides through steak and crusty bread with ease, while reducing the risk of accidental nicks during everyday use.

Precision CNC machining ensures that every FieldSpork delivers the same dependable performance— meal after meal.
