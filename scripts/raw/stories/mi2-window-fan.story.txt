Environmental commitments

Most window fans were designed decades ago with one goal — move air. But once they’re turned off, they often leave your window exposed, allowing outside air, heat, dust, and humidity back into your home.Mi2 Fan was created to rethink the traditional window fan from the ground up.

Designed as a self-sealing window cooling system, Mi2 Fan is built to open for airflow when in use and close to help seal the window when off. The goal is simple: help keep cool air inside longer while reducing the need to constantly remove and reinstall a traditional fan.

What started as a simple idea became a mission to create a smarter, more modern approach to window cooling — one designed for comfort, convenience, and everyday use.

We’re excited to bring Mi2 Fan to Kickstarter and invite you to be part of the launch.

Long-lasting design

Mi2 Fan is being designed with durability and everyday use in mind. From its self-sealing airflow system to its weather-conscious construction, the goal is to create a window fan that can remain installed and operate season after season.

Unlike traditional window fans that are often removed and reinstalled repeatedly, Mi2 Fan is engineered as a more permanent cooling solution designed for long-term convenience, comfort, and performance.
