Risks and challenges
Gen3 is the latest evolution of our  KVM Dock, built on the proven Gen2 architecture. It adds Gigabit Ethernet, SD/TF card readers, an optimized I/O layout, and a higher-capacity power system to deliver greater efficiency for multi-device workflows.

Potential Risks:

The addition of new interfaces and enhanced power management may require firmware optimization in rare system configurations. Global logistics fluctuations or component availability could impact production schedules; however, we have established multi-source procurement channels for key components to minimize potential risks.

Why We’re Confident:

We have successfully delivered the first two generations of our KVM products. Our engineering team brings over 20 years of experience in high-speed data transmission, display technologies, and power delivery design. Multiple BD215J prototypes have undergone comprehensive functional, thermal, and stability testing. 

With mature manufacturing partnerships, extensive engineering validation, and direct communication with our backers, we are confident in delivering Gen3 on time and to the high standards our community expects.
