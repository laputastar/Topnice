Environmental commitments

Modern laptops are becoming thinner and more portable, but many familiar ports are quietly disappearing —USB-A,SD card slots,optical drives, and more. Devices may look cleaner than ever, yet everyday connectivity often depends on a growing collection of adapters and cables, making workflows feel fragmented and less intuitive.

That led us to ask a simple question:What if these essential functions could come together again in one streamlined device?

DISCORE combines aUSB hub,SD/TF card reader, andexternal optical driveinto asingle compact workstation. One device handles multiple expansion needs, helping reduce cable clutter and minimizing the need for extra accessories in your bag or on your desk.

More than just additional ports, DISCORE is designed to create a cleaner, more focused workspace experience — helping your setup feel simpler, your workflow smoother, and your creative moments less interrupted by small distractions.

9-in-1 Expansion Ports

DISCORE features a minimalist white aluminum-alloy design with a slim and lightweight form factor. It includes4 USB-A ports,1 USB-C port, a built-inCD/DVDoptical drive, and dualSD / TF card slotswith support for high-speed10Gbpsdata transfer.

IntegratedUSB-CandUSB-Aconnection cables allow convenient compatibility with both modern and traditional deviceswithout requiring additional adapters.

Driver-Free Plug and Play

Connect DISCORE, insert a disc, drag in your files, and start burning. Withplug-and-playsetup, creating CDs or DVDs is quick, simple, and hassle-free.

Let the device handle the complexity, so your workflow stays simple.

Featuring bothUSB-CandUSB-Aconnectivity, DISCORE works with both modern USB-C devices and traditional USB-A computers without requiring extra adapters.

Dual Card Slots with High-Speed Read & Write

Insert your camera'sSDcard or your drone'smicroSDcard directly into DISCORE for quick and convenient file transfers. Fewer accessories, fewer cables, and a smoother workflow.Powered byUSB 3.2 Gen 2with up to10Gbpsbandwidth for fast, reliable performance.

One Docking Station, a Desk Freed from Cables

No more juggling multiple cables and adapters for different devices. DISCORE brings commonly used ports together inone streamlined hub, helping reduce desk clutter and simplify everyday connectivity.The result is a cleaner workspace and a smoother workflow, with faster access, easier transfers, and less interruption between tasks.

Keep a Physical Warmth tor Your Data

Compared with a typical hub, DISCORE carries a sense of familiarity from the optical media era. It connects more than ports and storage —it reconnects moments, memories, and archived content from the past.

Old discs that have been sitting unused for years can be accessed again, bringing photos, videos, documents, and personal archives back into view.

DISCORE is more than an external optical drive.It serves as a bridge between past and present, helping stored memories become accessible once again.

Preserve Your Data in a Physical Form

As cloud storage becomes part of everyday life, more and more data exists only in digital spaces we cannot physically see. At the same time, many people are paying closer attention to long-term storage andindependent backupsfor important files.

DISCORE supportsCD and DVD burning, making it easy to archive contracts, family videos, graduation projects, and other valuable content. Once recorded, your files can be stored asa physical backupthat remains independently accessible.

Some memories and important files simply feel more meaningful when preserved

Spend More Time Creating

For photographers, designers, and video creators, interruptions can easily break creative flow. In many cases, it’s not the creative work itself that slows things down, but the constant switching between devices, adapters, and card readers.
