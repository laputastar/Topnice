Introducing the ProCare DirectDraw

Why DirectDraw™ Was Created

As a solo operator, I set out to create what would eventually become the DirectDraw™ Precision Injection System.

After years of operating lawn and landscape spray equipment, I encountered the same challenges every day:

• Measuring chemicals• Mixing tanks• Cleaning equipment• Product waste• Calibration uncertainty• Lost production time when changing products and rebatching chemicals

I believed there had to be a better way.

The original proof of concept came together quickly, but solving the larger problem took years. A system is only useful if it works with the wide variety of products operators use every day. Many liquid products vary significantly in viscosity, formulation, and suspended particulate content, creating challenges for traditional injection technologies.

What frustrated me most wasn't just the waste—it was the lost time. Every product change meant draining tanks, rinsing equipment, measuring chemicals, mixing new batches, recalibrating, and getting back into the field. While the equipment sat idle, valuable production time was being lost.

Development continued until the right technology became available. As flow measurement technology advanced, it finally became possible to accurately monitor and verify chemical flow rates across virtually all liquid products used in the industry.

The result is DirectDraw™.

Rather than estimating chemical application through tank concentrations, dilution ratios, and assumptions, DirectDraw™ directly measures the chemical being injected into the spray stream in real time. Operators can see exactly what the system is doing, make immediate adjustments, and verify application rates with confidence.

What started as an effort to solve everyday frustrations evolved into a patented system designed to help operators spend less time mixing and more time spraying.
