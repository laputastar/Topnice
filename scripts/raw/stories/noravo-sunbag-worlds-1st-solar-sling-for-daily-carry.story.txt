WHY NORAVO SUNBAG EXISTS

You pack light. You take only what you need for the day — your phone, camera, earbuds, wallet, keys.But open your sling. Cables tangled. Power bank buried under a jacket. Lens cap somewhere at the bottom. You spend more time digging than doing.And by afternoon, your phone is at 10%. Your camera battery is blinking. Your earbuds case is empty.There’s no outlet in the park. No charger on the street. And even if you brought a power bank, it’s lost somewhere in the mess — or already dead.A regular sling just carries things. It doesn’t help you stay organized. It doesn’t help keep your gear powered.

You don’t need a bigger bag. You need a smarter one.

MEET NORAVO SUNBAG

A sling that carries your day. And charges it, too.8 liters of organized space. Two flexible solar panels — front and back. Up to 17W of sun‑power. USB‑A and Type‑C. Plug. Wait. Go.Oxford fabric. Water‑resistant zippers. 0.93kg. Details that work while you walk.No battery. No complexity. Just the sun, your gear, and the moments between outlets.

SUN ON BOTH SIDES

You don’t always face the sun — and your bag doesn’t either. That’s whyNORAVO SunBagplaces flexible solar cells on both sides of the sling, so it can collect light from more angles throughout your day.NORAVO SunBagintegrates flexible solar cells into both the front and back of the sling. Whether you‘re walking, sitting, or leaning against a wall, one side is always catching light.The flexible design means no rigid boards — just a comfortable, natural shape that moves with you.

Noravo SunBag gives you realistic solar top-up power for outdoor days — up to 17W in ideal direct sunlight, and around 8–9W in typical one-side use. Not a wall charger replacement, but a useful way to keep small devices going between outlets.

POWER WHERE YOU NEED IT

With a built-in USB-A + Type-C junction box,NORAVO SunBagcan charge small devices directly from sunlight — no wall outlet, no separate power station, and no complicated setup.

JUST RIGHT FOR A DAY OUT

NORAVO SunBagis not just built to charge — it is built to carry. The 8L interior gives your everyday essentials a clear place, from your phone and camera to earbuds, wallet, keys, cables, and small notebook.

No oversized backpack. No messy sling. Just enough organized space for workdays, city walks, photo sessions, and the moments between.

BOTTOM STRAP. BIG FREEDOM.

Some gear is better carried outside the bag.NORAVO SunBag’sbottom strap lets you secure a mini tripod, rolled jacket, or soft extra gear without taking up your main compartment.

Your camera stays ready. Your essentials stay organized. Your hands stay free.

LIFE WITH NORAVO SUNBAG

Commute, walk, shoot, rest, or travel light.NORAVO SunBagkeeps your essentials close and gives your small devices a solar top-up when sunlight is available

MADE FOR REAL LIFE — FABRIC

Light rain, splashes, scratches, and daily friction are part of real carry.NORAVO SunBagis designed to handle everyday movement while helping keep your essentials protected.

DETAILS THAT LAST

A sling is only as good as the parts that hold it together.NORAVO SunBaguses durable hardware, reinforced stitching, and a hidden magnetic closure to make everyday carry feel smoother, stronger, and more secure.Details you don’t see — until you need them.

FROM SKETCH TO NORAVO SUNBAG

THE TEAM BEHIND NORAVO

Noravo began with a simple belief: everyday gear should make life easier, not heavier.As a small team of product designers, engineers, and outdoor photography users, we often found ourselves carrying more than we wanted — a camera, a phone, earbuds, cables, a power bank, and sometimes a tripod. The bag was full, the cables were tangled, and the battery was still something we had to worry about.

That is where the idea for our solar camera bag started.We did not want to create a bulky outdoor bag with solar panels added only for appearance. We wanted to design something people would actually carry — compact enough for daily movement, organized enough for camera
