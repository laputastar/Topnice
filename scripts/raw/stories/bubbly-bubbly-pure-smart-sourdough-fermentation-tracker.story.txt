Risks and challenges
1. Manufacturing risks - we have successfully completed pre-production runs, already have working prototype units and have stocked parts for the first large production run of 5000 devices. If the campaign goes beyond these quantities there is risk of delaying the extra units by a month or two.

Possible risks include:
- component supply delays
- production schedule changes
- packaging adjustments

2. Product quality risks - while preparing for manufacturing we have set up strict quality control procedures involving both automatic and manual inspections. There is still a chance we will miss an underperforming device and deliver it to end users but we will be happy to replace such units immediately.

3. Delivery - we will deliver the products, even if it means incurring a loss for us. The only risk you are taking is that your reward might not reach you on time, but we'll do our best to avoid that. We will publish frequent updates on fulfilment status and you’ll always be up to date.

Possible risks include:
- international shipping delays
- customs processing delays
- battery-related transportation requirements
