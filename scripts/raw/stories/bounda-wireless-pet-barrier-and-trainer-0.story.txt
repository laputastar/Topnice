Bounda – Risks and Challenges
As with any hardware project, we want to be transparent about the potential risks and our plans to overcome them.

1. Manufacturing and Component Sourcing
Bounda uses dual‑band LF + 2.4G modules, IPX6‑rated housings, and rechargeable batteries. Global supply chains can be unpredictable.
Mitigation: We have secured secondary suppliers for key components and buffer stock for the first production run. Our factory partner has 8+ years of experience in pet electronics and maintains a 98% on‑time delivery record.

2. Firmware and Calibration
The product’s accuracy depends on proper calibration of signal strength and boundary detection. In rare cases, environmental interference (e.g., metal structures, home appliances) may affect performance.
Mitigation: We will provide a detailed troubleshooting guide and video tutorials. All units undergo 100% factory calibration before shipping, and we offer free firmware updates (if needed) via a simple USB connection.

3. Shipping and Fulfillment
Delays in international shipping or customs clearance could push delivery beyond August.
Mitigation: We have already booked air and sea freight capacity. Backers will receive tracking information and real‑time updates. We also keep a 15% safety stock for lost or damaged parcels.

4. User Education
Some backers may find the concept of “invisible boundary” unfamiliar and need time to train their pets.
Mitigation: We will include an illustrated quick start guide, video tutorials, and 24/7 email support. A dedicated FAQ section on our website will also address common setup issues.

5. Kickstarter Specifics
If the campaign does not reach its goal (though we are already over 100% funded for our previous projects), or if unforeseen costs arise.
Mitigation: We have a backup budget from our successful WILL campaign. Bounda shares components with our existing products, reducing engineering risk.

We are committed to full transparency and will post regular updates on production, testing, and shipping. Your trust is our priority.

Every Corner Of Your Home Deserves Protection

Have you ever come home after a long day only to find the trash knocked over—again? Or noticed fresh claw marks on a sofa you just replaced? Tried to keep your pet out of the nursery, kitchen, or certain areas of the home—only to find yourself constantly reminding and stopping them? And then there are the behavior challenges—excessive barking, guarding food or toys, chasing people or vehicles, or simply refusing to listen when it matters most. You love your pets. But love shouldn't mean living with chaos.

Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

There are plenty of ways to manage pets at home — but most only solve part of the problem. Physical barriers can block movement, but they take up space and affect the appearance of your home. Underground fences require installation, wiring, and ongoing maintenance. Repellent sprays can be messy and inconsistent. Traditional dog trainers can help correct unwanted behaviors, but they can't protect specific spaces. Pet owners often end up buying multiple products just to solve one problem.

One Device. Two Solutions. Double Protection.

Safe in Every Spot, Clean in Every Corner.

That's why we created Bounda. A system designed to solve two challenges at once: Protect the spaces that matter. And help correct unwanted behaviors. No extra barriers. No complicated installation. No need to buy multiple devices.

What is Bounda?

The All-in-One Wireless Pet Boundary & Training System

Bounda is more than a traditional pet barrier. It's a wireless pet boundary and training system designed to help pet owners manage both space and behavior with a single device. Simply place a transmitter near the area you want to protect. When your pet enters the boundary, the receiver collar provides a gentle correction to help them learn where they should and shouldn't go.
