Risks and challenges
We believe in being transparent with our backers about the potential challenges of this project.

The good news is that product development has already been completed, and we have secured manufacturing support from an experienced factory partner with whom we maintain a close working relationship.

The primary challenge is achieving the production scale required for efficient manufacturing. While our manufacturing partner is fully capable of producing VULYX, crowdfunding success plays an important role in determining production volume and efficiency.

Our crowdfunding goal has been carefully calculated to help us move confidently into production while maintaining product quality and delivery commitments.

Another challenge is international shipping. Delivery schedules may occasionally be affected by customs clearance, transportation capacity, or regional logistics conditions beyond our control.

To minimize these risks, we work with experienced logistics partners and will closely monitor the fulfillment process. If any delays occur, we will keep our backers informed.

We are committed to maintaining open communication throughout the campaign and fulfillment process, and we will do everything possible to deliver VULYX to our backers as smoothly and efficiently as possible.
