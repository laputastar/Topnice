Three Cameras, One Intelligent Space System

Which setup is right for you?

Camtells is not just a single camera. It is an AI camera system designed to understand your space.Mark, Semi, and Cee work together to cover different parts of your environment: Mark handles wide-area coverage, Semi fills blind spots, and Cee brings voice, screen, and interactive AI into everyday indoor spaces.

Together, they connect AI camera directing, event search, object memory, daily recaps, personalized alerts, and multi-camera coordination into one complete system — helping you see less footage, and understand more of what matters. Each camera can also work on its own, so you can start with one device and expand your Camtells system over time.

Why Today's Cameras Still Fall Short

What Makes Camtells Different

Search Your Space Not Your Timeline

When something happens, Camtells helps you search by people, objects, locations, and events - instead of manually digging through hours of footage. Ask a question, select a target, or mark an area in the frame. CamAgent helps find the relevant moments across your cameras, so you can understand what happened faster.

Your Space Has Memory Now

Camtells can help remember clearly captured people, vehicles, objects, pets, and selected targets, making future searches faster and more natural.

As your space is continuously recorded, it becomes easier to review repeated appearances, trace where a target was seen, and understand what happened before and after each key moment.

One Al Director for Every Camera

Camtells does more than show multiple camera feeds. When Mark, Semi, and Cee are watching your space together, CamAgent helps identify which view matters most and brings that camera angle to the front.Whether you are watching live or reviewing playback, Camtells reduces the need to jump between feeds or guess where an event happened. Instead of checking every camera manually, you get a clearer view of the moments that matter.One space. Multiple cameras. One AI Director.

AI Auto-Framing Across Every Camera

No Monthly Fees. Built-in eMMC Storage.

To support long-term and large-scale circular recording, Camtells has specifically chosen a higher-cost and more reliable eMMC local storage solution for each Camtells camera, instead of a cheaper ordinary storage option. This helps ensure better durability and stability for continuous local recording and repeated data writing over time. So your everyday footage can stay on your own device instead of being locked behind a long-term cloud storage plan.

Smarter Alerts. Customized Reminders

Every space has different priorities. Camtells lets you create personalized IF / THEN alerts
