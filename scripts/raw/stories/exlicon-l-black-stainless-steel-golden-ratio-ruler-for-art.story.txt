Golden Ratio Applied to Our Design

The golden ratio is a special number, about 1.618, often seen in nature and design. It’s known for creating beauty and balance. Represented by the symbolφ = (1 + √5) / 2, this ratio is admired for its pleasing proportions.

It works by dividing a line or shape into two parts, where the larger part divided by the smaller equals the whole divided by the larger. Used in art and architecture, the golden ratio helps create designs that look harmonious and visually appealing.
