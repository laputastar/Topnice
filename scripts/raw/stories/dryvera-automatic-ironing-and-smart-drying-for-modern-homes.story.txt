Why Dryvera Is the Smarter Choice

User Pain Points

Every morning, your routine can be slowed down by small but frustrating problems.

The shirt you want to wear is wrinkled.The clothes you washed are still damp.Your shoes are wet after rain or workouts.Your hat is difficult to dry without losing shape.And traditional ironing takes more time than you want to spend.

Most existing solutions only solve part of the problem.

A traditional iron requires an ironing board and manual effort.A handheld steamer still needs you to stand there and work.A regular dryer may not be suitable for shoes, hats, or quick single-garment care.Many automatic ironing products only offer fixed programs with limited control.

Simplify Your Daily Clothes Care

One Device. Multiple Care Needs.

Dryvera brings multiple garment care functions into one smart, compact station.

Instead of using separate tools for ironing, drying, shoe drying, and hat care, Dryvera allows you to handle different daily care tasks with one device.

The process is simple:

Place your garment or accessory on Dryvera.

Choose the care mode on the touchscreen.

Select the fabric type. Dryvera automatically recommends an optimal temperature and drying/ironing time based on the material.

Adjust the temperature and time manually if needed for delicate or thicker fabrics.

With smart temperature and timer control, Dryvera ensures each garment or accessory receives the right level of care, preventing damage to delicate fabrics while efficiently handling thicker or damp items.

One Touchscreen. Five Care Functions.

Choose the right care function with just a tap.

Dryvera features a modern touchscreen interface that makes every function clear, simple, and easy to control.

Instead of turning knobs or guessing which button to press, users can select the right function directly on the screen:

·Shirt Ironing– smooth wrinkles from shirts and everyday tops·Pants Ironing– care for trousers with a dedicated ironing mode·Clothes Drying– dry damp garments quickly and evenly·Shoe Drying– dry shoes with a specialized drying attachment·Hat Drying– refresh and dry hats without complicated setup

The touchscreen also allows users to adjust key settings such as fabric type, temperature, working time, and care intensity. For different materials or care needs, users can customize the temperature and cycle time, then save their preferred settings for future use.

During operation, the screen clearly displays the selected mode, temperature, remaining time, and cycle status, so users always know what Dryvera is doing.

Effortless Garment Care: Smooth, Refresh, Repeat

Dryvera smooths and refreshes shirts, T-shirts, light tops, and everyday garments with minimal effort.

Itsdedicated inflatable garment moldsupports clothing in a natural shape, reducing wrinkles evenly and eliminating the need for a traditional ironing board.

Simply place the garment on the mold, select the appropriate care setting, and Dryvera automatically handles the ironing process.
