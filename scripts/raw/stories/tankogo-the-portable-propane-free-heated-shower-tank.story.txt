[RISKS & CHALLENGES]
Risks and challenges
The Tankogo family has moved beyond early concept development. Final moulds are approved, production is underway, and working units are already being used by creators and testers.
The remaining risks are the same practical challenges that can affect any physical-product launch: component availability, production yield, quality-control findings, packaging revisions, freight scheduling, customs clearance, and regional last-mile delivery.
We are working with established manufacturing partners and using our existing sprayer-engineering experience to reduce these risks. We will inspect production units, communicate progress through regular Kickstarter updates, and be transparent if a change affects the timeline.

Launching a new hardware product always involves potential challenges, particularly when moving from prototypes to large scale manufacturing.

Tankogo benefits from the experience and infrastructure of Sprayers Plus, a company with nearly 40 years of manufacturing experience in spray systems and liquid delivery equipment. Because our team has already developed and launched numerous products, we have a strong understanding of product engineering, manufacturing processes, and quality control.

Multiple working prototypes of Tankogo have already been developed and tested throughout the design process in order to refine and optimize the system before production. The units have undergone extensive testing to evaluate performance, durability, and usability in real world conditions.

This testing includes stress testing of the pump and heating systems as well as durability evaluations to ensure the product can withstand typical outdoor use.

While this development process significantly reduces the likelihood of defects or design issues, manufacturing and global logistics can still present unforeseen challenges. Component availability, production scheduling, and international shipping timelines can occasionally affect delivery schedules.

If any issues arise, we are committed to communicating transparently with our backers and providing regular updates throughout the entire process.

Our goal is to deliver a reliable, well engineered product that reflects the quality standards that Sprayers Plus has built its reputation on for nearly four decades.

Environmental commitments

Anyone who spends real time outside knows the feeling. You come back from a surf, a hike, a gravel ride, a backpacking trip, or a day at the beach with the kids and the dog, covered in salt, sand, sweat, or mud. The nearest hot shower is back home, and you don’t really want a freezing rinse from the hose. Or maybe there’s no hose nearby at all, and you don’t want all that sand and mud getting in the car.

Propane showers work, but they’re bulky, need fuel, and you don’t want an open flame near your van, tent, or kids.

Solar bags heat slowly, if the sun cooperates at all.

Wet wipes aren’t a shower.

Tankogo is the first portable hot shower powered entirely by electricity. No flame, no fuel, no propane tanks to refill.It combines a 15 L (4 gal) water tank, built-in electric heating, a rechargeable cordless pump, and a full shower setup in one travel-ready unit.

For backpackers and quick cleanups, Tankogo Mini is small enough to drop right into your pack. No heating, no setup, just pressurized water on demand for muddy boots, sandy gear, or a fast cool-off.Use Tankogo for:

Campsite and overlanding showers

Rinsing muddy dogs after hikes

Washing sand and salt water from feet and skin

Cleaning paddleboards, surf gear, bikes, shoes, and camping equipment

Washing children after messy outdoor days

Creating a convenient hand-washing station

Vanlife, road trips, festivals, and off-grid setups
