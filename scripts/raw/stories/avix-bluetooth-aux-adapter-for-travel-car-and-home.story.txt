Risks and challenges
The main risks for AVIX are production scheduling, component supply, battery shipping, and final quality control.

Before mass production, we will confirm the final sample, key components, packaging, and inspection standards.

Because AVIX includes a built-in rechargeable battery, we will choose suitable logistics channels according to each destination region.

Before shipment, we will check the product appearance, RX/TX modes, Bluetooth connection, audio playback, charging function, buttons, indicator lights, and basic call function.

Any small adjustments before production will focus on improving stability and user experience, without changing the core functions.

Still Using Wired AUX? Go Wireless Instantly.

Many devices still use a 3.5mm AUX port.

Airplane entertainment systems, TVs, monitors, treadmills, computers, car AUX systems, old speakers, and amplifiers often still rely on wired audio input or output.

But today, we are used to listening with Bluetooth headphones, Bluetooth speakers, phones, and tablets.

Then the problem appears:

You want to watch a movie on an airplane with your own Bluetooth headphones, but the seat screen only has a 3.5mm headphone jack.

You want to watch TV at night without disturbing anyone, but your TV does not support Bluetooth audio output.

Your car AUX system and old speakers still work well, but they cannot easily play music from your phone wirelessly.

You do not need to replace these devices.

You only need to change the way they connect.

ONE ADAPTER. TWO WAYS TO CONNECT.

Sometimes, you want to send audio from an AUX device to Bluetooth headphones.

Sometimes, you want to play music from your phone through AUX speakers or a car audio system.

AVIX brings both needs into one compact device.

Switch to TX mode to connect 3.5mm audio devices, such as TVs, airplane entertainment systems, treadmills, and computers, to Bluetooth headphones.

Switch to RX mode to play Bluetooth audio from your phone, tablet, or computer through car audio, old speakers, or home audio systems via AUX.

Whether you are traveling, watching movies, working out, driving, or listening at home, you no longer need to keep searching for different cables and adapters.

TX MODE: Use Your Headphones Anywhere.

You may already be used to your own Bluetooth headphones.

But many devices still only offer traditional 3.5mm AUX audio output.

Airplane entertainment systems, TVs, monitors, treadmills, computers, and game consoles can all create this problem.

When you encounter this kind of port, you may have to find a wired headset again, or simply give up using the Bluetooth headphones you already like.

Now, just plug AVIX into a standard 3.5mm AUX audio output, and you can send the sound wirelessly to Bluetooth headphones or Bluetooth speakers.

On a trip, you can use your own Bluetooth headphones to watch movies on an airplane entertainment system.

At night, you can watch TV and listen privately without disturbing others.

At the gym, you no longer need to be limited by headphone cables.

For devices that still use AUX audio output, AVIX helps them fit better into today’s wireless listening habits.

Listen Together With Two Headphones.

Using Bluetooth headphones alone is convenient.

But when two people watch TV together, travel together, or enjoy airplane entertainment together, connecting only one pair of headphones is not always enough.

In TX mode, AVIX supports connecting two Bluetooth headphones at the same time.

Two people can use their own familiar headphones and listen to the same 3.5mm audio source together.

No need to share one pair of earbuds.

No need to fight over one side of the headset.

No need to carry an extra splitter.

Whether you are watching a movie at home or sharing entertainment while traveling, listening together becomes easier.
