Why we made this:Bidetsywas born from a simple frustration: not being able to install a bidet in the bathroom.

Our solution:We madeBidetsy, a freestanding portable bidet that does not require installation or plumbing, so people who find it difficult to install a bidet, such as renters, can have one without the hassle ofhiring an expensive plumber.

Value proposition:TheBidetsyhas excellent water pressure, an enormous 20L (5 gal) refillable water tank and long lasting rechargeable battery. Our mission is to allow all people that want a bidet to have one even if they can't install one.

🛠️ WHAT WE’RE CREATING

Bidetsyis afreestanding portable bidetthat offers:

Rechargeable battery operation

Compact hassle free design

Freestanding stability (no awkward squeezing or holding required)

Easy-fill water tank

It’s designed to be:

More powerful than squeeze bottles

More portable than electric bidet attachments

More hygienic than wipes or paper

Bidetsyis a first of a kind innovation that allows users to have access to a bidet without needing expensive installation or plumbing. The features are as follows:

NO INSTALLATION REQUIRED:This freestanding portable bidet requires no installation or plumbing and can be easily placed next to any toilet for thorough sanitary cleansing. No leaks unlike fixed bidets.

POWERFUL WASH:The bidet delivers a strong yet comfortable stream of water for a thoroughly clean feeling. Up to 35PSI (similar to mains tap water pressure)

LARGE WATER TANK:The enormous 20L (5 gal) self-contained water tank holds enough capacity for countless uses before needing to be refilled from your sink faucet (estimated weekly refill based on 1 person with several uses per day).

FITS PERFECTLY:The slim size 15cm (W) x 48cm (D) x59cm (H) or 6" (W) x 19" (D) x 23" (H) allows it to fit perfectly in tight bathroom spaces next to the toilet.

EXCELLENT DESIGN:Stainless steel hose and high quality durable plastic that is factory tested so it is built to last.

RECHARGEABLE BATTERY:Included long lasting rechargeable 12V 4800mAh battery with charger (estimated monthly recharge). 4-8 hour recharge time estimated.

COMPLIANCE:The unit is compliant with the required CE / UKCA markings.
