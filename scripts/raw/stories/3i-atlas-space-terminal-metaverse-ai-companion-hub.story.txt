Live2D Enhanced Face Expressions

Proactive Interaction

The system proactively perceives everything in the surrounding environment via algorithms. Based on emotional metrics, it will either comfort you or listen patiently when you vent your frustrations.

Full Privacy Protection

Our AI uses algorithms to proactively sense your surroundings, offering comfort or listening to your rants based on emotional data. To ease privacy worries from constant sensing: all data stays encrypted locally, and the device shuts down sensors while idle, fully complying with US & EU AI rules. Per US law, AI must reveal it’s not human after long chats—our workaround: enjoy your companion on a separate terminal.

Real Long-Term Memory

We combine short-term user behavior tracking with long-term memory curve modeling to seamlessly integrate in-game events, anniversaries, and personal diaries into character interactions.

Unlocking a character’s past memories progressively upgrades their full spectrum of emotional expressions tied to unfolding story events.
