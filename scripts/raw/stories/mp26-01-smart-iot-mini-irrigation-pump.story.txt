[RISKS & CHALLENGES]
Risks and challenges
MP26-01 is our upgraded and optimized product. We spent over a year enhancing the performance of both hardware and software—from pump water stability testing to software and electronic circuit upgrades. The design is now finalized, and our manufacturing partner has produced multiple batches of successfully tested units.

That said, a few risks remain:

1、Production Schedule Adjustment  
Although the parameters of our 3D printing facility have been optimized, minor delays may occur due to fluctuations in the global supply of electronic components, batch-to-batch performance variations in Bambu PLA material, or unexpected capacity constraints.  
To mitigate this risk, we have secured material suppliers and pre-scheduled production slots.

2、Software and Hardware System Integration and Mass Production Risks  
When the companion mobile application (app) is adapted to mainstream operating system versions—including Android 12 through 14 and iOS 16 through 18—compatibility inconsistencies may arise in edge cases. Ensuring consistency during mass production may require additional quality control measures.  
We will conduct multi-stage inspections for each production batch and continuously optimize functionality, release firmware updates, and perform necessary calibrations.

3、Logistics and Global Transportation Variability Risks  
International delivery may be affected by customs delays, holiday peak periods, or disruptions in express courier services. Delivery lead times to end customers may vary objectively across countries and regions.  
To mitigate this risk, we partner with reliable global logistics providers and provide real-time shipment tracking updates via the Kickstarter dashboard and email notifications to keep backers informed of transportation progress.

4、User Experience Assurance Risks  
End users may encounter a steep learning curve when initially configuring Wi-Fi connectivity, setting up multi-pump coordinated control networks, and debugging advanced functions. In extremely rare cases, individual units may experience performance degradation after prolonged operation under high load.  
Mitigation Measures: Provide a structured English user manual—including embedded QR codes linking directly to video tutorials; resolve after-sales inquiries within 48 hours and initiate replacement or repair within 72 hours.

We are committed to transparent communication. In the event of any unexpected issues, we will promptly share the latest updates and strive to find the best solutions for all our supporters.

Environmental commitments

We’ve brought you a fun little cube: the MP26-01. It helps you automatically water your plants. Based on your understanding of your crops, you can customize your watering plan — from scheduled watering, watering duration, to interval between waterings — all customizable. With the companion app, you can record and review your past watering schedules. The MP26-01 is a mini smart water pump, and we hope it becomes your first reliable irrigation assistant on your planting journey.

Two main colors are available: brown and green. These are two basic colors, representing soil and vegetation, and they blend well into your garden or next to your plants. Of course, we’ve also experimented with some bold and vibrant colors.

The main body and internal structure of the MP26-01 are 3D printed, and the material is biodegradable PLA.

It fits multiple scenarios and supports various DIY usages. Suitable for all plants that need irrigation, and it is also perfectly applicable to rainforest tank landscaping.

The 5-meter silicone tubing is field-cuttable to accommodate variable plant spacing and diverse layout lengths. Its gray, light-shielding construction effectively suppresses algal proliferation, ensuring long-term pipeline cleanliness and uninterrupted flow performance.

Fine Drip Mode.Water comes out in tiny droplets with slow micro water output, ideal for succulents and drought-tolerant small plants, providing gentle water supply without waterlogging.

Water Column Mode.Water flows in a straight column with large water output, suitable for large green plants and vines for fast and sufficient watering.

When the temperature drops below 41°F, the water inside the product may freeze and expand, causing the product to crack.If used outdoors, please keep the product at a temperature above 41°F and store it indoors in a dark place to prevent damage from low temperatures and UV-induced aging.

MP26-10 is made of PLA biodegradable eco-friendly material. Do not expose the product to environments above 60°C, as this may cause softening
