A Dice Worth Wearing

Power Ring isn't something that gets packed away after game night. Made to be worn every day, it combines the look of a premium ring with the satisfying interaction of a spinning fidget accessory.

The rotating D20 band provides a smooth, tactile motion that's enjoyable whether you're making a roll, thinking through your next move, or simply keeping your hands busy throughout the day.

Comfortable enough for daily wear and distinctive enough to spark conversation, Power Ring brings a piece of your adventure with you wherever you go.

Outer Dragon Shell– Crafted from zinc alloy, the Dragon Shell forms the outer layer of the Power Ring and gives the ring its fantasy-inspired appearance. Featuring dragon artwork and detailed textures created through the die-casting process, this part was made to feel like a true artifact from a legendary world. It also serves as the protective outer structure, surrounding and shielding the spinning mechanism inside the ring.

D20 Number Ring -The D20 Number Ring is crafted from brass using a precision die-casting process, creating crisp, highly detailed numbering, a premium solid feel, and most importantly, a smooth inner surface that allows the ring to rotate effortlessly.

As the rotating center band of the Power Ring, the D20 Number Ring is responsible for every roll. Its spacing, balance, and rotation were refined through multiple design revisions to achieve a smooth, satisfying spin while preserving the unpredictability and excitement of a real D20 roll.

Bearing Housing Ring -Made from CNC-machined aluminum, this internal spinning system guides the movement of the D20 Number Ring. The component was carefully refined to help the ring rotate smoothly and consistently while keeping the movement controlled and stable during spins.

Inner Wearable Ring- Made from CNC-machined aluminum, this is the core structure that sits on the finger and supports the full Power Ring assembly. It provides comfort, stability, and a balanced feel while securely holding all moving components together.

Precision Bearing Pins- The bearings help connect and stabilize the spinning system inside the ring. They play an important role in maintaining the smooth movement, alignment, and overall performance of the rotating mechanism.

The Ring In Action

When it's time to roll, simply flick the D20 band and let fate decide.

The numbered band spins smoothly around the ring before slowing to a stop. Once it comes to rest, the result is read using the gold roll indicator.

A quick, simple, and satisfying way to roll a D20 - always within reach.

The Making of Power Ring

The D20 ring we’ve been refining for over 2 years.

Power Ring is the final result of dozens of different design concepts, prototype samples, and material tests. From the beginning, our goal was simple: create a wearable D20 ring that spins smoothly, feels satisfying in hand, and still keeps the randomness of a real D20 roll.

To achieve this, we went through multiple rounds of testing, refining mechanisms, adjusting materials, and improving the spinning system. We worked closely with different designers and suppliers to fine-tune the balance, durability, bearing movement, and overall feel of the ring.

Every prototype helped shape the final design — leading to the version of Power Ring you see today.

The Power Ring features an inner diameter of 17 mm and is designed to be worn comfortably on different positions of the index finger, depending on your individual finger size.

Its versatile design also allows it to be worn on the thumb or middle finger, making it suitable for both the left and right hand.

Since finger sizes vary from person to person, the fit may differ slightly depending on where you choose to wear the ring. The 17 mm inner diameter offers flexibility for multiple wearing styles while maintaining comfort and functionality
