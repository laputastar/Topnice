Risks and challenges
Astral is a new format, which comes with real challenges. As a first-time launch, artist onboarding and community adoption are unknowns we're taking seriously. We've navigated this by building relationships with Boulder's music community well before launch—partnering with Stone Cottage Studios, Nameless Art Collective, Drip Rhythm Community Dances, and 
getting buy-in from many local artists and audiences.  

Event production carries its own variables—attendance, logistics, turnout. All three showcase events are free and hosted at established Boulder venues with experienced partners, which significantly reduces that risk.

The honest truth is that Astral is a pilot. We are testing a new format in a real community, gathering real feedback, and building from there.

AstralOwn Your Music, Support Real Artists

Astral is a retro-futuristic album card, inspired by the charm and collectibility of trading cards. For fans, they are a way todirectly supportan artist, receive something personal in return, and feel genuinely connected to the music and the person behind it.

In a culture shaped by frictionless streaming and convenience, musicians earn fractions of a cent per play while fans have few meaningful ways to contribute and connect to the artists they love. Concert prices have risen to compensate, making live music less accessible. Physical music formats—while beloved—struggle to integrate into modern life. The meaningful bonds between artists and their communities have been quietly eroding, as social media andbig musiccontrol talent and attention.Astral exists to creatively respond.Astral is about re-materializing music as something tangible, ownable, and culturally connective. It's about creating a new economic pathway for working artists. And it's about restoring something that streaming quietly took from us: that art can actually belong to you, held in your hand, and valued.How it WorksUsing NFC (near field communication), each card taps to your phone, and the artists's music is yours toown— in the form of a high resolution download and access to a portal of exclusive updates and material. The format is designed from the ground up to meet the modern listener where they are—bridging the intimacy of physical music ownership with the convenience of digital access. We're treating music not as passive consumption, but as a living cultural exchange—one built on non-extractive economics, empowering a diverse creative community, and the belief that music, at its best, is where shared joy and financial empowerment find their rightful alignment.

The 2026 Launch

Astral's inaugural launch series is an opportunity to pilot this format within a vibrant scene of independent musicians in Boulder, CO—getting Astral cards into the hands of real music fans and gathering immediate, real-world feedback on the cultural and practical resonance of the format.

Astral's maiden voyage will produce a cohesive, limited edition collection featuring several dozen independent artists—driving arts lovers to collect, discover, and directly support the music around them. Each card is part of something larger: a shared release that makes individual artists part of a collectible cultural moment.

The launch centers around three showcase-style live events—part concert, part social marketplace, where artists both perform and offer cards at their booths.

Artists keep 75%+ of revenue for the launch series.

This series acts as a foundation from which Astral can take a validated stance in the greater music and arts ecosystem Boulder—as well as a basis from which to develop ourV2 cards, which will support immediate, in-hand playback without the use of a phone.
