It Starts with the Sound

Two effects with real character. TheDelayruns from a clean digital echo to a warped, breathing tape machine and stacks up into long, endless trails without ever running away. TheReverbis a lush studio plate you canfreezeand play right over the top of. Studio-grade sound, made to be pushed live.

Hands-On First, Deep When You Want It

Three dedicated knobs cover the essentials, so the core of each effect is immediate. Grab them and you have a sound in seconds:

When you want to go further, anendless encoderwith aPARAM buttonsteps through everything else - eight pages on the Delay, six on the Reverb - shown one at a time on theround colour display. Turn faster to move faster, so big and fine changes both feel natural.

Shallow learning curve, deep ceiling.Nothing is buried, and nothing is in your way.

Tempo and Recording

Knows the beat - no tapping required

AnAuto-BPMalgorithm detects the tempo in the incoming audio in real time and shows it big on the display - and itworks with vinyl, too. Prefer to set it yourself?Tapthe beat, or lock toMIDI clockover USB. Tempo drives the delay cycles, the reverb tremolo, and the beat LEDs pulse to.

Record your set with no extra interface

Aclass-compliant USB audio interfaceis built in - plug in over USB-C and the unit shows up as an audio input,driverless on macOS, Windows and iOS.Record the output straight into your DAW or smartphone and choose the sourcePRE(dry) orPOST(processed). No separate recorder, no drivers.

Fits the Mixer You Allready Own

The MDFX units mount magnetically to the side of your mixer, vertical or horizontal, and connect as a master insert or via aux send. They extend the setup you already trust instead of replacing it.

True stereo, balanced 6.35 mm jacks, and a full100% dry to 100% wetrange mean they sit happily in front ofPioneer, Allen & Heath, Xone, Rane, Model 1and many more.
