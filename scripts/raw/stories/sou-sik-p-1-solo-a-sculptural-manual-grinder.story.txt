SOU SIK Knows the Grind.

Hand grinding is not just about making coffee smaller.

It is resistance. Rhythm. Precision. Flavor. Tactile joy.It is the quiet ritual before a better cup.

If you use a hand grinder often, you know the feeling: uneven resistance, stuttering motion, and a broken rhythm.

P-1 Solo began with one simple question:why should hand grinding still feel this rough?

P-1 Solo uses a planetary gearset to make hand grinding smoother, steadier, and easier to control.With a 1:3.1 gear ratio, the crank requires less effort than conventional hand grinders, turning resistance into a lighter, more consistent grinding rhythm.

Less hesitation. Less sticking. More mechanical pleasure in every turn.

P-1 Solo uses a larger burr designto improve grinding efficiency and reduce resistance through the grind. With smoother bean flow and a steadier cutting process,each grind feels more consistent, more controlled, and more satisfying.

Co-developed with LEBREW, P-1 Solo’s replaceable burr system gives you two distinct grind profiles.

Burr 01 is made for clean, bright, layered flavors.Burr 02 is made for sweeter, fuller, more intense cups.

2 rotations. 120 clicks.From espresso to cupping, P-1 Solo gives you visible, repeatable control across the full grind range.

P-1 Solo is more than a grinder. Its layered internal structure, visible mechanics, and modular burr system give it the feel of a true mechanical coffee toy — precise, tactile, and enjoyable to explore.
