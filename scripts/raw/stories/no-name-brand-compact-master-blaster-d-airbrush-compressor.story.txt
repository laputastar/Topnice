In search for a perfect airbrush compressor, we landed on our next chapter – digitally controlled version of our Compact Master Blaster.

We wanted something that can be versatile enough to satisfy majority of airbrush users: compact and quiet enough for miniature painters to keep it as a tabletop air supply with convenient airbrush holders and accessible controls, yet powerful enough to keep up with custom painting projects on a helmet or motorcycle.

These are some of the solutions you can find in this new model:

Digital controller that allows users to adjust auto start/stop settings, so compressor can be better optimized for the job.

Aluminum air tank – not only ensures stable air flow without any pulsation but also set for longevity with rust-free material.

Protective shell get a secondary function with magnetic airbrush holders that now can be adjusted and positioned where user wants them.

A quick glimpse to a bright display is enough to see the actual working pressure in the air hose.

We appreciate you support and our team is determined to continue bringing you the best products for your hobby or profession!

Environmental commitments

Long-lasting design

One of the most common failure points of an airbrush compressor for airbrushing is its tank. Made of steel, it can develop rust-caused air leaks if not drained after each use. Our new compressor comes with an aluminum air tank. We still recommend draining it often, but it's much more forgiving and rust-free.
