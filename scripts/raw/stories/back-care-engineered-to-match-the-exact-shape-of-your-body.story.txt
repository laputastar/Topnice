Risks and challenges
Hello Kickstarter! Entering the era of mass customization brings a myriad of opportunities and benefits. However, this ambitious approach also presents several risks and challenges that require careful consideration and strategic management.

Privacy and Security: Using the 3D Avatar Body application to capture personal body measurements and create a digital avatar involves the collection, transmission and storage of sensitive personal data. We will compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) to prevent risk of data breaches or unauthorized access.

Technology Reliability: Relying on advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence, 3D modeling, and large-scale 3D printing introduces the risk of technical failure or inaccuracy. WE are confident in effectiveness of the technology in accurately capturing body dimensions and translating them into personalized products is critical to customer satisfaction.

Supply chain complexity: We are ready for mass customization adds significant complexity to the supply chain and manufacturing processes. Our team is prepared to manage the production of highly personalized products at scale requires sophisticated logistics and can lead to longer production times, higher costs, and potential bottlenecks in the supply chain.
Material sustainability and quality: We use materials made from more than 50% recycled water bottles requires ensuring consistent quality and performance of these recycled materials across products can be challenging. Material durability, comfort and resilience are critical to product success and customer satisfaction.
