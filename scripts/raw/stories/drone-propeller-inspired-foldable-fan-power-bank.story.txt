Meet PropGo — a compact power station that cools you and charges your gear. Power up to four devices at once while staying comfortably cool. When you don’t need airflow, the blades fold flat and PropGo becomes a slim, pocket‑friendly power bank.

Drone-Inspired Cooling

Dual propeller blades inspired by drone design plus a brushless motor deliver high airflow with low noise for long, efficient cooling.

Removable Blade Guard

Smooth speed control between 4,400–5,200 RPM to match your comfort.

Folding Design – Compact & Portable

Blades fold into the body to transform PropGo into a slim, pocket‑friendly 14,000mAh power bank.

No bulky protrusions – just clean, compact portability that slips easily into your bag or backpack.

45W Fast Charge & 8.2V DC Output

The USB‑C port handles both input and output at 45W.

Plug in to recharge the internal battery from empty to full in just 2 hours. On the output side, it delivers enough power to charge laptops, tablets, and handheld gaming consoles at their optimal speed—no wall outlet needed.

Unlike standard power banks, PropGo includes a DC 8.2V output—ideal for 2S battery chargers, camera battery plates, portable speakers, rotary tools, and DIY electronics.

While USB‑C and USB‑A cover your daily phone and tablet needs, this extra port is a thoughtful addition for photographers, makers, and professionals.

Charge Multiple Devices Simultaneously

Power up to four devices while the fan runs.

Integrated Type‑C and Lightning cables eliminate extra cords. External ports include USB‑C (PD), USB‑A, and DC 8.2V for flexible connections.

Supports multiple fast charge protocols:

✅ FCP (in/out)✅ AFC (in/out)✅ DRP Try.SRC, PD 3.1 (in/out)✅ QC 2.0 / 3.0 / 3.0+ (out)

That means it is seamlessly compatible with your MacBook, iPhone, Huawei, Samsung, Nintendo Switch, and most other mainstream devices.

High‑Capacity 51.8Wh Battery

This drone‑inspired fan doubles as a serious 51.8Wh (≈14,000mAh) power bank.

It has enough juice to recharge laptops, iPads, phones, Nintendo Switch, and more—with fast PD charging, multi‑protocol compatibility, simultaneous outputs, and comprehensive safety features.

Four LED indicators show battery level at a glance: 100%, 75%, 50%, or 25%.

164W Smart Protection

An intelligent protection system monitors total output. If combined output exceeds 164W, PropGo will auto‑cut and safely restart to prevent overheating or short circuits — no manual reset needed.

We’re a small team of industrial designers, electrical engineers, and outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to replace multiple devices with one smarter tool. After three years designing portable power solutions, we focused on making something that’s useful, durable, and genuinely convenient.

Our Design Philosophy

Form follows function — but function should be thoughtful.

The drone-propeller aesthetic isn't a gimmick. We chose it because drone blades are engineered for one thing: moving maximum air with minimum noise and energy. That same aerodynamic efficiency makes our fan powerful, quiet, and long-lasting.

We believe every feature should earn its place. That’s why the blades fold—so when you don’t need the fan, you’re not stuck with an awkward, bulky shape. It becomes a slim, pocket‑ready power bank. No compromises. Just smart, intentional design.
