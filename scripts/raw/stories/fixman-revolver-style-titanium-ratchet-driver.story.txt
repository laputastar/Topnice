Not Typical Ratchet System

Custom-Designed Hidden Ratchet System

Most ratchet drivers are built around off-the-shelf mechanisms. It’s a practical shortcut, but it can come with compromises: steel internal parts that may rust over time, less intuitive direction switching, and extra exposed components.

FixMan takes a different path.We designed our hidden ratchet system from the inside out and integrated it directly into the titanium body for a cleaner look, faster mode switching, and a more refined user experience.

No bulky add-on. No exposed ratchet module. No shortcut hidden inside.

Even the internal ratchet components are CNC-machined from titanium — because the parts you don’t see still matter.

3 Modes. Smooth Switching.

Tighten Mode：Drive screws forward with smooth, continuous ratcheting.

Loosen Mode：Quickly reverse and remove screws with ease.

Locked Mode：Disable the ratchet for full manual control.

Smooth action.Solid feedback.Your tool. Your control.

Stores 10 Standard 1/4" Bits up to 53 mm

Revolver-Style Bit Chamber

Smooth rotation. Fits 1/4" bits up to 53 mm. No extra bit case.

FixMan’s revolver-style bit chamber stores up to 10 standard 1/4" (6.35 mm) bits, each held securely in its own magnetic slot.

Each slot can fit 1/4" bits up to 53 mm long, so even longer bits can stay organized inside the tool. Combined with the built-in bolt-action extension,FixMan can reach deep into tight spaces — up to about 75 mm beyond the body.

Rotate the selector to find the bit you need. Each satisfying click locks firmly into place, keeping your most-used bits organized, secure, and close at hand.

10 Bits Included: PH1 / PH2 / PH3 · SL4 / SL6 · H3 / H4 / H5 · T20 / T25.

Designed for fast swaps and dependable retention, the holder keeps your bits firmly locked during use while remaining easy to change whenever needed.
