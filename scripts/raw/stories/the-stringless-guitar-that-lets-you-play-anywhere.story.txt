What's in the box

Our design is fully original. For the guitar’s front panel, we take inspiration from high-quality audio speakers and use an acoustically inspired woven fabric surface, rather than the solid plastic panels commonly found on traditional guitars.

In speaker design, woven acoustic fabrics are widely used to help optimize sound transmission and diffusion. They reduce unwanted reflections and “muffled” resonance while allowing sound to pass more naturally and evenly.

Applied to our guitar, this structure helps deliver a clearer, brighter, and more transparent tone, with improved detail and a more refined overall sound character.

Traditional stringless guitars often place the volume control on the side for aesthetics. However, we found this is less intuitive in real use.

That’s why we moved the volume control to the top of the front panel, making it more direct and easier to adjust during play. We also integrated the power and control functions into a single switch, simplifying operation and improving the overall user experience.
