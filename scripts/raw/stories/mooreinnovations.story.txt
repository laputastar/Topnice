The Story Behind SqueakPoint™
SqueakPoint™ didn’t start in a lab.
It started on my living‑room floor.
One afternoon, my
10‑year‑old daughter
, a competitive
gymnast
, was playing with our dog
Cooper
. She had pulled the squeakers out of his toys, slipped them into her socks, and started running around the house while Cooper chased her, going wild every time he heard the squeak.
I laughed—until something clicked.
In gymnastics, young athletes constantly struggle with
pointing their toes
at the right moments. It’s not because they don’t try. It’s because they
forget
in motion. My daughter has battled this exact issue, and those small mistakes lead to
real point deductions
in competition.
That’s when I wondered:
What if feedback happened instantly—without a coach yelling, without stopping the routine?
I asked her to try something.
“Do a cartwheel and a round‑off.”
She went.
BAM — squeak.
Flat foot. No toe point.
Again.
Squeak.
Again.
Then something changed.
She focused. She adjusted. She tried again.
This time—
silence
.
No squeak.
I said, “That’s the goal. Don’t squeak. Point your toes.”
In that moment,
SqueakPoint™ became real
.
Why This Matters
Kids don’t learn best from lectures.
They learn from
immediate, intuitive feedback
—especially when they’re moving fast and thinking about a dozen things at once.
Most training tools today rely on electronics, apps, and screens. But kids don’t need more technology. They need
simple signals they can feel and understand instantly
.
SqueakPoint™ is a
battery‑free, electronics‑free mechanical training tool
that delivers real‑time physical feedback using
physics and biomechanics
. If movement is done correctly, there’s silence. If not—you hear it.
No charging.
No screens.
No distractions.
Just learning in the moment.
Why We’re Here
We’re launching SqueakPoint™ on Indiegogo to bring this simple idea from our home to families, athletes, and coaches everywhere. Your support helps us finalize testing, refine production, and bring SqueakPoint™ to market.
This project is deeply personal to me—as a parent, a founder, and someone who believes the best innovations often start with paying attention to real life.
Thank you for being part of this story.
—
Jon Moore
Founder, Moore Innovations
