A Cute Retro-Style Smart Clock Combines Gentle Wake-up AI Tutor, Storyteller to Help Kids Build Independent Sleep & Daily Routines

Environmental commitments

As Parents and  Electronics Design Team with over 10 years of experience, we know the daily struggle: chaotic mornings, screen-fueled bedtime battles, and the toll it takes on kids' sleep, eyesight, and routines. Traditional alarms are harsh; screens are addictive. So we asked: what if we could create more than just an alarm clock?

The answer is PRUNUS — a purpose-built Pomodoro timer and daily routine tool designed exclusively for children. After months of research into kids' learning focus and sleep habits, we independently developed the hardware, timing logic, and kid-friendly operating system. It helps children build healthy time management habits: focused study with the Pomodoro technique, regular rest reminders, and consistent sleep schedules — all through a gentle bedside companion that tells stories, answers questions, and plays music, helping kids naturally let go of screen dependence.

✅ Core Functions (Main Features)

Custom Pomodoro Timer: Multiple preset time modes for kids’ study, reading and break. We designed all timing modes based on child psychology.

Sleep Schedule Management: Built-in countdown mode helps children develop regular bedtime habits.

Simple & Safe Hardware Design: Ergonomic shape and easy-to-use buttons, fully designed and tested by our team for young children.

✅Auxiliary Voice FeaturesThis device is equipped with a basic voice module powered by AI technology. This is only a secondary supporting function, not the core of the product.All development work including hardware, timing system and content production is completed independently by our creative and R&D team. Voice auxiliary function is a minor part of the whole project.

✅ Cute Retro Compact Design + Colorful LED ScreenMini square vintage chassis with rounded-edge, classic red/soft blue two optional colorways, cute tripod foot stand + rotating top control knob, vivid gradient LED display showing real-time clock , ergonomic kid-friendly button layout for easy independent operation by small hand

✅Project status and roadmapReady before launch: child-safe industrial design, core functions verified (AI voice, alarm clock, night light), food-grade ABS shell, key suppliers confirmed, and passed necessary safety tests (power, heat resistance, drop, battery).

Post-investment plan: precision mold production → small batch trial production → comprehensive safety certification → mass production → environmentally friendly packaging (including English) → global shipping.
