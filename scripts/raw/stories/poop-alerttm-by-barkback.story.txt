Meet Poop-Alert™ by BarkBack

Poop-Alert™ by BarkBack is a motion-activated talking yard sign designed to remind dog owners to clean up after their pets in a fun, attention-grabbing way.Unlike ordinary signs that people ignore, Poop-Alert™ speaks up when someone walks nearby using a built-in motion sensor and customizable voice messages.

Dog waste has become a frustrating problem for homeowners, neighborhoods, parks, and businesses. Traditional yard signs are often ignored, leaving property owners dealing with an unpleasant and unsanitary issue.

We wanted to create something impossible to ignore — a sign that actually speaks up.

After months of testing and refining prototypes, we developed Poop-Alert™ by BarkBack: a motion-activated talking sign designed to grab attention in a fun, memorable, and effective way.

Risks and challenges

We have already completed working prototypes and spent months testing and refining the current Poop-Alert™ system. Through real-world testing, we’ve continued learning how to improve durability, motion sensitivity, weather resistance, and overall user experience.

Kickstarter funding will help us move into expanded production while also supporting future improvements and the development of next-generation Poop-Alert™ features and designs.

Like any hardware-based product, manufacturing timelines and electronic component sourcing can present challenges, but we are actively working with suppliers and refining our production process to help ensure quality and reliability.

Our goal is to communicate openly with backers throughout the process and deliver a product that continues improving over time.

Environmental commitments

Long-lasting design

Poop-Alert™ has been designed with durability and long-term outdoor use in mind. The sign is built using weather-resistant materials and a rechargeable power system, with an optional solar panel for extended use. We continue testing and improving the design to increase reliability, durability, and overall product lifespan

Reusability and recyclability

Many components of the Poop-Alert™ system can be reused, replaced, or recycled, helping reduce unnecessary waste while extending the life of the product.

Poop-Alert™ also supports rechargeable power options, including an optional solar panel attachment designed to reduce battery waste and extend outdoor usability over time. Our goal is to continue improving the product with long-term usability and reduced maintenance in mind.
