Risks and challenges
At ZAVOD, we approach every project with a clear understanding of the risks involved in watch production — and with practical experience in managing them.
Our team is behind the successful RIDER1991 and REAL TIME watch projects, both developed and delivered through structured production processes. Over more than 15 years of hands-on experience in the watch industry, we have built reliable relationships with manufacturing partners, suppliers, and logistics providers.
This experience allows us to plan production realistically, maintain quality control, and respond quickly if adjustments are required.

Production Readiness

THE GENERAL is developed on a proven platform using established components, including the Miyota 9015 automatic movement and long-term manufacturing partners in Ukraine and Hong Kong.
We apply a careful partner selection process and work only with suppliers that meet our technical and quality standards. This significantly reduces typical risks associated with new product launches.

Our team is fully prepared to move into production immediately after the campaign.

Operating in a challenging environment

We also believe in being transparent with our backers.
ZAVOD is a Ukrainian company, and this project is being developed during wartime. This reality brings additional operational challenges that many brands do not face. At the same time, it has shaped our approach to work: focus, discipline, and responsibility for what we create and deliver.

We do not treat this as a limitation — but as a commitment to do our work as well as we possibly can.

Our commitment

THE GENERAL is a project about responsibility — and we apply that principle to the way we manage production, timelines, and communication with our backers. 
We are fully committed to delivering watches that meet the standards presented in this campaign and to keeping our backers informed throughout the process.

Risks & Challenges

THE GENERALis a modern field watch shaped by real experience. Created in Ukraine during wartime, it reflects a mindset forged under pressure — clarity, resilience, and action.

Built for real-world endurance, it combines a 200m water-resistant steel case, a flat sapphire crystal with anti-reflective coating, and the provenMiyota 9015 automaticmovement. High legibility is ensured by applied luminous markers, Super-LumiNova hands, and a structured dial designed for fast reading in all conditions. Inspired by modern tactical aesthetics, it brings together mechanical reliability, durable materials, and uncompromising functionality.

Story. Philosophy. Idea

The General is a Ukrainian automatic field watch created during wartime — in honor of those who chose to stand up when neutrality was no longer possible. The idea was born in the first months of the full-scale invasion, when every Ukrainian faced a choice. Volunteers. Medics. Engineers. Civilians. Soldiers. The name emerged after a landmark article by General Valerii Zaluzhnyi published inTIME.
