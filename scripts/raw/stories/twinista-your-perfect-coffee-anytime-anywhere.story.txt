Four simple steps to your barista moment.

Just a straightforward workflow that feels familiar if you know espresso, and easy even if you are new.

Step 1: Heat(Gas burner, stove, fire, grill, embers)Step 2: Tamp(fresh grounds and tamp or use E.S.E. pod.)Step 3: Take & Turn(Detach the tank, preheat with two turns, lock portafilter)Step 4: Twist & Enjoy(Crank steadily for ~25 seconds)
