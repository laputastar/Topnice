Active cooling. More than speed

Traditional hubs rely on passive cooling, allowing heat to accumulate inside the enclosure.SoLid features an active cooling system that helps circulate airflow through the device, reducing heat buildup and supporting more consistent performance during intensive use.

Don't let heat limit your performance.SoLid's active cooling fan + multi-zone airflow keeps SSDs stable — reliable for high-speed transfers, pro recording, and long hours of creation.

SoLid features a high-speed active cooling fan and a large-area honeycomb ventilation design. Cool air flows in through the vents around the chassis, and the fan creates a steady airflow that quickly carries heat away from the SSDs, controller chip, and PCB.
