1. Manufacturing Challenges
Bringing a new product to life on Kickstarter inevitably comes with challenges — from production timelines to maintaining uncompromising quality standards. 
We are not relying on third-party coordination. We are a fully integrated team with our own factory, managing the entire process in-house — from product design and engineering, to prototyping, manufacturing, campaign launch, and final fulfillment.
Before launch, every product goes through extensive testing and validation to ensure it is truly production-ready. Because design and manufacturing work under one roof, we can move faster, maintain tighter quality control, and solve issues immediately when they arise.
With direct oversight at every stage, we’re able to deliver a smoother production process, greater consistency, and more reliable fulfillment for our backers.

2. Fulfillment & Shipping
Delivering rewards on time is a critical part of creating a great experience for our backers. We understand that international logistics, customs procedures, and fluctuating demand can sometimes impact shipping timelines.
To prepare for this, we have already developed a comprehensive fulfillment strategy and partnered with reputable global logistics providers to ensure reliable and efficient delivery.
Throughout the entire process — from production to final shipment — we will keep our backers informed with regular updates. Once orders are shipped, tracking information will be provided so you can follow your package every step of the way.

Our Commitment
While every Kickstarter project involves some level of uncertainty, we are fully committed to delivering what we promise.
We have laid a strong foundation through careful planning, reliable partnerships, and thorough preparation. If any challenges arise along the way, we will address them promptly and communicate transparently with our backers.
