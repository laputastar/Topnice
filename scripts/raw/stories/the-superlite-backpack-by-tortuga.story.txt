[RISKS & CHALLENGES]
Risks and challenges
We've worked hard to get the Superlite ready for Kickstarter and full-scale manufacturing. In the Spring, James and I spent 11 days in Vietnam working through samples with the factory and their sample room.

We're confident in our ability to produce high-quality bags at scale because we’ve been doing it since 2010. This isn’t our first production run.

That said, things don't always go as planned. Here are the risks we see and our plan to address them:

FABRIC: ULTRA™ is a premium, specialty fabric made in small runs with long lead times. It’s not readily available at our production scale. Supply constraints are possible. Our supplier, Challenge Sailcloth, is aware of our plans and has reserved fabric from their upcoming production run specifically for this Kickstarter. Any rewards beyond the first 1,000 bags will be delivered in a second shipment of finished goods.

MANUFACTURING: We have a trusted manufacturing partner, Jion, lined up and will conduct quality assurance checks during and after production. Manufacturing delays can still happen. Bags are physical products. If we run into any problems, we will send an update through Kickstarter immediately. Quality comes first, followed by timely delivery.

FULFILLMENT: We're using an experienced logistics team to deliver your products. Sometimes stuff happens that can't be planned for: a supplier misses a deadline, a package gets held up at customs, a shipment gets delayed. We'll keep the communication open and honest with you so you always know where your order stands and when it's arriving.

The more successful the campaign, the greater the risk of delays based on fabric availability and production capacity. We aim to fulfill pledges in the order in which they were received.

Lightweight and Durable

The Superlite Backpack from Tortuga is designed for ultralight travelers who refuse to compromise on durability or comfort.

Weighing just2.2 lbs (1 kg)and built from ULTRA™, with fibers 15x stronger than steel by weight, the Superlite will help you pack lighter, move faster, and go farther without ever worrying about your bag.

Walk Through the Superlite

Made with ULTRA™

Most lightweight fabrics are too delicate for world travel. Not this one.

The Superlite's balance of weight and strength comes from ULTRA™, the most advanced pack fabric in the world. ULTRA™ is lightweight, waterproof, and abrasion-resistant with fibers15x stronger than steel by weight.

At Tortuga, we've been making travel backpacks for 16 years. In that time, we've been named both aWirecutter pickand aPack Hacker pick.

Almost90,000 travelershave carried our bags on their most memorable trips. They've rated us 4.6 (out of 5) across more than 7,700 reviews.

Your Luggage is Too Heavy

Travel can be a drag. Your trip of a lifetime shouldn’t be weighed down by physical and mental baggage.

Suitcases are too big and heavy for world travel. But even travel backpacks have gotten too weighed down with unnecessary features.

The Superliteweighs 44% lessthan the average travel backpack.

How light can a travel backpack be without sacrificing durability or comfort? We’ve been working on this problem for a decade.

The Superlite uses only14% of your carry on weight limit(15 lb, 7 kg), even on the strictest airlines. The rest is yours for clothes, gear, and everything else you actually want to bring.

Never Check a Bag Again

The Superlite is 34L so it’scarry on compliant, worldwide. Your bag always fits in the overhead bin. Yes, even on Ryanair.

No checked bags. No wasted time. No dead weight.

Comfortable on Long Days

Most ultralight bags skimp on the harness. They use thin straps without padding and load lifters. They punish your shoulders to save weight.

For the Superlite, comfort was the only "feature" we weren't willing to cut. The Superlite isn'tjustlightweight. It also has all the comfort features Tortuga is known for.

For long carries or heavier loads, add the optional hip belt for even more support. The hip belt will transfer up to 80% of the weight from your shoulders to your hips.
