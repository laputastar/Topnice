Risks and challenges
This is our 18th Kickstarter project, and over the years we’ve gained a lot of experience bringing ideas from concept to production and delivery. Our team has successfully developed and fulfilled many campaigns, and we’ve built strong relationships with trusted manufacturing and logistics partners along the way.

HUNT is already well into development, with working pre-production samples completed and tested. Our manufacturing partners are experienced in producing titanium products, and we’ve planned our production timeline carefully to ensure a smooth transition into mass production.

As with any project, the most common challenge could be logistics or shipping delays, particularly if the campaign exceeds expectations (which would be fantastic!). To prepare for this possibility, we’ve built extra buffer time into our production schedule and coordinated early with our suppliers and fulfillment partners to handle higher volumes if needed.

Transparency is very important to us. Throughout the campaign and production process, we’ll keep backers informed with regular updates. If any unexpected issues arise, we’ll communicate openly and share exactly how we’re addressing them.

Please note that the products shown in our photos and videos are pre-mass production samples. During the manufacturing process, small refinements—such as minor design adjustments or size optimizations—may be made to ensure the best quality and performance.

Thank you for trusting us to bring HUNT to life. We’re excited to deliver this project and can’t wait to see where your adventures take it.
