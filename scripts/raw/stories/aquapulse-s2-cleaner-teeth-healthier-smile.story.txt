Risks and challenges
We have completed the core development and prototype testing of AquaPulse S2, including ultrasonic cleaning, five cleaning modes, the built-in water tank rinsing function, charging, and waterproof design. However, as with any hardware product, there may still be challenges when moving from prototype to mass production.

First, during production, we may face potential issues such as component supply delays, mold adjustments, or changes in the manufacturing schedule. To reduce these risks, we will maintain close communication with our suppliers, confirm the availability of key components in advance, and conduct small-batch trial production before full-scale manufacturing to ensure stable product quality.

Second, the waterproof performance, water spray function, battery life, and ultrasonic cleaning performance all need to be carefully tested during the production stage. Before shipping, we will carry out functional testing and quality inspections to make sure each unit works properly and delivers the experience we promised.

In addition, international shipping may be affected by customs clearance, weather conditions, holidays, or logistics arrangements. If any unexpected delays occur, we will keep backers updated in a timely and transparent manner and provide clear shipping progress whenever possible.

We truly value the trust of every backer. While challenges may arise during the project, we are fully prepared to move forward responsibly and transparently through production, testing, and fulfillment, and we will do our best to deliver AquaPulse S2 successfully to all our supporters.
