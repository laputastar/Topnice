This Isn't Just a Couch, It's a Soccer Goal.

Goali transforms from a comfortable kids couch into an indoor soccer goal in seconds.

Created for active kids and modern family homes, Goali brings playtime and everyday furniture together in one clever design.

Designed to give kids a place to play without taking over the family living room.

Goali combines everyday seating and active play in one compact design, transforming from a couch into an indoor soccer goal in seconds.

Goali Transforms in Seconds

Simply remove the cushions and lift up the couch.

The round disc shaped cushions double as targets which can be hung from the left centre or right hand side of the crossbar.

Adjust the height of the targets to set different challenges. The targets easily attach to the velcro parts of the crossbar.

Keep Track of Every Score

Goali also comes with 10 x removable velcro scoring patches.

Unlike traditional foam play furniture, Goali features an internal reinforcement system that securely connects key foam panels inside the cover.

Combined with high-density foam construction, this helps maintain shape, improve stability during play, and ensure reliable performance in both couch and goal mode.

Designed to be used, transformed, and enjoyed every day.
