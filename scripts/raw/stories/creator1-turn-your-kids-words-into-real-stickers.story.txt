Risks and challenges
Creator 1's tooling and molds are complete. We are in the pre-production phase — the physical hardware exists and works. The risks ahead are about time, not invention.

Software and hardware integration.
Final tuning of the software-hardware system may take longer than planned. If it does, we expect a delay of approximately one month from our current estimate. We will not ship a product that hasn't passed our internal quality bar — we'd rather be honest about a delay than rush something into your child's hands.

Safety certification and compliance.
Creator 1 is a children's product, and we take that seriously. Drop testing and regulatory certifications across key markets (including children's product safety standards in the US, EU, and other regions) can extend beyond initial timelines. We have already submitted our applications. If certification takes longer than expected, this may push our ship date. We will update backers the moment we know more.

Our commitment.
We will post a project update immediately if either of these timelines shifts — not weeks later, not buried in fine print. Backers who've trusted us with their money deserve to know first.
