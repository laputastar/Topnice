[RISKS & CHALLENGES]
Risks and challenges
We’ve been building oLand since 2023 through concept development, prototyping, engineering samples, and repeated refinements to both the hardware and the overall experience. That gives us strong confidence in the product, but we also want to be transparent: bringing a product like oLand from prototype to full-scale production comes with real challenges.

Because oLand combines a holographic display system, AI interaction, and physical hardware, the main risks are manufacturing scale-up, software readiness, and logistics. Final production can reveal unexpected issues with component availability, assembly consistency, or calibration. To reduce these risks, we are continuing to refine the product with our manufacturing partners and building time into our schedule for final validation, quality control, and any necessary production adjustments.

On the software side, our AI features will continue to improve after launch. We will be clear about what is ready at shipping and what will be introduced through future updates, so backers know exactly what to expect.

Because oLand is manufactured in mainland China, changes in tariffs, customs policies, or global shipping conditions could affect delivery costs or timelines. We are working closely with our manufacturing and logistics partners to minimize these risks as much as possible.

Most importantly, we are committed to regular, honest communication throughout the process. If challenges come up, we will share updates openly and work hard to deliver a product that lives up to what we’ve promised.

Four Ways to Interact with oLand

Use Pod touch controls, AI voice chat, a tank-facing camera, and mmWave presence sensing to trigger different kinds of interaction.

Pod Touch Controls

Each pet comes with its own character-themed Pod.

Hold to talk:Press and hold the Pod to start AI voice chat. Your pet listens, responds in character, and remembers past conversations — so the next reply can build on what you shared before.

Moli Asks First, You  Press Back:When you’re busy, working, or just not in the mood to speak, your pet can start the interaction for you. Moli may pop up with a simple question on screen — like “How are you feeling right now?” — and you can answer by pressing the Pod to choose A or B. A quiet little check-in, no voice needed.

Tank-Facing Camera

A built-in camera faces only the inside of the tank. It tracks your real marimo, detects your hand, and recognizes objects placed inside the tank.

Reach In. It Notices:When your finger enters the tank area, Moli notices immediately — pausing, turning, and reacting to the movement in front of it.

The Soul of Marimo, Set Adrift:The tank-facing camera locates the real marimo inside the tank, so holographic Moli can appear from the same position instead of a fixed point on screen.

mmWave Radar Presence Detection

Walk up. It wakes:A built-in mmWave radar detects when you're near, so your pet can wake when you approach and settle down when you leave. It also stays synced with local time, weather, and season — so the world inside oLand changes with the real world around it.
