Risks and challenges
Riko has already completed tooling, multiple rounds of validation testing, and is currently in pilot production. Our goal is to begin fulfillment shortly after the Kickstarter campaign concludes.

While we have worked hard to reduce development and manufacturing risks before launch, there are still challenges we continue to monitor as we prepare for global delivery.

Food Compatibility

Freeze-dried foods vary significantly in size, density, ingredients, and absorption characteristics. Different brands and recipes may perform differently during preparation.

What we’re doing:

We have tested Riko with a wide variety of freeze-dried foods and continue refining preparation parameters to improve consistency across different recipes and brands.

Software and Smart Features

Riko includes firmware and app-connected functionality that will continue to evolve after launch.

What we’re doing:

Core feeding functions are fully operational. We will continue releasing software updates to improve reliability, performance, and the overall user experience.

Multi-Cat Access Module

The optional Multi-Cat Access Module is still under development and requires additional testing and validation.

What we’re doing:

The module is expected to ship separately after the main product. We will continue sharing progress and updated timelines with backers throughout development.

Shipping and Fulfillment

International shipping and customs processes may occasionally cause delays beyond our direct control.

What we’re doing:

We are working with experienced logistics partners and will provide regular updates and tracking information throughout fulfillment.

Transparency and Long-Term Support

We believe trust is earned through clear communication and long-term commitment.

What we’re doing:

If challenges arise, we will communicate openly and promptly. We are also exploring replacement parts availability and long-term support options to help owners keep using Riko for years to come.
