[RISKS & CHALLENGES]
Risks and challenges
Bringing any hardware product to market involves challenges, and we believe it's important to be transparent about them.

The good news is that StrokeKeeper is well beyond the concept stage. We have already developed working hardware prototypes, completed extensive real-world testing, and launched the StrokeKeeper app in the Apple App Store. Kickstarter funding will help us transition from pilot production into larger-scale manufacturing, supply, and fulfillment.

That said, there are still risks involved in bringing StrokeKeeper to backers.

Manufacturing and Production

As with any hardware product, manufacturing timelines can be affected by component availability, production scheduling, or unexpected delays from suppliers. To reduce these risks, we have already completed product design and prototyping, identified manufacturing partners, and built additional time into our production schedule.  We have secured an agreement with our smart button manufacturer for the first 1,000 items (buttons/clips) to be available on expedited basis.

Quality Assurance

Delivering a reliable product is one of our highest priorities. Before any smart buttons are shipped to us, production units will undergo quality assurance testing to verify functionality, connectivity, and overall performance. 

Software Development and Support

The StrokeKeeper mobile app is already operational and available in the Apple App Store. While this significantly reduces software-related risk, ongoing updates, bug fixes, operating system changes, and future enhancements will continue after launch. We will actively support the platform and communicate any significant software updates to our community. Android development is complete, an testing/refinements are currenlty ongoing.  We fully anticipate Google Play Store launch prior to Kickstarter Campaign ending.

Supply Chain and Logistics

Global shipping and supply chain disruptions can occasionally affect inventory availability, manufacturing schedules, and delivery timelines. While many of these factors are outside our direct control, we are planning conservatively and building flexibility into our fulfillment schedule to help minimize potential impacts. Of course, this may only impact those orders above and beyond the 1,000 button supply we've secured an agreement for.

Shipping and Fulfillment

Shipping timelines can vary based on carrier performance, customs processing, and regional delivery conditions. We will work with experienced fulfillment partners and provide regular updates throughout the fulfillment process so backers always know where things stand.

Our Commitment to Backers

Perhaps the most important challenge for any Kickstarter project is maintaining clear communication. If delays or unexpected obstacles arise, we commit to being transparent with our backers. We will provide regular updates, explain any challenges we encounter, and share the steps we are taking to keep the project moving forward.

We've invested significant time, resources, and personal passion into developing StrokeKeeper. With a working prototype, a live mobile application, successful field testing, and a defined path to production, we believe we are well-positioned to deliver on our promises and bring StrokeKeeper into the hands of golfers everywhere.

StrokeKeeper is a wearable clip-on device that lets you stay focused on your game. No constant screen checking. No entering scores after every hole. No distractions mid-round. No paper scorecard or lost pencil.

Golf Scoring Pain Points

No more digging for pencils, balancing scorecards,or trying to read your own handwriting on the 16th hole.

Every time you stop to enter a score you step out of your round...and lose focus. Golf requires focus.

No adding scores  between holes.  No recounting strokes.  No slowing down the group.

The StrokeKeeper Solution

Clip it to your hat, belt, or golf gloveSecure, lightweight, always within reach

Tap once per stroke, long-press to advance the holeNo screens. No distractions. Just a simple click
