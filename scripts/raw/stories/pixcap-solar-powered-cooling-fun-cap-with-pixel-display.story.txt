[RISKS & CHALLENGES]
Risks and challenges
Every hardware project comes with its own unique set of logistical and manufacturing complexities, and PIXCAP is no exception. Our team values transparency above all else, which is why we want to outline the potential challenges we may face and exactly how we plan to overcome them:

1. Supply Chain & Hardware Integration
PIXCAP is an intricate blend of electronics and high-quality apparel. Integrating a flexible solar panel, an LCD module, and a custom 3-speed cooling fan into a single, featherlight 171g structure requires exceptional precision during mass production.

Our Solution: We have spent the last several months securing our tooling, conducting pilot production runs, and establishing deep partnerships with mature components suppliers in Shenzhen. Because our custom plastic injection molds and structural testing are already complete, we have significantly minimized the risk of unexpected hardware re-engineering.

2. Component Sourcing & Lead Times
Global electronics sourcing can occasionally suffer from unexpected component shortages, which can impact final assembly timelines.

Our Solution: We maintain buffer stock strategies with our primary suppliers. Additionally, we have already verified secondary, pre-qualified backup vendors for critical modules (including the Bluetooth chips and power management components) to ensure production continues seamlessly even if a bottleneck occurs.

3. Global Logistics & Fulfillment
International shipping dynamics can be unpredictable due to fluctuating customs protocols, clearing times, and regional carrier capacities.

Our Solution: We are partnering with veteran global fulfillment agencies specializing in Kickstarter campaign distribution. All packages will feature end-to-end tracking. By operating on a rolling delivery strategy starting in September 2026, we will dispatch backer rewards in structured batches to ensure an organized and efficient global rollout.

Should any unexpected delays arise, we promise to keep our backer community informed via clear, detailed, and regular project updates. Thank you for your trust and support in bringing PIXCAP to life!
