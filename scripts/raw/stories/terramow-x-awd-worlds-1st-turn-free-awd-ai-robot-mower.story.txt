Risks and challenges
Launching an advanced outdoor robot like TerraMow X involves challenges across manufacturing, certification, logistics, and final quality control. While we have previous Kickstarter fulfillment and mass-production experience, unexpected issues may still affect the schedule.

Manufacturing and Supply Chain
TerraMow X uses advanced components including AI vision sensors, motors, batteries, control systems, and precision mechanical parts. Component availability, production scheduling, or quality-control adjustments may lead to delays.

Certification and Compliance
TerraMow X must meet safety, electrical, wireless, and regional regulatory requirements. Certification timelines may vary by market and could affect delivery schedules in certain regions.

Logistics and Delivery
International shipping, customs clearance, carrier capacity, and last-mile delivery conditions may impact fulfillment timing. We will work closely with logistics partners and keep backers updated if delays occur.

Our Commitment
TerraMow has successfully delivered its first Kickstarter campaign and continued gaining real-world production experience through later product launches. We will apply that experience to TerraMow X, communicate transparently, and do our best to deliver a reliable product to our backers.

Why We Built TerraMow X

After bringing our first-generation TerraMow S series to real homes, we learned that users valued simple setup, reliable AI vision, and practical everyday mowing. But many also asked for something stronger — a mower built for larger lawns, steeper slopes, uneven terrain, and more complex yards.

That feedback led to TerraMow X: our most capable robot mower yet. It keeps TerraMow’s wire-free simplicity and vision-first intelligence, then adds all-wheel drive, Shuttle Drive™ turn-free mowing, stronger cutting power, and expandable capability for real-world lawn challenges

Why TerraMow X Wins

AI Vision Built for Real Yards

Six-camera 3D vision captures detailed surroundings in real time, helping TerraMow X understand your yard quickly and navigate with greater precision.

TerraVision™ 2.0 leads navigation under trees and along walls, while nRTK Assist adds centimeter-level accuracy in open areas.

TerraVision™ 2.0 combines AI semantic perception with 3D visual sensing to recognize common yard objects in real time and adjust its path to reduce mowing interruptions.

Turn-Free Mowing. No Turf Stress.

Shuttle Drive™ Mowing cuts forward and backward, reducing repeated turnarounds and keeping each mowing pass continuous.

Ackermann steering replaces harsh in-place pivots with smooth, natural turns, reducing scuffing, soil compaction, and visible track marks at row ends.

Cleaner Cuts, More Cutting Power

Three cutting motors and a 20-in deck deliver stronger cutting power and wider coverage for tall, dense grass.

Straight blades power through overgrown grass, while razor blades deliver cleaner, finer cuts for routine mowing.

The deck adapts to uneven ground, helping prevent scalping and maintain a consistent cut.

Follows lawn edges and adjusts within ±6 in (15 cm), helping deliver a cleaner, closer finish along borders.

AWD Traction for Tough Terrain

Independent 4-wheel drive delivers power to each wheel for stronger grip on slopes, ruts, roots, and uneven ground — keeping TerraMow X moving where ordinary mowers may struggle.

Conquer steep slopes, uneven terrain with steady traction and control.

Dual suspension cushions bumps and uneven ground, keeping TerraMow X steady for smoother movement.

With up to 2.76 inches of clearance, TerraMow X rolls over roots, rocks, ruts, and uneven patches across real yards.

Modular Design. Easier to Maintain.

Key parts such as the battery pack, cutting deck, and wheel-drive suspension assemblies are designed for easier access and replacement with standard tools — making routine maintenance faster, simple
