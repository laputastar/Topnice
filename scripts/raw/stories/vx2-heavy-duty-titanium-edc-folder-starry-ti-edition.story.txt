Variable VX2Larger Size, More Practical Performance

IfVX1follows acompact and agiledirection, thenVX2takes things further as aheavy-duty EDC folding knife.

It features alarger, more heavyweight formthat still retainseveryday carry practicality, while offering better capability forhigh-intensity outdoor use.

Compared with thecompact, portability-focused VX1,VX2also delivers amore solidandmore capable everyday user experience.

A Distinctive Hand-Finished Blade

One of the most striking features of this knife is itsblade.

The blade surface isfinal hand-finished,usingsandpaper and polishing compound,and gradually refined fromcoarse to fineto create a fine and distinctive surface texture.

The groove area is treated withEDM machining,adding depth and detailfor a richer overall look..

M390 Powder Steel Blade

M390 is a premium blade steel from Austrian Böhler,known for edge retention, corrosion resistance,and dependable real-world performance.

Made throughthird-generation powder metallurgy,it has a fine, uniform carbide structurethat supports strong cutting performanceand lasting edge stability.

For a practicalheavy-duty EDC folder,it is simply the right kind of steel.

What Makes the Blade So Tough?

The blade is made from 4 mm-thick M390 powder steeland heat treated through avacuum oil quench process,followed bycryogenic treatmentandtriple tempering.

The result is a blade hardness of60–62 HRC,giving VX2 the kind oftoughness,edge stability,andlong-lasting sharpnessyou wantin a seriousheavy-duty folder.

Even under heavy, uneven impact, the blade showed no visible damage.

Razor Sharp. Built to Stay That Way.

Even with a 4 mm-thick blade,VX2 does not sacrifice cutting performance.

To achieve this, its highly demanding geometric blade designis finished with a time-intensivehand-ground flat grind,creating anear-ideal V-shaped thin edge.

Less cutting resistance.Cleaner cuts.A smoother overall cutting experience.

Original T-Shaped Blade Geometry

The T-shaped tip gives the knifeastronger, more tactical look,

while reinforcing the overall characterof aheavy-duty folder

and making it better suitedforhigh-intensity outdoor bushcraft use.
