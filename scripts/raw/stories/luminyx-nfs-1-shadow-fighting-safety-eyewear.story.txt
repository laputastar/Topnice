Environmental commitments

Luminyx NFS-1s are safety eyewear with light built in. A soft, even ring of LEDs wraps the inside of the frame, casting diffuse,shadow-fightinglight, exactly where you're looking, nearly eliminating all shadows —no headlamp, no drop light, no holding a flashlight in your teeth.

Because the light comes from all around your line of sight, it fills in every corner and crevice instead of throwing a single harsh beam. Wherever you turn your head, the light goes with you. Control it hands-free, swap the rechargeable batteries when they run low, and stay protected the whole time with ANSI Z87.1-rated lenses.

One pair of glasses. Full protection, hands-free, shadow-eliminating light.
