How BURTRAN Works

Using BURTRAN is simple and takes just a few steps:

1. Put on the maskWear BURTRAN comfortably over your eyes. Its ergonomic design blocks out light and creates an ideal environment for relaxation.

2. Connect to the BURTRAN appPair the mask with the BURTRAN mobile app via Bluetooth and choose the relaxation or power nap session that best suits your needs.

3. Let BURTRAN personalize your experienceBuilt-in sensors monitor your relaxation-related brainwave activity. BURTRAN's AI analyzes this information in real time and adapts your audio experience to help you relax more naturally throughout your session.

4. Recharge in minutesWhether you're on a flight, taking a break at work, or unwinding at home, BURTRAN helps you disconnect, recharge, and return feeling refreshed and ready for what's next.

Designed for Everyday WellnessBURTRAN is designed to support relaxation and recovery in everyday life. It is intended for wellness purposes only and is not a medical device.
