Two Ice Shapes🧊

Most home ice makers give you just one option — one shape, one size, one way to chill.

But everyday drinks and slow-sipping drinksneed different ice.Iced coffee, soda, and juice need quick bullet ice. Whiskey and cocktails deserve large sphere ice that melts slowly.

JoyMayLab gives you both in one machine.

One Machine. Two Ice Types. Every Drink, Covered. 🧊

No extra molds. No second ice maker. No complicated cleanup.

With fast ice-making, one-touch cleaning, smart status indicators, 1-year warranty, and fast shipping, JoyMayLab makes better ice easier for every drink moment.

Large Sphere Ice⚪

You paid good money for that whiskey.Don't let cheap ice ruin it.

Regular ice melts fast — watering down your drink before you're even halfway through. The flavor fades. The experience disappears.

The ice ball melts up to 3x slower than crushed ice.Your whiskey stays cold.Stays strong. Stays exactly the way it was meant to be tasted — sip after sip, to the very last drop.

One ball. Zero compromise.Not just for whiskey.

🔥BBQ & Parties— Keeps every drink cold from the first guest to the last round. No constant refills. No warm beer killing the vibe.

🍓Fruit Sodas & Summer Drinks— Crystal clear, slow-melting, and stunning in the glass. Cold, full-flavored, never watered down.

Fast Bullet Ice✅

Hot day. Warm drink. No ice ready.

With JoyMayLab, press start and fresh bullet ice drops into the basketin 6–8 minutes.

From iced coffee and soda to juice, lemonade, and party drinks, bullet ice helps cool every everyday drink quickly and easily.

After drinks, parties, or daily use, the last thing you want is to take apart and scrub your ice maker.

JOYMayLab makes cleanup simple with a one-touch auto-cleaning mode that helps rinse the system in just 10 minutes.

Less mess after every use. More time to enjoy your drinks — and a fresh machine ready for the next round.

Smart Status Indicators✨

Smart indicators make ice-making easier.

Power light on — the machine is running.Ice Full light on — the basket is full and ready.Red Add Water light on — refill the tank before the next cycle.

Everything is clear at a glance.

🤩4-Step Ice Making

1️⃣Pour clean water into the water tank.No complicated setup — just add water and get ready to make fresh ice.

2️⃣Connect the power cord and turn the machine on.JOYMayLab is designed for simple home use, so you can start making ice anytime.

3️⃣Select Bullet Ice or Sphere Ice based on your drink.Use bullet ice for everyday refreshment, or sphere ice for whiskey, cocktails, and slow sipping.

4️⃣Fresh ice is ready to serve.Add it to your drink and enjoy the perfect chill at home.
