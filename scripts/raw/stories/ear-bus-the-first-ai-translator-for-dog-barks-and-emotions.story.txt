The A12 doesn’t just translate—it comforts. When it detects a specific emotion, it automatically plays music designed for that mood. Available on both the device and your phone.

BUILT ON REALDOG DATA

The A12’s AI translation model is trained on sound data from the 20 most common dog breeds worldwide, allowing it to understand how different dogs express themselves in various situations.

Continuously learning from real-world canine vocalizations, A12 evolves to better understand what your dog is feeling—from excitement and happiness to hunger, anxiety, and alertness—helping transform everyday barks into meaningful emotional insights and deeper companionship.

PS: The system prioritizes identifying emotional states and behavioral intentions rather than producing literal word-for-word translations.

Unlimited Translations No Subscription Required

Many pet translation apps charge per translation or require ongoing subscriptions.The A12 is different. Once you own the device, you get unlimited bark translations—no per-use fees and no subscriptions. Simply turn it on, and it’s ready to interpret your dog’s sounds anytime.

Communication with your dog should feel natural and free, without limits or extra costs.

High-Fidelity Audio Hardware

To capture your dog’s full vocal range—from 1,120 Hz to 20 kHz—the A12 is equipped with high-performance acoustic hardware. Under normal conditions, it can pick up barks from up to 10 meters away, ensuring even the subtlest emotional changes are never missed.
