MEET AMKOV GO1 Detachable Kids Print Camera

2-In-1 Detachable Design

A revolutionary 2-in-1 camera system that separates photography from printing—helping kids stay focused on creativity while reducing weight, waste, and distractions.

At just70g—lighter than an egg—the detachable camera is designed specifically for young creators. Lightweight enough for all-day adventures, it's comfortable to wear, easy for small hands to hold, and won't weigh kids down during play.

With less bulk and more freedom, children can move naturally, explore confidently, and stay focused on capturing the moments that matter.

When playtime is over, simply reconnect the camera to the printing dock.Kids can review their photos, pick their favorites, and print only the memories they truly want to keep.By separating shooting from printing, G01 reduces waste, saves paper, and lets children stay fully focused on capturing the moment.

The printing dock houses a1300mAh high-capacity battery. Once connected, it automatically charges the camera—one device, two functions. No extra power bank needed, just uninterrupted fun wherever you go.

Print Smarter, Save More

Traditional print cameras: shoot → print → waste.

With AMKOV G01, kids can shoot → select → print. By previewing photos before printing, families save paper, reduce unnecessary prints, and let children choose the photos they love. Kids learn to enjoy the process of creating and sharing, printing only the moments they want to keep.

Colorful DIY Prints

Using ink-free thermal printing, kids can print photos instantly without ink or toner.Choose from black, red, or blue thermal paper, then customize each print with drawings, notes, and colors. Every photo becomes a canvas for creativity and self-expression.

Built For Creative Focus

By separating shooting and printing, kids can fully immerse themselves in exploration.With 48MP photos, they can focus on interesting angles, exploring their surroundings, telling stories, and expressing creativity.

All-Day Battery

Designed for a full day of exploration.The camera features a 600mAh battery, and the printing dock holds 1300mAh.On a single charge, you can print up to500 photos, capturing parties, family moments, vacations, and everyday adventures without worrying about running out of power.

Fun Details Kids Will Love

G01 lets kids record1080p full HD videos, capturing movement, action, and everyday adventures. From playground slides to family games, every moment comes alive in vivid detail.

Theflip-up lenslets kids see themselves while framing shots, so they can take selfies and group photos without asking for help. Perfect for family moments and creative videos, it empowers children to create independently and build confidence.

The1.8-inch IPS screengives kids a clear, bright view of their photos and videos, making framing, reviewing, and selecting shots simple and fun.
