Risks and challenges
EVLA is a hardware product that has already reached the prototype stage and is currently undergoing final preparation for mass production.

While we have functional prototypes and established manufacturing support, there are still risks involved in bringing the product to large-scale production and global distribution.

These risks may include production adjustments, component availability, manufacturing scheduling, and logistics or shipping delays.

We are working closely with our manufacturing partners to reduce these risks as much as possible. Based on our current prototype validation and production preparation, we believe we are well positioned to deliver EVLA as planned.

We will continue to provide regular updates throughout the production and fulfillment process.

Portable Light for Indoor & Outdoor

In our busy, fragmented lives, what we truly long for is not more stuff—but better moments.

A gentle light that transforms ordinary evenings into memorable experiences, bringing warmth to gatherings, comfort to quiet nights, and atmosphere to every adventure.

EVLA was designed to light up the moments that matter—indoors or outdoors.

Whether you're camping outdoors, relaxing in the living room, reading in bed, enjoying coffee on the balcony, gathering with friends in the backyard, or adding a warm touch to your workspace,

EVLA is yourportable atmosphere maker—bringing light, comfort, and personality to every corner of your life.

Where Atmosphere Meets Everyday Living

Designed with a clean silhouette and carefully crafted lighting, EVLA blends naturally into any environment, creating an inviting atmosphere without overwhelming the space. Whether placed beside your bed, on a balcony table, in a cozy living room, or under the stars at a campsite, EVLA feels perfectly at home.

Outdoors, it elevates every adventure. Indoors, it enriches every moment.

Beneath its elegant design, we've integrated a thoughtful collection of features designed to elevate your everyday moments:

Dynamic ambient lighting, adjustable brightness, multiple lighting modes, portable cordless freedom, long-lasting battery performance, and intuitive controls—all working together seamlessly to create the perfect mood for any occasion.

Every feature is designed with balance and simplicity in mind.

EVLA doesn't demand attention—it quietly transforms the way a space feels.

Designed for Everyday Moments

1. Convenient Top Hook

Designed for effortless portability, the built-in hanging cord and carabiner let you place your light wherever you need it.

Hang it from a tent ceiling, tree branch, backpack, or bedside rack to instantly transform any space.

From the outdoors to everyday living, it brings warm ambient lighting wherever you go.

Hang it. Carry it. Enjoy it anywhere.

2. USB-C Rechargeable Design

Outdoor adventures shouldn't be interrupted by a lack of power.

EVLA features a built-in rechargeable battery and convenient USB-C charging, allowing you to recharge easily at home, in your vehicle, or with a portable power bank.

No disposable batteries. No unnecessary waste. Just reliable illumination whenever you need it.

From weekend camping trips and patio evenings to quiet moments by your bedside,it’s always ready for what’s next.

3. Multi-Mode Ambient Lighting

At the heart of EVLA is a collection of carefully designed lighting modes, created to match every moment.

Switch between a realistic flickering flame effect, warm ambient glow, colorful mood lighting, and practical white light to create the perfect atmosphere for any occasion.

Whether you're camping beneath the stars, relaxing before bed, or hosting an evening gathering,these versatile lighting modes transform ordinary spaces into memorable experiences.

Light that does more than illuminate — it creates atmosphere.

4. Built-In White Noise & Nature Sounds

EVLA features a selection of soothing ambient sounds, including gentle rainfall, forest ambience, ocean waves, soft wind, white noise, and relaxing melodies.
