What if your HDMI connection could become wireless — and one device could share content across multiple screens at the same time?

With LinkHub, screen sharing becomes simpler, cleaner, and more flexible.

LinkHub is a hardware-based wireless screen casting system designed to work without Wi-Fi. No apps, drivers, or complicated setup required — simply plug in and connect automatically.It delivers stable low-latency transmission, clear 4K visuals, and multi-screen wireless sharing, making collaboration easier for meetings, classrooms, exhibitions, and home entertainment.

Compatible with both USB-C and HDMI devices, LinkHub also supports up to 100W PD pass-through charging during screen casting, helping create a more efficient and streamlined wireless display experience.

LinkHub works without Wi-Fi, app downloads, or driver installation. Simply connect the transmitter to your computer and the receiver to the display for automatic screen casting. No more repeated setup, device searching, or interruptions caused by unstable network connections — making presentations and sharing easier to start.

Truly plug in, cast instantly, and enjoy a smoother, more convenient viewing experience anytime.

No more switching devices repeatedly or dealing with complicated cable setups.LinkHub supports multi-device collaboration, allowing one transmitter to connect with multiple receivers at the same time for synchronized multi-screen display.Simply press one button for quick pairing — no Wi-Fi, no app, and no complicated configuration required.

No cables, no splitters, and no need to connect multiple screens to a single computer.Whether for meetings, training sessions, exhibitions, or collaborative work, all screens can stay synchronized with smooth and stable visuals, helping communication and presentations flow more efficiently.

Say Goodbye to Lag and Blurry VisualsLinkHub uses an independent hardware-based wireless transmission architecture instead of relying on network streaming or software compression, helping reduce the latency, stuttering, and image quality loss often seen in traditional wireless screen casting.Supports up to 4K@30Hz high-definition output, delivering clear text edges, detailed graphics, smooth video playback, and synchronized audio performance.

Say goodbye to delayed screen response.With its dedicated hardware wireless architecture, LinkHub keeps end-to-end latency controlled at a low level for a smoother and more responsive experience. Whether moving a cursor, switching slides, or streaming 4K video, interactions feel more natural and fluid, helping preserve fine visual details and smooth motion.

Stable Wireless Casting, Automatically OptimizedLinkHub features dual-band 2.4GHz + 5GHz wireless transmission that automatically switches to the best signal for smoother and more stable casting.5GHz delivers lower latency and clearer streaming, while 2.4GHz improves coverage and connection stability in complex environments.

Even in meeting rooms, classrooms, or exhibitions, LinkHub helps reduce lag and disconnections, with wireless transmission distances up to 50m in open spaces.

Dual Transmitter Versions for More DevicesType-C Version with up to 100W PD charging, plus an HDMI version designed for wider device compatibility.One receiver, two transmitter options.Laptops, tablets, smartphones, gaming consoles, and more — simply plug in and start casting.

Supports Windows, macOS, Linux, and other major platforms.No drivers, no apps — connect and start casting automatically.Compatible with laptops, tablets, and smartphones for both mirror and extended display modes.
