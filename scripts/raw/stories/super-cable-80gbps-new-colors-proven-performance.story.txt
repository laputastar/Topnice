A proven cable, refreshed for modern setups

The previous Super Cable projects showed that a USB-C cable can be more than a backup accessory. It can move large files, connect high-resolution displays, high-power gear, and stand up to daily use.

This color edition keeps that performance-first design and makes it easier to match your desk, studio, travel kit, or Apple setup.

Built for high-speed workflows

Super Cable 80Gbps is made for high-speed workflows with modern USB-C setups.

It supports up to 80Gbps bi-directional bandwidth and up to 120Gbps bandwidth boost mode with compatible devices.

Move large files, work with external SSDs, connect docks, and keep your setup moving with one cable built for modern devices.

Built for AI-ready desks

From latest-generation Macs and high-performance laptops to external SSDs, docks, displays, and creator workstations, modern workflows are getting faster and more connected. Super Cable 80Gbps is built to keep up.

Modern workflows need faster connections

External SSDs, high-resolution displays, docks, and compact workstations all depend on stable high-speed bandwidth.

Super Cable 80Gbps supports up to 80Gbps, with bandwidth boost up to 120Gbps on compatible USB 80Gbps devices.

Real-world speed test

In a Blackmagic Disk Speed Test setup, Super Cable reached over6,300 MB/s writeand5,900 MB/s readwith compatible high-speed storage.

That is the kind of bandwidth modern external SSDs, video workflows, and AI-ready workstations can actually use.

Actual results depend on the connected SSD, host device, port, and test environment.

Internal micro-coaxial structure for stable high-speed performance

High-speed workflows demand more than a USB-C connector. Inside Super Cable 80Gbps, multiple internal micro-coaxial cables help protect high-speed data transmission from interference, supporting more stable performance for external SSDs, docks, displays, and modern USB-C workflows.

Many ordinary cables use simpler twisted-pair structures to reduce cost. Super Cable uses an internal micro-coaxial structure because 80Gbps-class performance needs cleaner signal paths, better shielding, and a cable built for demanding setups.

Display, storage, dock, and power in one cable

Super Cable 80Gbps supports modern workflows with DisplayPort 2.1 and PCIe 4.0 compatibility.

Use it with compatible displays, external SSDs, docks, and professional peripherals.

Supports high-resolution display setupsDual 8K@60Hz or triple 4K@144Hz on compatible devices, docks, and displays.

Up to 240W USB-C Power Delivery

Super Cable 80Gbps supports up to 240W USB-C Power Delivery with compatible chargers and devices, giving your setup extra charging headroom for laptops, tablets, phones, docks, and future USB-C power accessories.
