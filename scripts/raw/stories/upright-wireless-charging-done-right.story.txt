Risks and challenges
Bringing any consumer electronic to life comes with real challenges, from component sourcing to final assembly and global shipping. The biggest risk we see is changes to the timeline.

Upright needs to pass multiple certifications, including Qi2, plus regulatory testing like FCC, UL, CE, RoHS, etc. Certification is the longest single step in our entire process, and while we're pushing to move it forward as fast as possible, these timelines aren't fully in our control. If anything causes a delay, this is where it's most likely to come from.

We've completed multiple prototype iterations with multiple suppliers to lock in quality and consistency, and we've partnered with an experienced supply chain management team that conducted in-person audits of our manufacturer. We also have a backup manufacturer in place, so a single-vendor issue doesn't become a project-killing one.

Unexpected delays can happen in any hardware project. We're committed to keeping every backer informed throughout, until Upright is in your hands.

Environmental commitments

A 25W Qi2 wireless charging stand balancing heavy, weighted stability with a premium hardwood finish.Crafted to blend effortless, one-handed docking into your daily routine across your desk, nightstand, or living space.We reimagined the wireless charging stand — giving wireless power an elegant physical form that commands a space rather than cluttering it.

Upright was designed to fit in with the rest of your home. By re-imagining what a phone charger looks like, and using materials commonly found in architecture, it naturally feels right in any room you put it.

Upright is crafted from premium natural materials that are both beautiful and durable. Solid hardwood and hand-cast concrete, chosen for their weight, presence, and organic aesthetic.

The wood has a clear, matte finish that preserves its natural grain, with no two pieces looking exactly alike.

Six finishes across three wood tones and two concrete colorways means it works with any home aesthetic.

At up to 25W, Upright charges significantly faster than most wireless chargers, which typically top out at 7.5–15W.

Paired with a 30W charging adapter, a compatible device can go from 0 to 50% in about 30 minutes — fast enough for a reliable top-up before you head out.

Weighing in at over 600 grams, Upright is one of the heaviest stands in its category. The weighted base makes it easy to dock and undock with one hand, without having to hold down the charger, and prevents it from lifting off the table when you undock.

Thoughtful Details

Small details go a long way. The charging pad is soft-touch, giving it an elevated feel while protecting bare phones. The bottom of the base is lined with a soft bumper to protect the surface it sits on and reduce movement.
