Risks and challenges
As with any manufacturing and technology-driven project, there are several risks and challenges we would like to transparently share with our backers.
■ Production & Manufacturing
FujiCoolon™ is designed using specialized materials and structural engineering to achieve passive cooling without electricity.
Ensuring consistent performance across all units requires precise manufacturing control.
To mitigate this, we are working with experienced Japanese manufacturing partners and have already completed prototype validation and small-scale testing. We will continue quality checks throughout production to maintain performance standards.
■ Performance Expectations
Since FujiCoolon™ operates without fans or active cooling systems, its effectiveness depends on environmental conditions such as ambient temperature, airflow, and surface contact.
To address this, we are committed to providing clear usage guidelines and transparent test data, so backers can fully understand the product’s optimal use cases and realistic performance.
■ Shipping & Logistics
Global shipping may face delays due to customs procedures, international logistics disruptions, or unforeseen external factors.
We plan to ship directly from Japan using reliable international carriers and will provide tracking for all orders. We will also maintain regular communication and updates to keep backers informed throughout the process.
■ Timeline & Demand
If demand exceeds expectations, production and fulfillment timelines may be extended.
To prepare for this, we are planning scalable production and have built buffer time into our schedule. We will prioritize transparency and provide frequent updates in case of any changes.
■ Budget & Funding Risk
As this is an all-or-nothing campaign, production will only proceed if the funding goal is reached.
The funds raised will be allocated to manufacturing, quality control, packaging, and international shipping. We have carefully calculated costs to ensure project feasibility.
■ Audience & Market Risk
As a relatively new concept in passive cooling solutions, it may take time for a wider audience to fully understand the product’s value.
To overcome this, we will continue to improve our communication through visuals, demonstrations, and real-world use cases.
■ Our Commitment
We are committed to transparency, regular communication, and delivering a product that meets the expectations of our backers.
Learn about accountability on Kickstarter
