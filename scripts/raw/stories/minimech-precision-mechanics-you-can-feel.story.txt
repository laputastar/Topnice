MiniMechis a miniature cylinder designed to provide the tactile feel of real mechanics in a pocket-sized package.

Each press creates a vacuum inside the cylinder, creating a smooth resistance that you can feel.

Wear it on your keys, keep it on your desk, or simply enjoy the tactile sensation when you need a moment of focus.

Feel the resistance

Adjust the force

Carry it anywhere

MiniMech was inspired by the hydraulic cylinders that power the world's largest machines.

We transformed that industrial mechanism into a precision-machined pocket-sized object you can carry every day.

Inspired by real hydraulic cylinders, MiniMech transforms mechanical motion into a tactile experience.

Each movement feels precise, controlled, and surprisingly satisfying.

Fine-tune the experience: soft and fluid, or firm and defined. MiniMech adapts to your preference.

Simply pull the rod and rotate it to adjust the internal valve.

↻Clockwise— stronger vacuum, maximum resistance.

↺Counterclockwise— reduced vacuum, smoother movement.

Dial in the exact mechanical feel you prefer.

Pocket-sized. Always present. Never demanding attention. A small mechanical moment — whenever you need it.

The movement is what brings MiniMech to life.Every press delivers a smooth, controlled response that feels consistent from start to finish. Simple in design, yet endlessly satisfying to experience.
