No More Stress Over Open Windows or Unlocked Doors | Instant Real-Time Alerts & All-Round Smart Home Protection

Dual-Sensor Anti-Break-In Protection

Magnetic contact sensor + high-sensitivity vibration detector. It captures both opened doors and smashed window glass to block break-ins in advance.

Built-in 110dB Siren, Direct Wi-Fi Connection — No Hub Required

No extra gateway or receiver to buy. The loud local alarm triggers instantly to scare off intruders when threats are detected.

Pre-Assembled Complete Kit, Peel & Stick Instant Use

Magnet strip, CR123A battery and heavy-duty adhesive tape are all pre-installed. No assembly, no drilling, tool-free setup in seconds.

Multi-Scene Universal Security Coverage

Works perfectly for entry doors, windows, cabinets, drawers, safes and pet gates to secure every vulnerable spot around your house.
