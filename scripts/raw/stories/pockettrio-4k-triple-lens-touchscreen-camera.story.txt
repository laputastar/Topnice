MeetPocketTrio.

We designed this camera for you. It is the perfect bridge between Y2K vintage vibes and modern digital convenience. It captures your world effortlessly, keeping you entirely in the moment while delivering the aesthetic and speed you need.

Here is what makes it your perfect everyday companion:

Minimalist Y2K Retro Design

It features apure white, compact bodythat perfectly balances modern fashion with ultimate portability. More than just a burden-free daily camera, it acts as a vintage fashion accessory to complement your OOTD. Simply slip it into yourshirt pocketand you are ready to capture inspiration whenever it strikes.

Versatile Triple-Lens System

It brings your vision to life with a unique hardware combination: a4K main cameraalongsidedual 1080P lenses (wide-angle & front-facing). Whether you are capturing sweeping cityscapes, recording a daily vlog, or taking spontaneous selfies with friends, it provides true optical versatility for endless creative framing.

Smooth Smartphone-Like Touch

Equipped with anIncell fully laminated displayandfull-screen touch controls, it completely eliminates the glare and lag found in traditional entry-level cameras. The intuitive swipe and tap interactions offer azero-learning-curveexperience, feeling just as responsive and familiar as the smartphone you use every day.

Ready-to-Post Aesthetic Filters

Skip the complex color grading. It features16 creative color modes,8 preset scenes, andbasic facial beautification. Get perfect cinematic and Lo-Fi vintage tones instantly with trending Gen-Z filters like"Japanese Style", "Dopamine", "Cream", "Soft CCD", "Cold White CCD",and"Gothic". Every shot is a ready-to-post masterpiece right out of the camera.

5G WiFi Instant Sharing

Forget the hassle of tangled cables and computer transfers. With a built-inhigh-speed 5G WiFi moduleand adedicated App, your vlogs and photos sync to your phone in a matter of seconds. You are always just one tap away from posting your life directly to TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat.

Social-Media Ready Quality

Beyond its buttery smooth1080P/60fps baseline recording, it effortlessly outputs stunning64MP photosand5K/4K video resolution. Complete with16x zoomand full-screen adjustments forISO, exposure, and white balance, it ensures your content always meets the high-definition standards of any major short-video platform.
