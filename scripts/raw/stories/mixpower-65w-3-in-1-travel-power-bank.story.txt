A 15,000mAh travel power bank, wall charger, and earbud storage case with USB-C fast charging, a built-in cable, and swappable plugs.

Mixpower is a compact 3-in-1 travel power hub from MOXSEMI, built for people who want fewer chargers, fewer loose cables, and a cleaner everyday carry setup.

WHY WE ARE BUILDING IT

Most daily and travel charging setups turn into a pile of small accessories: a wall charger, a backup battery, a charging cable, adapters, and small-device charging gear. Mixpower is our attempt to make that setup simpler without pretending that one product can replace every specialized device.

WHAT MIXPOWER DOES

Mixpower combines AC wall input, backup power, USB-C output, a built-in USB-C cable output, USB-A output, and low-current earbuds support in one compact travel device. It is designed for workdays, commuting, short trips, hotel rooms, and everyday charging moments when you do not want to carry a separate charger, cable, and power bank.

CONFIRMED SPECIFICATIONS

AC Input: 100-240V~ 50/60Hz 1.5AUSB-C Input: 45W MaxUSB-C Output: 65W MaxUSB-C Cable Output: 27W MaxUSB-A Output: 12W MaxEarbuds: 200mA MaxNet Weight: 405g

EVERYDAY BLUETOOTH EARBUDS

Mixpower is not only about charging larger devices. It also includes everyday Bluetooth earbuds that store and charge inside the main device, so you do not need to carry a separate earbuds case for quick calls, music, or backup listening.

To pair, take the earbuds out of the integrated compartment and select "CV101" from your phone's Bluetooth menu. The earbuds support HSP, HFP, A2DP, AVRCP, SBC, and AAC, with up to 10m Class 2 transmission range.

Basic earbuds details:Bluetooth pairing name: CV101Call time: approx. 3 hoursMusic time: approx. 4-5 hours depending on volumeEarbuds battery: 3.7V 28mAh x 2Charging from Mixpower: 200mA max

The earbuds are designed as a convenient everyday audio companion inside the Mixpower system. They are not positioned as premium HiFi or ANC earbuds.
