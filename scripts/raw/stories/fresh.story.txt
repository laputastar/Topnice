RISKS AND CHALLENGES

Every physical product project comes with risks, and we believe in being transparent about them from the start.

Fresh+ is a product that involves formulation, packaging, manufacturing, and global distribution. While the development process is already underway, there are still areas that require careful execution before large-scale fulfillment can begin.

One of the key challenges is production scaling. Moving from small-scale samples to full production requires coordination with manufacturing partners to ensure consistency, quality, and timing. Delays can sometimes occur during this stage due to ingredient sourcing, packaging refinement, or production scheduling.

Another important factor is shipping and logistics. As with many physical products, international fulfillment timelines can vary depending on location, customs processing, and distribution partners. We are actively working to plan fulfillment in a way that minimizes delays and provides realistic delivery expectations.

There is also the possibility of formulation refinement during final production stages. While the core concept and ingredient structure are established, minor adjustments may be made to improve stability, taste, or usability based on final testing and manufacturing feedback.

We are committed to keeping backers informed throughout the entire process. Regular updates will be provided so supporters can follow the development journey from production to delivery.

Our goal is to be transparent, realistic, and responsible in how we manage this project from start to finish.

Oral Wellness Hydration Mix

Oral Wellness Hydration Mix

Most of us carry gum, mints, or mouthwash because we want to feel fresh and confident around other people. But those solutions are often temporary and easy to forget.

I wanted a simple daily ritual that could become part of my morning routine something as easy as mixing a packet into water and starting the day feeling refreshed, hydrated, and ready for whatever comes next.

That's why I created fresh+.

fresh+ is a daily oral wellness hydration mix designed to support freshness, confidence, and hydration as part of a simple everyday habit. It fits alongside brushing and flossing, not in place of them.

Whether you're heading into a meeting, a date, a workout, or just another busy day, fresh+ is designed to help you show up feeling your best.

After months of formulation, testing, and refinement, we're ready to bring fresh+ to life.

By backing this project, you're helping us move from development into production and become one of the very first members of the AURA community.

fresh+ is a powdered hydration drink mix that is added to water and consumed once per day. It is designed for simplicity and consistency, allowing it to easily fit into existing routines without disruption.

Most users choose to take it in the morning as part of their daily preparation or before important social or professional moments. The format is intentionally minimal so that it can be used anywhere, whether at home, at work, or while traveling.

The focus is not on complexity, but on making it easy for people to build a daily ritual that supports how they feel in their day to day interactions.

Ingredients and Formulation

fresh+ is made with ingredients commonly used in hydration and wellness-focused products.

Commonly used in oral care products and sugar-free gums, xylitol is widely associated with freshness-focused routines and a cleaner-feeling mouth

Nano-Hydroxyapatite

A mineral naturally found in tooth enamel and commonly used in modern oral care products designed to support a clean, polished feeling.

Frequently included in freshness-focused formulations and known for its role in helping maintain a fresh taste experience.

Oral Probiotic Blend

Included as part of a balanced oral wellness approach and designed to complement a daily freshness routine.

Using fresh+ is straightforward. Mix one pouch with 8 oz of water. It can be taken in the morning as part of a daily routine or at any point in the day when someone is preparing for social or professional interactions.

Because of its simple format, it requires no additional steps or preparation. It is designed to be consistent, portable, and easy to maintain as part of everyday life.

Intended Use and Audience

fresh+ is intended for anyone looking to feel of fresh and confident in everyday interactions. It is suitable for individuals who value structured habits and prefer minimal, easy-to-follow wellness practices.

It is not intended as a replacement for oral hygiene practices or professional care. Instead, it is designed to complement existing routines as a lifestyle-oriented hydration product.
