Environmental commitments

Every cyclist knows their power. Few know where that power goes.

The reality is simple:most of the energy you produce never becomes speed. It disappears into aerodynamic drag, rolling resistance, and drivetrain losses. For decades, understanding those losses required access to wind tunnels, laboratories, and expensive testing protocols.

For professional teams, this data became a competitive advantage. For everyone else, it remained inaccessible.Until now.

THE WASTED WATTS TRACKER

Power meters tell you how hard you push the pedals.Wasted Watts tells you how efficiently those watts become speed.

For the first time, cyclists can measure aerodynamic drag, rolling resistance, equipment performance, and riding efficiency directly on the roads they ride every day.

No wind tunnel. No laboratory. No assumptions.Just real-time accurate data, collected in real conditions.

Cycling performance is often reduced to a single metric:pedalingpower.

Butpushing more wattsdoes not automatically win races, set personal records, or improve efficiency. It’s only half the battle. If that power is lost to aerodynamic drag and rolling resistance - the wasted watts - you are just burning matches to fight the wind. True speed can therefore only come through reducing these losses.

Our mission is to make the invisible visible and make performance optimization accessible to every cyclist by revealing the hidden factors that determine speed. By transforming complex aerodynamic analysisinto a practical tool with outstanding precisionand reactivity, we aim to help riders make smarter decisions, train more effectively, and unlock performance gains that were previously reserved for elite teams.

Because the fastest rider is not always the strongest rider. Often, it’s the most efficient one.

PROFESSIONAL MEASUREMENT TECHNOLOGY

The Wasted Watts Tracker was designed as a professional-grade measurement system capable of delivering highly accurate results outside laboratory environments.

Core technologies include:

Centimeter-accurate GPS RTK positioning

Real-time aerodynamic measurement

Synchronized power meter integration

High-precision speed detection

Automatic calibration systems

The platform achieves measurement precision as high as 0.5% over 60 seconds,making it the most advanced aerodynamic field testing system ever made available to cyclists.

Until now, serious aerodynamic testing required dedicated facilities, specialized operators, and thousands of dollars in testing fees.

A typical wind tunnel session costs between $1,500 and $2,500.

And once the session ends, the data stops.

Wasted Watts brings that level of analysis directly onto the road.

Test your position.

Test your equipment.

Test your strategy.

Anytime you ride.

Precision is only valuable if it can be trusted. Wasted Watts has been rigorously tested against known aerodynamic variations,demonstrating its ability to detect even the smallest performance differences.

In this example, the system accurately measures incremental changes in drag with a standard error of just 0.13%, equivalent to approximately 0.32 watts at 45 km/h. The result is a level of accuracy that allows cyclists to confidently evaluate position changes, equipment choices, and aerodynamic optimizations directly on the road.

HOW THE WASTED WATTS TRACKER COMPARES
