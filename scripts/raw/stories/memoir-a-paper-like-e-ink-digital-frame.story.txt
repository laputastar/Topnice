Risks and challenges
We're incredibly excited to bring Memoir to Kickstarter, and we're confident in our ability to deliver.

Unlike many crowdfunding projects, Memoir is not just a concept or early-stage prototype. The core hardware, firmware, software, cloud infrastructure, and mobile application have already been developed and validated through multiple prototype iterations. Our focus is now on scaling production and delivering a polished product to our backers.

That said, manufacturing hardware always comes with some level of risk.

The most likely challenge we may encounter is related to component procurement, supplier lead times, or quality control during production. Memoir relies on premium display modules and carefully selected electronic components, and while we've established relationships with experienced manufacturing partners and suppliers, occasional delays can occur due to component availability, production scheduling, or quality assurance requirements.

If any issue arises, our priority will always be quality over speed. Rather than shipping a product that doesn't meet our standards, we'll take the necessary time to resolve the issue and ensure every Memoir frame delivers the experience we've promised.

The good news is that the most challenging part of the journey-product development-is already behind us. With a working product, validated prototypes, established suppliers, and an experienced engineering team, we believe the remaining risks are manageable and primarily related to manufacturing timelines rather than product feasibility.

Our commitment is simple: transparent communication, regular updates, and a relentless focus on quality.

No shortcuts. No surprises. Just Memoir, made right.

Memoir was founded byAshwin RadhakrishnaandMohan Kashivasi, who together bring more than four decades of experience building embedded systems, connected devices, and consumer technology products. Backed by a multidisciplinary team spanning hardware engineering, firmware, software, mechanical design, manufacturing, and product experience, Memoir is being developed end-to-end by the people building it.

The team brings expertise from leading technology companies includingQualcomm,Broadcom,Freescale, andSTMicroelectronics, and has collectively contributed to the development and shipment of millions of connected devices used around the world. This experience helps ensure that Memoir is not only thoughtfully designed, but also engineered, tested, and prepared for reliable production at scale.

Over the past year, we've transformed Memoir from an idea into a fully functional product through multiple prototype iterations, extensive testing, and continuous refinement across hardware, software, and user experience.

Memoir represents thousands of engineering hours, countless design iterations, and a relentless focus on creating a display experience that feels more natural, beautiful, and thoughtfully integrated into everyday living. Today, we're excited to bring Memoir to life with the support of the Kickstarter community.
