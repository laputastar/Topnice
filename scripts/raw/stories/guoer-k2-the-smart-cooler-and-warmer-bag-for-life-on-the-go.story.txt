How to Back GUOER K2

MeetGUOER K2, a smart temperature-control bag designed to keep your meals, drinks, and daily essentials ready wherever life takes you.

With cooling and warming modes, a two-layer storage design, USB and car power compatibility, and a stylish handbag-inspired look, GUOER K2 brings everyday temperature control into a compact, portable form.

USE IT IN MORE PLACES

GUOER K2 is designed for flexible power options, so you can use it in more places. Use It in the Car, at Home, at Work, or Outdoors.

FOOD NEVER STAYS JUST RIGHT

Life moves fast. Drinks warm up, lunch cools down, and snacks lose freshness when you’re away from home. GUOER K2 helps keep meals and drinks closer to the temperature you want.

WHY GUOER K2 STANDS OUT

Traditional insulated bags only slow down temperature change. Mini fridges are too bulky for everyday carry. And most lunch bags simply cannot actively cool or warm your food.

COOL OR WARM,ONE BAG

Use Cooling Mode for drinks, fruit, desserts, sushi, and chilled snacks. Switch to Warming Mode for lunch, coffee, tea, soup, and warm meals.

Cold when you need it. Warm when you want it. Ready when life happens.

SMART THERMOELECTRIC COOLING & WARMING

Powered by an advanced Peltier thermoelectric core, GUOER K2 moves heat away from the storage chamber to help keep items cool.

A bottom air intake and side heat exhaust move heat out, while the insulated inner compartment stays separated from the airflow path.

Reverse the current, and the same system provides gentle warming — all without a compressor or refrigerant.

STILL COLD AFTER 10 HOURS

Even after 10 hours, GUOER K2 still shows clear cold-retention performance. Tested at around 75°F / 24°C, our ice comparison and cooling data help demonstrate how well it keeps contents cold over time.

COMPACTOUTSIDE, SPACIOUS INSIDE

GUOER K2 features a practical two-layer design for everyday carry — an insulated lower compartment for meals and drinks, plus a dry upper compartment for daily essentials.

Lower Layer: 6L Temperature-Control Zone

Keep meals, drinks, fruit, snacks, and small food containers neatly packed in one easy-access compartment.

Upper Layer: 2L Dry Storage Zone

Keep your phone, wallet, keys, tissues, and personal items separate from food and moisture.

No more carrying a lunch bag, tote, and cooler separately — K2 combines daily storage and meal carry in one compact system.
