Compact Yet Powerful

Voltbou Power is a compact yet powerful 240W desktop-grade power bank built for creators, travelers, and power users. Featuring dual USB-C fast charging with up to 140W + 100W output, it can charge two laptops simultaneously while powering phones, tablets, cameras, drones, and more. Equipped with an intelligent LED display, advanced 4S2P battery architecture, and 24000mAh / 88.8Wh airline-safe capacity, it delivers real-time power monitoring and reliable high-performance charging wherever you go.

Intelligent LED display

Intelligent LED Display gives you complete real-time visibility of your charging system. Instantly monitor power output, voltage, current, battery level, temperature, and charging status across every port, allowing smarter power management and a more professional desktop-level charging experience wherever you are.

Triple-Port Max240W Output

With a combined maximum output of 240W across all three ports, Voltbou Power delivers true desktop-level charging performance in a portable form. Power laptops, tablets, smartphones, gaming devices, and accessories simultaneously without sacrificing charging speed, making it the perfect all-in-one power solution for work, travel, and everyday carry.

Dual Laptop Fast Charging

Equipped with powerful 140W and 100W USB-C outputs, Voltbou Power supports full-speed charging for two laptops simultaneously, delivering stable desktop-level performance for creators, professionals, and multi-device workflows anywhere.

Advanced 4S2P High-Performance Battery Pack

Built with an advanced 4S2P battery architecture using 8 high-efficiency 18650 cells, Voltbou Power delivers stable high-output performance, improved thermal efficiency, and reliable long-duration power for demanding multi-device charging environments.

PD100W Ultra-Fast Input

Voltbou Power supports up to 100W PD fast input charging, allowing the power bank itself to recharge significantly faster than traditional portable batteries. With high-speed USB-C input, you can spend less time waiting and more time powering your devices, making it ideal for creators, travelers, and professionals who are constantly on the move.

Wide Device Compatibility

Voltbou Power is designed to work seamlessly across a wide range of modern devices, including laptops, smartphones, tablets, cameras, drones, gaming handhelds, wireless accessories, and more. With advanced USB-C fast charging support and intelligent power distribution, it automatically adapts to different devices and charging requirements, delivering stable, efficient, and reliable power wherever you need it.

Compact Yet Powerful

Despite its powerful 240W desktop-level output, Voltbou maintains a compact and portable form factor designed for everyday carry. Measuring just 172mm × 59mm × 52mm, it easily fits into backpacks, camera bags, and mobile workstations while delivering enough power to charge laptops, creator gear, gaming devices, and multiple high-performance devices wherever you go.
