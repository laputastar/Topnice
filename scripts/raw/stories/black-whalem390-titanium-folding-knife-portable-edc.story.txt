Design Inspiration

We are proud to introduce our high-end folding knife, crafted from titanium and M390 super steel. This knife is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts, hunters, and collectors, and we have named it the "Black Whale." We were seeking durable, high-quality blades capable of withstanding even the harshest conditions. Our knife is designed to deliver the perfect balance of form, function, and style.

From Sketch to Reality: The Design Behind Rambo

Sketches can be breathtaking, but the real test lies in transforming inspiration into a tangible creation that fits perfectly in your hand. To faithfully honor the original design, we combine CNC precision machining with exquisite hand finishing. Every knife is a 1:1 replica of the design drawing—flawless the moment you hold it.

design and processing

M390 steel blade | Sharp, reliable, ready to use

The blade is made of M390 stainless steel, delivering sharp performance with excellent edge retention and corrosion resistance. Rated at 60–62 HRC, it is tough enough for wood, rope, or food preparation, and easy to maintain.

Black Whale details

M390 steel blade— high wear resistance, very good corrosion resistance, and excellent edge retention, easy to maintain.

Titanium handle — lightweight, durable, and built to last — with a smooth texture that makes it extremely comfortable to grip.Equipped with a titanium frame lock, known for its strength, simplicity, and reliability. By integrating the lock into the handle itself, it provides a secure lockup, fewer moving parts, and outstanding durability.

The custom pocket clip blends seamlessly with the knife's contour. It offers secure, discreet carry while enhancing the knife's sleek and balanced profile.

Carrying method

In the outdoors, reliability is everything. The Black Whale handles rope cutting, food preparation, and shelter building with ease — a compact tool that punches above its weight when you need it most.
