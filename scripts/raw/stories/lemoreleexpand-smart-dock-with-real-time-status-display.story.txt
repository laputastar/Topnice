Risks and challenges
As with any hardware product, bringing Lemorelee from development to large-scale production involves several challenges, including manufacturing coordination, component sourcing, firmware optimization, compatibility testing, and logistics management. Because this product supports multiple operating systems, display protocols, and connected device types, extensive testing is required to ensure stable performance across a wide range of usage scenarios.

To minimize these risks, our team has already completed core product development, structural design, and functional validation before launching this campaign. We are also working closely with experienced manufacturing and supply chain partners to maintain consistent production quality and delivery timelines. While unexpected delays related to supply chains, international logistics, or component availability may still occur, we are committed to maintaining transparent communication with our backers throughout the entire process. Our priority is to deliver a reliable, high-quality product that meets both our standards and the expectations of our supporters.

❤️ About Lemoreleexpand

✨ Your Workspace Shouldn't Be A Mystery

Modern workspaces depend on displays, storage devices, networks, chargers, and peripherals working together seamlessly. Yet most docking stations give you almost no visibility into what's actually happening behind those connections.

You shouldn't have to open system settings to check a monitor's refresh rate. You shouldn't need diagnostic tools to verify network speed. And you shouldn't have to guess whether your devices are performing at their full potential.

Lemoreleexpandchanges that.

Bycombining intelligent monitoring,professional display expansion, andcentralized controlinto a single desktop hub, ithelps you see more,do more, andstay in controlof your entire workspace.

👀 See Your Workspace In Real Time

Most docks connect devices but provide little visibility into what's actually happening across your workspace.

Lemoreleexpand's built-in Workspace Dashboarddisplaysmonitor status,connection performance,network status,storage activity, andcharging informationat a glance.

Instead of opening software or digging through system settings, the information you need is always right in front of you.

🖥️See Display Status Instantly

Monitor resolution,refresh rate, andconnection statusare displayed directly on the dock, making it easy to verify your setup at a glance.

Whether you're using a single display or a dual-screen workstation,critical display information remains visibleat all times.

⚡Verify Performance At A Glance

Performance indicatorsinstantly show whether USB devices, memory cards, and Ethernet connections are operatingat their expected speeds.

From USB 3.2 transfers to Gigabit networking,bottlenecks become easier to identifybefore they impact your workflow.

🔋Monitor Power In Real Time

Charging activityandpower information are displayed directlyon the dashboard, helping you verify that connected devices are receiving the power they need.

No hidden menus. No uncertainty. Just instant visibility.

🪟 Give Every Task More Room

Switching between windows wastes time and breaks focus.Lemoreleexpandhelps createa larger,more organized workspaceby supporting multiple displaysthrough a single USB-C connection.

More screen space meansless clutter,smoother multitasking, and amore productive workflow.

🔌Connect The Displays You Already Own

Built-inHDMIandDisplayPortconnectivity make it easy to work with modern monitors, projectors, and professional displayswithout additional adapters.

Use the screens you already own and expand your workspace however you choose.

✨See More. Do More.

Supportingup to dual 4K displays at 60Hz,Lemoreleexpanddelivers the visual clarity and screen space needed for demanding workflows.

More content stays visible, helping you work faster and stay organized.

🎯A Workspace That Adapts To You

From mirrored displays to independent multi-screen configurations, flexible display modes allow your workspace to match the way you work.

Whether you're presenting, creating, or multitasking, your setup remains adaptable and efficient.
