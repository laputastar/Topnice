Risks and challenges
Every crowdfunding campaign comes with its own set of hurdles, and bringing a heavy-duty, premium product like SIKRE to life is no small feat. However, our successful beta run in late 2025 has prepared us fully for what's ahead. Our background, meticulous planning, and local manufacturing setup have positioned us to mitigate risks effectively, while bringing new and improved materials to the roof box market.

Here is how we are preparing for the road ahead:

1. Production & Manufacturing
Manufacturing requires precision, and we now have our tooling and parts in place. It's now simply time to activate. To keep quality tight and oversight simple, we chose to build SIKRE right here in Utah. By keeping production local, we eliminate added supplier delays, and potential long feedback loops. We can be physically present at the facility to troubleshoot any production issues in real time.

2. Supply Chain & Material Sourcing
SIKRE relies on premium materials—specifically NEW reinforced ABS shells with a rugged finish and high-grade stainless-steel hardware. We have swapped t-track on early models for new and improved L-Track on interior walls. We have established relationships with reputable suppliers. We are launching this campaign having already blasted through the prototype phase. Funding from this campaign will allow us to focus on added accessory development and the ability to place bulk material orders immediately.

3. Shipping & Logistics
Shipping large, rugged items like roof boxes can be complex and expensive. However, we already have locked in shipping accounts, a packaging partner, and custom pallets so we are ready to roll.

Our Promise to You
As a veteran-owned, family-operated business, our word is everything. We are committed to absolute transparency. If life throws us a curveball—whether it's a minor production hiccup or a shipping delay—you will be the first to know. We will provide regular, honest updates throughout the entire post-campaign journey until your SIKRE box is securely mounted on your roof.

Thank you for trusting us and helping us bring SIKRE to life!

Our Family at the Core

We're Dan and Alicia Strieby, the heart behind SIKRE, a family-operated, veteran-owned business, proudly shared with our children, Colter and Leona. Grounded in our faith and a love for the outdoors, we're dedicated to building something meaningful; rugged and reliable storage for fellow adventurers who crave the open road.

How SIKRE Began

A few years ago, a layoff scare ignited a desire in us to create something resilient, something we could help push the boundaries with. What began as a sketch on an envelope has evolved into the robust storage solution you see today. It was a wake-up call to create something of our own—something that could stand strong if life ever threw another curveball.

Why it Matters to us

We have lived across the Rockies, even a memorable (and very brief!) attempt at camper living with Colter when he was 9 months old. Turns out sleep training in a Jayco didn't pan out. Nonetheless, our love for the wilderness only deepened. Now, we’re thrilled to be back in Utah, where our roof boxes are proudly manufactured. This reflects our commitment to quality American craftsmanship. Having experienced countless miles and the need for dependable storage firsthand, we envisioned a roof box that could truly keep up—a tougher, more robust design that wouldn't compromise your vehicle's aesthetic or delete your roof availability.

The Meaning of SIKRE

In Norwegian, SIKRE means "to secure." Our goal is simple: to craft a product that allows people to load up and hit the road with confidence that their gear is secure. Whether it's a weekend of skiing, a hunting trip, or a cross-country trek, we want you to focus on the moments that matter—without worrying about your gear. Welcome to the Sikre family. Let’s hit the road together.
