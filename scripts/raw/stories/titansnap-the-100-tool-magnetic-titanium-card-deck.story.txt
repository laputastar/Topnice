Specs & Materials

Why TitanSnap Isn’t Just Another Tool Card

Most tool cards try to do a little bit of everything — usually on one single plate, with limited functions, basic materials, and no clear way to organize them by real-life use.

TitanSnap changes that with up to100 useful toolsacross five magneticTC4 titaniummission cards, each built around a real use case: daily fixes, outdoor tasks, tech gear, rescue backup, and fishing.

We built TitanSnap to give tool cards a real mission: more utility, premium materials, smarter carry, and a deck you can actually configure.

100 Tools. 5 Mission Cards

TitanSnap turns five magnetic titanium cards into a pocket-ready deck with up to100 useful toolsfor daily fixes, tech carry, outdoor tasks, rescue backup, and fishing.

Open, cut, measure, tighten, scrape, store, signal, repair, prep, and adjust— all in one slim deck built to move with your gear. Carry one for focused utility. Complete the deck for serious backup.

Magnetic Snap Stack

Built-in magnets pull the cards into one clean, compact stack. Grab the deck, slide out the card you need, then snap it back when you’re done.

Fast access. Clean storage. Satisfying every time.

Five Mission Cards for Real-World Problems

Instead of random functions on one crowded plate, TitanSnap gives every card a purpose:T1 Daily, T2 Outdoor, T3 Fishing, T4 Tech, and T5 Rescue.

Each card handles a different kind of problem, so your tool deck feels organized, focused, and ready for real life.

T1 Daily Card — Your Everyday Problem Solver

For the small problems that show up everywhere — opening packages, tightening screws, measuring, scraping, cutting cord, and handling quick fixes around your desk, bag, bike, or home.

T2 Outdoor Card — Camp Tasks, Flattened

Camp-ready functions in a flat titanium form — open cans, cut cord, scrape, sharpen, prep food, adjust gear, and handle small outdoor tasks without carrying a full tool roll.

T3 Fishing Card — Small Tools for the Water

A slim titanium card for line cutting, hook correction, fish scaling, bait adjustment, and tackle-side fixes — without adding another bulky tool to your kit.

T4 Tech Card — Built for the Gear You Carry

A slim backup for your digital life — with tools for SIM cards, TF cards, cable wrapping, phone support, screwdriving, and quick tech-side fixes.

T5 Rescue Card — Backup for Urgent Moments

A compact emergency backup card for cutting bandages, signaling, measuring, opening, and handling unexpected situations with more confidence.

Carry One. Stack a Few. Complete the Deck

TitanSnap is built to fit the way you carry: take one mission card for a light daily setup, stack two or three for your favorite loadout, or complete the full deck for up to100 toolsacross every mission.

Titanium Utility, Built Flat

TitanSnap is built fromTC4 titanium— strong, lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and made to feel premium in your everyday carry.

Unlike simple stamped metal cards, each TitanSnap card is carefullyCNC-machined from titanium, with precise cutouts, drivers, slots, edges, and measurement details built directly into the metal.

Every hole has a purpose. Every edge has a job. Every detail is cut with precision.

Glow Markers for Low-Light Carry

Built-in glow markers absorb light and give TitanSnap a subtle glow in the dark —making the deck easier to spot in a pouch, tent, car, drawer, or low-light setup.

It’s a small detail with real carry value: easier to find, cooler to look at, and ready when the lights drop.
