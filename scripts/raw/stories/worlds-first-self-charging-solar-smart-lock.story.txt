What Makes V150 Plus Different

V150 Plus is designed to reduce charging, battery swaps, and low-power surprises by harvesting everyday doorway light and managing energy automatically.1) The first brand to apply anindustry-leading perovskite solar panelto a consumer smart lock.2) It is approximately 6 times more efficient than traditional silicon solar panels.(*Data sourced from the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) U.S. state sunshine database and DESLOC lab calculations.)

Perovskite solar panel can start charging inremarkably low-light(300 lux)whilst traditional solar panels require sunlight, preferably direct rather than diffused, the low-light is exactly the kind of light your shaded front porch gets or rainy/cloudy day gets  or the electric lights— it produces enough energy to power the lock all day.*Battery life may vary depending on usage conditions.*Data sourced from the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) U.S. state sunshine database and DESLOC lab calculations.

Charging From Day to Night, Sunny Days to Cloudy/Rainy Days

From sunrise to sunset, V150 Plus harvests energy across all daylight intensities—from bright sun to overcast skies. It keeps charging even as light fades, so your door is always powered.No matter direct or indirect sun light, V150 Plus adapts to real-world weather: sunny, cloudy, rainy, even indoor-like low light (≥300 lux). No matter the forecast, it keeps generating power reliably.

Perfect Match for Your Porches

Your front porches  maybe rarely get direct sun—but never mind. V150 Plus's perovskite solar panel turns that everyday, low-angle light into steady, battery‑free energy, runs continuously in typical covered entryways, with zero maintenance.

We built a proprietary light-sensing algorithm,BMS (Battery Management System),  that constantly monitors ambient light and battery status, dynamically allocating power for optimal performance at all times. The lock doesn't just collect energy — it intelligently manages every watt.

A removable10,000mAh lithium batterystores harvested energy that lasts up to 8 months, providing continuous power even through cloudy stretches or at night. In the unlikely event you ever need it, there's also a Type-C emergency power port.

*8 months is based on the usage of20 times a day, battery life may be vary by using conditions.

The DESLOC app gives you a clear view of battery level, charging status, and energy performance in one place, so you always know how your lock is powered. Withreal-time visibilityinto the system, power becomes something you can understand — not something you have to worry about.

DESLOC V150 Plus uses solar self-charging to reduce the need for frequent battery replacement, helpinglower everyday maintenanceand cut down on disposable battery waste over time.

Calculated from U.S. average sunlight, real‑home placement (orientation & shading), and 500mAh per AA battery capacity – the V150 Plussaves 146 batteriesper year with solar self‑charging.
