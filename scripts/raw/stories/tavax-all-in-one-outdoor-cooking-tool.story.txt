Risks and challenges
We are working with an experienced manufacturing partner and have completed multiple rounds of prototyping and testing to ensure TAVAX are ready for production. The design has been finalized using proven materials and manufacturing processes, which helps minimize production risks and delays.
Tavax's components have been fully costed, and we have ensured that the sale price will adequately cover all costs with a significant safety margin.
The main challenge we could face is shipping delays, To prepare, we’ve built extra time into our schedule and planned ahead for larger-scale production—just in case.
The product in our photos and videos is a working prototype. We may make small design tweaks or size adjustments during mass production to ensure the best possible product for everyone.
Thank you for your trust and support. Our goal: deliver a reliable, high-quality tool as promised.

PORTABLE CAMPING GRILL

For wilderness survival enthusiasts, backpackers, RV explorers, and weekend campers, the soul of a multi-person camping trip is always a satisfying wilderness cooking experience. It shouldn't be hampered bybulky kitchen utensils.

We've redefined wilderness cooking with our pure titanium baking pans—lighter, cleaner, and more durable.

A 0.6CM GEOMETRIC MARVELE

Traditional grills are space killers in backpacks, but TAVAX uses an innovative modular design, composed of four mainboards. When disassembled and folded, it's only 0.6 centimeters thick. It can slide into your backpack like a magazine, allowing you to travel light on every trip.

FLUID-MECHANICAL WINDPROOF SHIELDE

Outdoor weather is unpredictable, and this baking pan's side wind deflectors are a secret weapon against strong winds. A balance is achieved between "powerful external turbulence" and "maintaining internal micro-convection," ensuring food is heated efficiently and evenly.

PURE TITANIUM PLATEC+3O4 STAINLESS STEEL STAND

Dial: Pure Titanium

Extreme Fire Resistance:Possesses an extremely high melting point.handles direct flames up to 800°C / 1470°F.

Absolute Safety:Pure titanium is non-toxic and has natural antibacterial properties, making it the safest option.

Tough and Durable:Sufficiently robust and durable for long-term use.

Never Backs Up:Possesses extremely strong corrosion resistance; will not deform, crack, or rust.

Support: 304 Stainless Steel

Provides strong support for the overall structure, ensuring rock-solid stability in any environment.

THREE DISTINCT COOKING ZONES

Grill. Sear. Boil. All at once.

Small-grill zone:Fine mesh, perfect for sliced vegetables.

Large-grill zone:Classic grate pattern, searing the perfect "tomahawk" char marks.

Griddle&Boil zone:Solid flat surface, ideal for frying eggs or heating coffee.

NATURALLYEANTIBACTERIAL

Pure titanium's superior natural antibacterial properties prevent the baking pan from easily absorbing grease and residual odors, ensuring hygiene and safety in every outdoor kitchen session. From spring to winter, it's suitable for all seasons, allowing you to enjoy a high-quality barbecue feast anytime.

REDEFINING THE MOBILE KITCHEN

You can easily slip it into a backpack, or carry it by the webbing like a briefcase. As night falls, in the warmth of the fire, you will finally rediscover that long-lost freedom, that raw connection with nature.

We are a group of designers who love life and are passionate about hardcore outdoor exploration. At the intersection of cycling and camping, we found a new source of inspiration: infusing the classic aerodynamic lines and rugged contours of motorcycle helmets into the design of this griddle.

This is not merely camping gear; it is a groundbreaking fusion of industrial aesthetics. Through the precise deconstruction of geometric lines, we’ve imbued cold metal with mechanical beauty while ensuring ultimate portability and practicality. We hope this original design—born of the wild—will completely revolutionize the modern camping experience.
