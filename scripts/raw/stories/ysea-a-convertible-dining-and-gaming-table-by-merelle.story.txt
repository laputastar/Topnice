[RISKS & CHALLENGES]
Risks and challenges
As with any manufacturing project, bringing Ysea to life comes with challenges. We believe transparency is essential to build trust and set clear expectations.

Development & validation 
The Ysea table prototype, as well as all accessory prototypes, have been developed and built in close collaboration with our partners. These prototypes allowed us to validate feasibility, robustness, ergonomics, and real-world use. Everything presented in this campaign is the result of more than two years of development, testing, and refinement alongside our manufacturing partners. 

Production & timelines 
Ysea is produced in collaboration with experienced partners, using proven manufacturing processes. However, as this is our first production run, timelines may be affected by final validation adjustments or supply chain constraints. We have built buffer time into our planning to mitigate this risk. 

Logistics & delivery 
Shipping large furniture items requires careful coordination. While we work with reliable logistics partners, delays related to transport, customs, or local conditions may occur. We will keep backers informed at every stage if adjustments are needed. 

Accessories production 
Some accessories are manufactured in Asia to balance quality, precision, and accessibility. All components are designed by us, tested in real conditions, and integrated into a single, controlled production plan. 

First launch & demand 
Kickstarter allows us to launch Ysea in controlled quantities. High demand could impact production pacing, but it also enables us to scale responsibly while maintaining our quality standards. 

We are fully committed to clear communication throughout the project and will share regular updates so backers always know where things stand.

What makes Ysea different

We are Merelle.

Founded by Sophie & Larry, partners in life, parents of twin daughters, and passionate board game players.

Like many families, we know that time together is precious. Ysea wasn’t designed in a corporate meeting room; it was shaped around our own family dinners, late-night strategy sessions, and countless games. We simply wanted to spend less time clearing the table, and more time making memories.

The name Ysea comes directly from our home: its four letters are taken from the last letter of each family member's first name.

Designed in France and developed with experienced partners, Ysea is the result of years of reflection, testing, and refinement.

Today, we’re proud to invite you to take part in the next chapter of our story.
