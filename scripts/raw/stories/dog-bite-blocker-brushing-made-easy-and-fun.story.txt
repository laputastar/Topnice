Risks and challenges
FreshWoof is committed to providing the best possible quality and experience for both dogs and their owners. However, like any physical product, there are some risks and challenges we want to be transparent about.
  
FreshWoof is designed with durability in mind, especially for heavy chewers. That said, no dog toy is completely indestructible. Since every dog chews differently, aggressive chewers may wear through the toy faster than others. For dogs that chew excessively, we recommend using FreshWoof primarily for teeth brushing sessions.

Another challenge we continue to evaluate is creating a size that comfortably fits dogs of all breeds and sizes. FreshWoof's unique bone-shaped design helps address this challenge by allowing the toy to be positioned differently within a dog's mouth. For dogs with wider mouths, the toy can be placed further forward, while for dogs with narrower mouths, it can be positioned further back. Based on our current testing and data, we believe our universal size (S-M-L) size can comfortably fit dogs ranging from XS through Large, and in many cases even Extra-Large and XXL breeds. However, as we continue testing and collecting feedback, we may determine that adjustments or additional sizing options are necessary to ensure the best possible fit, comfort, and safety for every dog.

As with many manufacturing and shipping processes, delays can occasionally happen and orders may arrive later than the estimated delivery date. If this occurs, we will keep all backers updated throughout the process.

We are committed to transparency, communication, and continuously improving FreshWoof to provide the safest and best experience possible for your dog.
