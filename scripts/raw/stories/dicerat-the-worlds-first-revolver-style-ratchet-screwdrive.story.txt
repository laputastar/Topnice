Product Information

Creative Process

A screwdriver you won't be able to resist fidgeting with.

It swaps bits, drives screws, tackles a variety of complex tasks—and serves as the perfect handheld fidget toy.

Featuring a revolver-style bit cylinder—give it a flick of your finger, and it spins into action: a move that is both stress-relieving and undeniably cool. Its precision ratchet mechanism allows for seamless forward and reverse switching, ensuring your work gets done with crisp, effortless efficiency.

A practical tool? A playful gadget? A piece of mechanical art? It is all of these things—and yet, none of them exclusively. It is called DiceRat—a screwdriver you’ll find yourself reaching for just to give it a spin, simply for the fun of it.

Revolver-style Storage

“Every flick of the wrist is a mechanical romance: a fingertip ritual with the revolver-style bit chamber.”

“Turning tools into firearms: with every rotation, efficiency and enjoyment are simultaneously loaded.”

The powerful neodymium magnet structure within the storage compartment firmly secures the bits, preventing them from falling out.

Rear Free-Spinning Cap

The rotating cap at the top serves a dual purpose: it allows you to fidget with it for stress relief during your downtime, while also providing a steady anchor against your palm when driving screws, making the rotation process smoother and more effortless.

Ratchet Mechanism

A precision ratchet mechanism redefines work efficiency,Simply rotate your wrist—no need to lift or reposition—and say goodbye to the "arm-wrestling" struggle of driving screws.Three-way selector dial for effortless switching: forward drive, reverse drive, and locked—making it easy to tackle tight spaces.

The mechanism engages firmly, delivering a crisp, decisive feel with every turn.Effortless to drive, smooth and comfortable to handle.

Leverage Structure

Engage the Lever: Effortlessly Generate High Torque

Struggling with a stubborn screw? Simply flip down the top lever to instantly extend the leverage arm—delivering massive torque with minimal physical effort.

Adapting to diverse scenarios, the lever unlocks various operating modes—effortless and worry-free.

All-in-One Functionality

This is a true all-in-one solution.Whether disassembling electronics or repairing small appliances, you can tackle everyday tasks with effortless ease.

Simply attach the adapter to unlock infinite possibilities: it is fully compatible with a wide range of bits.

From repairing precision electronic devices to drilling holes in wooden boards, a single screwdriver can handle a wide variety of scenarios.

Think it’s just a revolver-style bit holder?Give it a spin.

Each face bears the markings of a die—This isn't mere decoration, but a deliberately hidden Easter egg.When you flick the cylinder, it becomes far more than just a home for your bits:Spinning, clicking, coming to rest... the number facing upwardreveals a random outcome.

Storage, play, decision-making—Three facets in one object; happiness, loaded and ready.
