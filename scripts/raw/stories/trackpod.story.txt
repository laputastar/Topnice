TrackPodis the next-generation tracking device that does what AirTags couldn't: work seamlessly acrossbothAndroid and Apple ecosystems without compromise.

The Problem We Solved

You own an iPhone, your partner uses Android. Your keys disappear. Your bag gets left behind. Your wallet vanishes at the worst possible moment. Traditional trackers? They only work if everyone uses the same phone. TrackPod breaks that barrier.

What Makes TrackPod Different

Truly Universal CompatibilityTrackPod works with Apple's Find My on iPhonesandintegrates with Android's Find Hub. One device. Both platforms. No app required. Just attach and go.

Battery That Actually LastsForget annual replacements. TrackPod runs on a standard button cell battery that lasts a full365 dayswith ultra-low power consumption. When it does run out, simply pop in a new battery—no charging cables, no hassle.

Built ToughWith anIPX65 water rating, TrackPod laughs in the face of spills, splashes, and surprise rain. Whether it's in your gym bag, on your keys, or attached to your luggage, it's ready for anything.

Precision PositioningKnow exactly where your stuff is with advanced precision positioning technology. No more "somewhere in the apartment"—get the exact location.

Hear It. Find It. Keep It.TrackPod features abuilt-in speakerthat gives audible alerts when you need it. Press the Play Sound button to trigger an alert on the device. Lost your keys under the couch? Listen for the beep.

Smart RemindersTrackPod's intelligent reminder system alerts you when you're about to leave without your tracked item. Heading to the door without your bag? Your phone gets a pop-up notification before you walk out.

Rename & PersonalizeCall it "Work Keys," "Travel Bag," or "That Thing I Always Lose"—TrackPod lets you rename your device for instant recognition.

One Device, One ConnectionClean, simple, focused. TrackPod connects to one device at a time, keeping things straightforward and reliable.

In a world of complicated trackers with short battery life, platform limitations, and dependency on apps you don't want, TrackPod is refreshingly simple. It's the tracker designed forreal life—reliable, durable, inclusive, and honest about what it does.
