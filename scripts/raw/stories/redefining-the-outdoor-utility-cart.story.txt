You bought a wagon to make outdoor life easier.But once you arrive, it's still just a pile of stuff on wheels.

Cooking? You're hunched over a low table.Snacks? Buried at the bottom.Kids tired? Nowhere to rest.Drinks for friends? No surface to put them on.

From Hauler to Hub — Meet LIN JAY.

LIN JAY isn't just a wagon. It's the center of your outdoor setup.

With four adjustable heights, a built-in water valve, dual-layer storage, and the load capacity to handle a full weekend's worth of gear — LIN JAY transforms from carrier to kitchen, from kids' seat to bar top, in seconds.

Four Heights — Because One Size Doesn't Fit Every Moment.

Switch between transport, cooking, dining, and workspace in seconds.

Lower it for heavy loads. Raise it for prep, work, or a standing bar. Every height is engineered for a real outdoor moment.

450L · 250kg · One Trip — Bring It All.

With a spacious 450L capacity, you can load tents, coolers, groceries, chairs, and cooking gear — all in one trip.

The reinforced frame handles up to 250kg (551 lbs), so heavy hauls feel effortless.

Built to stay steady across grass, gravel, and uneven terrain — wherever the weekend takes you.

Built for Real Terrain

All-terrain wheels with reinforced bearings keep LIN JAY rolling smoothly over surfaces that stop ordinary wagons cold — soft grass, loose gravel, sandy beaches, forest paths.

Thoughtful Details, Everywhere You Look.

Every part of LIN JAY is designed to solve a real outdoor frustration.

Smart Storage. Built-in Water - No lifting. No hassle.

Just connect a water container and turn the valve — you're ready to wash, cook, or clean.No buckets. No lifting. No mess. Just clean water, exactly where you need it.

Two Layers. Zero Chaos.

The dual-layer design separates small items from larger gear, so everything stays visible and easy to reach.No more shifting boxes. No more digging for keys. No more lost snacks at the bottom of the pile.

Side-Mount Hooks

Attach bulky items like camping chairs to the outside.Keep the interior open, clean, and easy to use.

Built-In Pockets

With multiple pockets and compartments built into the frame, your essentials stay within reach. No more searching, no more lost items — just grab and go.

Switch between front pulling and rear pushing with ease.Move comfortably across different terrains and situations.Whether you're navigating tight spaces or long distances, control stays smooth and comfortable.
