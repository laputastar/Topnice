Engrave Beyond the Desktop

T1 is not limited to flat desktop engraving. With proper setup, it can supportangled and vertical positioningfor larger orfixed objects. Designed for advanced creative scenarios where standard workspace limitations no longer apply.

Note: For safety and stability, T1 includes adefault tilt protection systemto ensure normal desktop operation. When using extended positioning setups, users should disable tilt protection through software (LaserGRBL / LightBurn) followingofficial instructions and tutorialguidance.
