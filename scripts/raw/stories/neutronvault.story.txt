A laboratory heritage reimagined for thecurieosity-driven. Inspired by sealed source containers, the Neutron Vault keeps your essentials secured and always within reach.

Made for the small things that matter most. Pills, emergency matches, or a lab note you cannot risk losing, the Neutron Vault gathers your everyday kit into one dependable backup you can always rely on, bringing scientific personality to what you carry.

Machined from robust corrosion-resistant stainless steel. The Neutron Vault closes with a tight fit to shield the interior from moisture, dust, and the rough rhythm of everyday carry. With its integrated keyring, it rides on your backpack, travel bag, or keys, always close at hand, the second you need what is inside. A small detail that adds personality to the way you carry essentials.

A canister of stored luminescence that comes alive when darkness falls. Charged by light, the strontium aluminate crystals add the signature Curieous touch, turning the Neutron Vault into an enthralling, vivid green beacon that is mesmerizing to watch and easy to find at a glance, a little reminder that your daily routine can carry some wonder too.

Sealed source containers are the shielding systems that keep powerful radiation safely contained. They are compact capsules that trap radioactive sources behind thick walls of solid metal so the material stays sealed away, preventing leaks, contamination, or unwanted exposure.

Working with radioactivity was much closer to a gamble before sealed source containers took control. Fragile glass tubes and improvised holders could crack or leak, spreading invisible contamination over tables, tools, and lab coats and turning one experiment into a hidden source of exposure.

Sealed source containers made radiation work more reliable. By locking the danger inside solid walls, they allowed radioactivity to be explored safely and turned it into one of the hidden engines behind the technologies that support modern life.
