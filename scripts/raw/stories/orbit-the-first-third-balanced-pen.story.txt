Specs at a Glance

Why Balance Changes Everything

Most pens focus on how they look, or what they are made of. But the real writing experience starts with something you feel from the first stroke:balance.

ORBIT is designed differently. By moving the center of gravity toward the first third of the pen, it lets gravity help bring the tip naturally onto the page — so your hand can guide, not press.

Less force. More control. A smoother writing flow.

Balanced at the First Third — Not the Center

ORBIT is balanced at the first third of the pen — not the center.

The forward-shifted weight helps the tip naturally settle onto the page, creating a writing feel that is more stable, more grounded, and easier to control.

Instead of pressing harder, your hand simply guides the pen forward.

A pen that naturally wants to write.

The 15-Minute Difference

With ORBIT, your hand guides instead of pressing. The front-balanced design helps the tip stay naturally grounded.

After 15 minutes, ordinary pens can make your grip tighten and your fingers work harder. ORBIT is designed to reduce that effort.

Stable from the first stroke. Comfortable after pages.

Self-Stabilizing, Even On a Tilt

ORBIT is not designed to resist movement — it’s designed to settle into stability.

With its forward center of gravity and internal offset structure, the pen naturally finds a balanced resting position, even on slightly tilted surfaces. Up to 5°, it returns to where it belongs.

No uncontrolled rolling. No chasing across the table.

Ergonomic Shape. Comfortable Grip

ORBIT concentrates weight near the tip for stability, while the grip is shaped for a more natural, relaxed hold.

The front section sits comfortably between your fingers, helping reduce pressure during longer writing sessions.

A balanced feel that's stable without ever feeling heavy.

100+ Compatible Refills

A great pen should not be limited to a single refill.

ORBIT is compatible with over 100 Parker-style refills from leading brands including Schmidt, Parker, Rotring, Zebra, OHTO, Schneider, Monteverde, Kaweco, Pelikan, and many more.

One pen. Hundreds of ways to write.

No Lost Springs. No Tiny Parts to Chase

Anyone who has replaced a pen refill knows the moment: the spring pops out, bounces once, and disappears.

ORBIT solves this small but frustrating problem with a fixed spring design. The spring stays secured with the pen tip, so there are no loose parts to lose.

No lost springs. No interruptions.

Schmidt P900 — Smooth, Low Resistance

Balance is only half of the writing experience. The refill matters too.

ORBIT uses a Schmidt P900 refill, selected for its smooth ink flow, clean writing feel, and low-resistance glide. Paired with the front-balanced body, it helps create a writing experience that feels stable, smooth, and effortless.

The balance helps the tip settle. The refill helps it glide.

Metal Build. Solid Feel

ORBIT uses different metals where they matter most.

The stainless steel front tip adds durability, the anodized aluminum body keeps the pen light in hand, and the internal steel weight helps create ORBIT's signature balance.

Built to feel as good as it looks.

Inspired by Proportion. Refined by Design

Inspired by the golden ratio, ORBIT is designed around balanced proportion.

From tip to tail, every detail is carefully structured to create a clean, timeless silhouette.

Balanced in form. Balanced in function.

For centuries, writing has been how we think, create, plan, and remember. Screens changed how we work, but they never replaced the simple connection between pen, paper, and thought.

ORBIT brings balance back to that experience — helping your ideas flow as naturally as they arrive.

Because writing should not feel forced. It should feel natural.
