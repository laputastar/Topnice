We believe that curiosity about the microscopic world shouldn't be limited by bulky equipment or eye strain. That's why we created NexScope—a digital microscope designed to make exploration more comfortable, intuitive, and accessible.

Discover the World from a New Perspective

More than just a microscope, NexScope is a gateway to discovery. Equipped with a 4.6-inch IPS eye-friendly display, a 12MP image sensor, 1080P video recording, a detachable dual-mode design, and expandable storage up to 512GB, it is built to support exploration both indoors and outdoors.

Dual-Mode Design,Seamless Scene Switching

Insert the microscope into the stable base for hands-free observation. Combined with dual-zone LED illumination and adjustable brightness levels, it is ideal for extended observation sessions and detailed inspection tasks.

Detach the main unit and take it wherever curiosity leads. Observe plant textures, inspect collectibles, examine crafts, or explore hard-to-reach surfaces with greater flexibility.One device. Two ways to explore.

Clear & Comfortable Large Display

Traditional microscopes often require users to bend forward and look through a small eyepiece for extended periods. Over time, this can lead to discomfort and fatigue.

NexScope features a built-in 4.6-inch IPS display that allows you to observe comfortably while sitting or standing naturally. The enlarged live view makes observation more enjoyable and accessible for users of all ages.

With 1080P resolution and a smooth 60Hz refresh rate, image movement remains fluid when adjusting focus or examining moving subjects.

Explore the Microscopic World in Greater Detail

With a magnification range from 100x to 1000x, NexScope allows you to explore both larger structures and intricate details.Whether viewing overall patterns or focusing on fine textures, switching between magnification levels is simple and intuitive.

See 1000x More, Discover New Possibilities

Plant Leaves:Under magnification, leaves reveal intricate vein structures and natural patterns that are difficult to appreciate with the naked eye.

Textile Fibers:Observe the woven structures and fiber arrangements that contribute to the texture and characteristics of different fabrics.

Quick Focus Adjustment for Detailed Observation

A smooth hybrid zoom and focusing system allows for precise image adjustment. Each turn of the focus control reveals additional layers of detail, helping you explore subjects with greater confidence.

See Familiar Objects in a Different Way

Electronic Components:Inspect solder joints, circuit traces, and small components with greater clarity, making it useful for electronics observation and educational applications.

Coins and Collectibles:Examine engravings, textures, and surface details that reflect craftsmanship and wear over time.

Dual LED Lighting System

In microscopy, lighting can be just as important as magnification. That's why NexScope features two independent illumination systems designed for different observation needs.

Eight LEDs surround the lens to provide even illumination with reduced shadows, making it suitable for observing the surfaces of opaque objects in handheld mode.The integrated base light shines upward through transparent or semi-transparent samples, making it useful for observing specimens such as plant sections, insect wings, and other thin materials.
