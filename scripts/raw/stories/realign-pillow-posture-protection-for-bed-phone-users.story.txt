Realign Pillowis designed to support your head and hands while helping you maintain better posture and a more comfortable viewing distance during side-lying phone use.

Problem with pillows

We all use our phones in bed.

The problem is not the habit.

The problem is thatregular pillows were never designed for it.

When you lie on your side, your head, hands, and phone all need support, but a regular pillow only supports sleep.

Realign’sdualside shelvesandcontoured headresthold your head and arms in a comfortable, supported position, helping you keep better spinal alignment, and a more comfortable viewing distance while using your phone in bed.

How Realign Compares

Rewards & Add on

Hi, I’mRuiang, the creator of Realign Pillow.

This project started from a very ordinary habit. Using my phone in bed.

I kept noticing the same problems. My neck would bend forward, my hands had nowhere comfortable to rest, and my phone naturally moved closer to my face.

Regular pillows helped me sleep, but they were never designed for the way I actually used my bed every night.

My first solution was simple. I placed a textbook next to my pillow to support my arm while holding my phone. It was not pretty, but it showed me that better support could make a real difference.

That small experiment became the beginning of Realign Pillow.

After testing different designs, prototypes, materials, and pillowcase options, Realign is now ready for Kickstarter.

I’m excited to share Realign Pillow on Kickstarter because I believe this is a small but real problem many people face.With your help, we can bring it to life.
