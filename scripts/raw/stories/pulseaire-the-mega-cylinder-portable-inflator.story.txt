Project Timeline

Flat Tire? Don’t Spend 10 Minutes Waiting.

A low tire warning never comes at the right time. It happens before work, during a road trip, at night, or right before an outdoor adventure. Most portable inflators can help, but many of them are slow, loud, and exhausting to watch. We wanted to build something that feels different the moment you press start — faster airflow, stronger power, and enough battery to handle real vehicles, not just small top-ups.

Why Are Most Portable Inflators Still So Slow?

Many mainstream portable inflators are built around a 60W motor, a 19mm cylinder, and a 4000mAh battery. That may be enough for light emergency use, but it often struggles with larger tires, EVs, SUVs, and high-pressure situations. Our design takes the opposite direction: a 150W high-power motor, a 30mm mega cylinder, and a true 8000mAh battery. Instead of making the pump smaller at the cost of performance, we made the core system stronger.

30mm Mega Cylinder. 706mm² Air Intake Area.

The cylinder is the heart of an inflator, and this is where the biggest difference begins. With a 30mm air cylinder and a 706mm² intake area, our inflator moves far more air with every cycle than typical 19mm or 17mm portable pumps.

That means less waiting, less heat buildup, and a much more confident inflation experience when dealing with real car tires. Instead of simply running longer, the larger cylinder helps the pump move air faster from the start.

Up to 2.5X More Airflow Than Mainstream Pumps

Airflow is what actually saves time. Compared with typical 19mm-class portable inflators, our larger 30mm cylinder provides around 2.5X more air intake volume, allowing the pump to move more air with every cycle instead of simply running longer.

When you are standing beside a tire, that difference feels real. The pressure rises faster, the waiting time feels shorter, and the whole inflation process becomes much less frustrating — especially when dealing with larger vehicle tires.

Faster Than Popular Inflators

In our internal testing, the 30mm cylinder design delivered faster inflation performance than many popular compact inflators using smaller cylinder structures, including 27mm-class and 19mm-class designs. Compared with typical 19mm-class portable inflators, it achieved up to 2.6X faster inflation. We did not want to create just another portable pump — we wanted to build one that clearly feels faster the moment you use it.

150W Motor Built for High-Pressure Tires

A bigger cylinder needs stronger power behind it. That is why we paired the 30mm air system with a 150W high-power motor, giving the inflator the strength needed for demanding tire pressure scenarios. Whether you are inflating an SUV tire, an EV tire, or a high-pressure bicycle tire, the motor is designed to keep output stable without feeling underpowered halfway through the job.

Built for Bigger Tires and Higher Pressure

EVs and SUVs are heavier, and their tires often require higher pressure and larger air volume than traditional fuel vehicles. Some EV tires can run around 20%–30% higher pressure than ordinary passenger car tires, which makes small inflators feel slow and strained. This inflator was designed with those modern driving needs in mind, giving EV and SUV owners a portable pump that actually matches the vehicles they drive.
