Confidence on Every Terrain

Rokibot G7 AWD delivers true all-terrain performance for effortless lawn care. Powered byTrue AWD traction, it handlesup to 80% slopeswith stability and control across challenging ground.

WithNetRTK and Visual SLAM navigation, it builds a precise real-time map of your lawn for accurate positioning, efficient coverage, and wire-free path planning. The result is a smarter and fully hands-free mowing experience across any yard.

Ultimate All-Wheel Drive System

From steep 80% (38.7°) slopes to wet, uneven ground—G7 keeps moving, gripping, and cutting without hesitation. Built for complex lawns, it delivers one ultimate solution where others falter.

Smooth Over Bumps

With anadaptive suspension system, the G7 keeps steady ground contact across uneven terrain, ensuring a smooth and uniform cut every time.

Agility Leaves No Trace

Precision 4-wheel drive lets the mower turn on the spot-without tearing or scuffing your lawn, even on delicate grass.

Twice the Power Half the Time

Professional efficiency in every pass. With a wide350mm cutting width,dual high-torque motors, and a turf-protective blade disc, the G7 cuts through dense grass with speed and precision.

Built for Efficient Lawn Coverage

Intelligent pathing meets high-end robotics. Achieve professional-grade efficiency with autonomous precision across any landscape.

Multi-Map Management

Create and store separate maps for disconnected lawns or complex estates.

Ride-on Edge Cutting

Ride-on edge cutting improves coverage along accessible lawn borders.

Multi-Directional Mowing

Angles are alternated to prevent grass flattening and promote healthy growth.

Mowing, Made Even Simpler

- No Antennas Simple Setup

No boundary wires or RTK base station required. With NetRTK precision positioning, G7 maps your entire lawn in minutes-just walk and go.

- Seamless Navigation

VSLAM visual positioning uses high-definition cameras to build a real-time map of the environment, enabling stable navigation even in GPS-challenged areas such as under tree cover or near buildings.

Smart Autonomous Mowing

- Multi-Zone Management

- AI-Powered Obstacle Avoidance

Auto-Return & Recharge

Smart low-battery detection ensures the mower automatically returns to its base when needed. Once recharged, it resumes mowing from where it left off-delivering a truly hands-free lawn care experience.
