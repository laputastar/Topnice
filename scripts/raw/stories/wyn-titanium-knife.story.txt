[RISKS & CHALLENGES]
Risks and challenges
We’ve successfully brought multiple products from concept to delivery, and we take timelines and quality seriously. That said, every hardware project comes with risks. Here’s what we’re watching closely and how we plan to handle it.

Production & Quality Control

Even with an established design, small variations can happen in machining, finishing, and assembly (fit, slider feel, detent engagement, blade tolerance). We will inspect every batch, test slider action and detents, and reject or rework parts that don’t meet our standards before anything ships.

Bronze Slider Consistency

Bronze parts can show slight color variation between batches and may develop a natural patina over time. This does not affect performance. We’ll maintain consistent material specs and finishing processes to keep appearance as uniform as possible.

Finishing Variations (Natural Titanium & Black PVD)

Natural titanium can show subtle tone differences depending on lighting and finishing. Black PVD is durable, but like any coating, it can show wear over time with heavy use or abrasion. We’ll work with our finishing partners to maintain consistent appearance and durability, and we’ll spot-check batches before assembly.

Supply Chain & Shipping Delays

We plan to ship quickly, but delays can still happen due to shipping carriers, customs, material availability, or unexpected disruptions (including holidays). If anything impacts the schedule, we’ll communicate early and clearly with updated timelines.

Reward Logistics & Address Collection

Shipping begins after the campaign ends and backers complete surveys. Delays in survey completion or address issues can slow fulfillment. Please respond to surveys promptly and double-check your address to help us ship on time.

Taxes, VAT, and Duties

International backers may be responsible for VAT, import duties, or local fees depending on your country. We cannot control these charges.

Warranty / Support

We stand behind our products. If you receive a defective item, contact us and we’ll make it right. Normal wear, blade-related damage, or misuse is not considered a defect.

Safety Notice

This is a cutting tool. Always handle blades carefully, keep fingers clear of the edge during blade changes, and store safely away from children. Please follow local laws and regulations related to carrying tools/blades in your area.
