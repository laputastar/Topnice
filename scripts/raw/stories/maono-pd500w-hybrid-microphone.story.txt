Risks and challenges
---Risks and Challenges

PD500W has moved beyond the concept stage and is now going through validation, creator testing, production preparation, and quality planning. Because this project combines microphone hardware, 2.4GHz wireless transmission, built-in recording, battery power, MaonoLink App control, and AI workflow tools, we want to be clear about the risks we are actively managing before delivery.

Kickstarter rewards are not the same as retail purchases, and estimated delivery dates are estimates rather than guarantees. Our responsibility is to reduce risk where we can, communicate clearly when plans change, and deliver PD500W with the quality creators expect.

---Manufacturing and Production

Final production may be affected by component lead times, assembly yield, tooling adjustments, supplier schedules, or packaging changes. To reduce this risk, we are validating DVT samples, preparing pilot production before mass production, and working with manufacturing partners to check assembly, packaging, and batch quality before fulfillment.

--- Audio, Wireless, and Recording Reliability

PD500W needs to perform reliably across real creator workflows: wireless camera and phone recording, USB-C recording, XLR output, two-person separate-track recording, built-in WAV recording, 32-bit float recording, safety track, timecode, battery life, charging, and use while charging.

Wireless range, battery life, and recording performance may vary depending on environment, interference, settings, connected devices, and usage mode. We will continue testing these workflows and communicate performance conditions clearly. If testing reveals issues that require more work, we may adjust the timeline rather than ship unresolved problems.

--- App, Firmware, and Compatibility

MaonoLink adds an important software layer to PD500W, including presets, sound controls, recording control, AI noise reduction, digital de-popper, transcription, and summary tools. Because the app needs to support Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, wireless mode, and USB mode, additional optimization may be required before or after fulfillment.

We will continue compatibility testing across supported platforms and improve app and firmware behavior through updates where needed. App control is planned for wireless and USB workflows; XLR use through an audio interface does not support MaonoLink control.

--- AI Feature Availability

AI transcription, speech-to-text, and summary are post-recording workflow features. They are not real-time transcription or real-time translation features, and they require an internet connection. Results may vary depending on language, accent, background noise, audio quality, and service availability.

The planned 300 free AI minutes per month, supported languages, account rules, region availability, and any related service terms will be confirmed before launch or fulfillment. We will be transparent about what the AI tools can do, what they cannot do, and any conditions needed for them to work.

--- Certification, Compliance, and Final Specifications

PD500W may require market-specific wireless certification, battery and shipping documentation, product labeling, final documentation review, and compliance checks before rewards can ship. These steps can sometimes take longer than expected, especially for a product with wireless transmission and a built-in battery.

Final certification status, specifications, package contents, and documentation will be updated once they are approved by the product and compliance teams.

---Shipping and Fulfillment

International fulfillment can be affected by shipping rates, courier capacity, customs processing, VAT or import-fee rules, package dimensions and weight, remote-area delivery, address accuracy, and regional policy changes. Final supported markets, shipping fees, and VAT/customs coverage will be confirmed in the campaign shipping policy and/or before fulfillment begins.

Before shipping, we will collect or confirm backer addresses through a Kickstarter survey or pledge manager. Accurate shipping information from backers will be important for smooth delivery.

---Timeline and Communication

Our current plan includes final validation, pilot production, mass production, quality inspection, and shipping to backers. Even with careful planning, hardware production can face unexpected delays.

If the timeline changes, we will share updates with backers, explain what happened, what we are doing next, and how it affects the delivery plan. Maono has experience developing and shipping creator audio products, and our previous Kickstarter projects have taught us how important transparent communication is to backers.

Our goal is to deliver PD500W responsibly, with clear updates, careful quality control, and the product experience creators are backing us to build.
