Risks and challenges
Delays are not anticipated, but international manufacturing and fulfillment can be influenced by factors outside our control, including changes in tariffs, transportation costs, supply chain disruptions, or broader global events. One of our primary priorities is ensuring that every magnetic pedalboard reaches backers safely, reliably, and at a reasonable shipping cost.
Shipping charges will be handled separately after the campaign through our pledge manager, allowing backers to choose from available delivery options based on their location. Depending on your country, additional customs duties, taxes, or import fees may apply and are the responsibility of the backer.
Our goal is to keep production and fulfillment running efficiently and, whenever possible, ahead of schedule. If any unexpected challenges arise, we will communicate clearly and promptly with all backers. Transparency throughout the entire process is extremely important to us.
