From Idea to Reality

Environmental commitments

Have you ever struggled to quickly detach a key or tool when you needed it most? Or wasted time dealing with tangled, inefficient keychains in everyday moments?

Existing solutions all come with trade-offs.

Magnetic connectors are fast but often lack reliability under load. Mechanical connectors are secure but slow and inconvenient, usually requiring two hands. Even everyday keyrings, carabiners, and clips force compromises between speed, strength, and usability.

We wanted something better — a connection system that is fast, secure, lightweight, and effortless in daily use.

And that is why TiLok was created

What do you get?

A connection system designed to redefine the way you carry and connect your everyday gear.

TiLok brings a new level of confidence when securing your most important tools — strong enough for demanding environments, yet fast and effortless for daily use.

It gives you the freedom to build a modular carry system that adapts to your lifestyle, whether at home, in the city, or outdoors.

And most importantly, it removes the small frustrations you deal with every day — slow connections, unreliable clips, and inconvenient access.

TiLok is designed to make carrying feel simple again.

Structural Strength Beyond Magnetic Systems

Unlike traditional magnetic connectors that rely solely on magnetic force for retention, TiLok integrates a mechanical three-point locking structure that fundamentally enhances load stability and security.

Once engaged, the load is distributed across three mechanical contact points rather than a single magnetic interface, significantly improving resistance under tension, movement, and daily stress.

This structural design allows TiLok to deliver a higher level of holding strength and reliability compared to conventional magnetic-only systems, making it suitable for demanding everyday carry scenarios.

Invisible by Design — Ultra Compact & Lightweight
