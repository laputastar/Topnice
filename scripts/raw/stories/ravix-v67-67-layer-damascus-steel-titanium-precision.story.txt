Risks and challenges
Ravix V67 combines 67-layer VG10 Damascus steel, a titanium alloy handle, and precision-machined details. This requires careful control over material preparation, CNC machining, surface finishing, assembly, and quality inspection.

We have already completed working prototypes and will continue optimizing production details before mass manufacturing. During production, we will closely monitor blade finishing, handle texture, pivot assembly, folding smoothness, and overall fit and finish to ensure each unit meets our quality standards.

International fulfillment may also bring challenges, as EDC tools can be subject to different import rules depending on the destination country or region. We will work with experienced logistics partners and review shipping requirements carefully. Backers should also check their local regulations before pledging.

If delays occur due to material preparation, machining, quality control, packaging, or customs clearance, we will provide timely updates and keep the process transparent.
