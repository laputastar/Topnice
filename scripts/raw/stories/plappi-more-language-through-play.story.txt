Why I'm building Plappi

I'm Nemanja, father of two. My partner doesn't speak my mother tongue - which means I am theonly sourcethrough which my child can learn my language. But between work, the household and everything else: when am I supposed to manage that?

Plappi grew out of exactly this frustration -not to replace me, but to make the time in between count, so my child keeps hearing and practising my language even when I can't be there.

Plappi is ascreen-free, voice-based language-learning devicefor children aged 2-10. At its core is not a chatbot - it is astructured language programme we built ourselves: weekly vocabulary that is introduced, practised and tested, organised into age-appropriate curricula and reinforced with spaced repetition so words stick for good. No screen. No ads.

Structured curricula- designed by us, updated monthly, based on how children actually learn a language best

Weekly vocabulary- introduced, practised and reviewed; difficulty grows with your child's progress

Spaced repetition- scientifically proven to make new words stick

Personalised bilingual stories- using your child's name, friends and favourite topics

15-minute sessions- matched to a child's attention span

27 languages from day one

Much more than "an AI"

Plappi isnot a typical AI appwith a chatbot bolted on. The real work - and our creative core - is thelanguage pedagogy: the curricula, the weekly vocabulary plans, the learning-progression logic and the spaced-repetition methodology, drawn from research on how children best acquire a second language. AI is simply the engine that delivers this human-designed programme by voice, screen-free - a means to an end, not the product itself.

Your data belongs to you. Full stop.

Made for parents

The parent app gives you a full dashboard: progress, words learned and upcoming reviews - and you decide what Plappi knows about your child and what it teaches next.

Risks and challenges

Hardware is demanding. We know the typical risks — supply chains, certification, timelines — and we plan for them deliberately: a conservative timeline (delivery September 2027), a buffer built into the budget, and personal on-site quality audits at our manufacturers in Europe or China. Should delays occur, we will keep you informed transparently and regularly — trust matters more to us than an optimistic promise.

We are building Plappi, a screen-free language-learning device for children, designed and made in Austria. The creative core of this project is our own pedagogy: structured, age-appropriate language curricula, weekly vocabulary plans, a learning-progression system and a spaced-repetition methodology — all designed by our team based on research into how children best acquire a second language.

AI is used only as the delivery engine that voices this human-designed curriculum and adapts difficulty and personalised stories to each child's progress. It is not a chatbot companion and not an AI-generated product — the curriculum, content structure, methodology and device design are human-made.

Privacy and child safety are core design principles: we use offline / on-device models wherever possible, process data exclusively on EU servers, never train AI models on children's data, and are fully GDPR- and EU-AI-Act-compliant.
