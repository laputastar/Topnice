[RISKS & CHALLENGES]
Risks and challenges
Every project comes with challenges, spanning from production to fulfillment, and we believe the best way to handle them is with honesty and transparency.

Manufacturing & Quality Control
Vera is built from precision materials, including Japanese β153 memory titanium, imported nylon retention wire, skin-friendly silicone nose pads, and high-toughness lenses, with each sourced from specialized suppliers. Prototyping is complete, final samples are approved, and our manufacturing partners are confirmed. We've set strict quality checkpoints throughout all production stages, for we will never deliver products that fail to meet our standards. We make every effort to ensure every pair of Vera glasses delivers reliable and satisfying quality.

Lens Customization Complexity
Offering prescription, progressive, photochromic, and eye comfort lenses means every order is custom-made. This brings more fulfillment complexity than standard products. We've tailored our post-campaign survey and fulfillment process specifically for this. If demand exceeds expectations, some orders may require additional production time. When that happens, it's simply because we're making sure every pair meets the standard Vera was built to. Quality always comes first.

Shipping & Logistics
We ship worldwide. Customs clearance, local carrier handoffs, and last-mile delivery involve factors beyond our direct control, but we stay engaged every step of the way. Our team monitors the fulfillment process closely and will do everything we can to keep your order on track. If anything comes up, we'll reach out promptly and work to resolve it as quickly as possible.

Our Commitment
We have been preparing for this launch since July 2024. The design is finalized, samples are fully tested, and mass production is well-prepared. This is no longer just a concept waiting for funding. We sincerely invite you to be among the first owners of this product we have polished for years. We will keep you updated immediately if there is any change.
