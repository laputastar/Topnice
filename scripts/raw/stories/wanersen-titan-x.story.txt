Risks and challenges
The TITAN-X project is significantly beyond the concept stage.

Working prototypes have been completed, tested, refined, and validated prior to launching this Kickstarter campaign. Manufacturing partners have already been secured, production processes have been established, and initial production has begun.

While every manufacturing project carries some level of risk, we have taken substantial steps to reduce uncertainty before asking for your support.

Potential challenges may include production scheduling, material availability, quality control, and international shipping timelines. To address these risks, we have worked closely with experienced manufacturing partners, completed multiple prototype iterations, and established quality inspection procedures throughout the production process.

Because tooling, engineering, and production preparation have already been completed, the primary focus after funding is scaling production, completing final assembly, performing quality control inspections, and fulfilling rewards to backers.

We are committed to transparent communication throughout the campaign and fulfillment process. Should any unexpected delays occur, we will provide regular updates and keep backers informed every step of the way.

Our goal is simple: deliver a premium titanium EDC pen that meets the quality standards demonstrated by our prototypes and exceeds the expectations of our supporters.

AtWANERSEN, we've always been fascinated by precision-machined everyday carry gear.

Knives, flashlights, tools, and pens all share something in common: they are objects we reach for every single day. The best ones don't simply perform a task. They create an experience.

When we began developingTITAN-X,we didn't start with the goal of making another titanium pen.

We started with a simple question:

What if a pen could be as satisfying to use as your favorite piece of EDC gear?

Most pens are designed solely around writing performance. While writing quality is important, we believed there was an opportunity to create something that delivers enjoyment long before the tip touches paper.

That idea led us to focus on the click.

After countless prototypes, refinements, and adjustments, we developed a mechanism that produces the crisp mechanical feedback we were searching for. A click that feels deliberate. Precise. Rewarding.

A click that makes you want to press it again.

Around that mechanism, we built everything else: a fully machined Grade 5 titanium body, deep CNC-milled grip texture, a ceramic-ball pocket clip, and a rugged design language inspired by premium EDC equipment.

The result isTITAN-X.

A pen built not only to write, but to be carried, used, appreciated, and enjoyed every day.

Whether you're signing contracts, sketching ideas, taking notes, or simply enjoying the feel of finely engineered gear, TITAN-X was created to become the pen you reach for first.

Thank you for joining us on this journey and helping bring TITAN-X to life.
