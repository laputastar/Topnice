Semiconductor Cooling + Active Fan Cooling

The dangers of excessive charging heat are well known. Here are some of the most common effects:

1. Shortened Battery Lifespan

Excessive heat accelerates battery degradation, leading to:

Reduced battery health

Shorter battery life between charges

Battery aging begins to accelerate above 35°C (95°F) and becomes significantly worse with prolonged exposure to temperatures above 55°C (131°F).

2. Reduced Device Performance

When temperatures rise, smartphones automatically throttle performance to protect internal components.

This can result in:

Lower gaming frame rates

Slower video editing and processing

System lag and reduced responsiveness

The problem becomes especially noticeable when gaming while wireless charging.

Active Fan Cooling

When your phone reaches35°C (95°F)—the critical threshold for battery health—the active cooling system automatically kicks in.

A high-speed fan spins at up to10,000 RPM, rapidly drawing heat away from the battery and core circuitry.

The cooling fan is optimized for low-noise performance, operating atless than 19dB.

Enjoy powerful cooling without the distraction of noise.

Efficient cooling. Quiet performance. Uninterrupted focus.
