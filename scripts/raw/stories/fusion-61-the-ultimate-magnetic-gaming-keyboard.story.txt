Brand New PCB Architecture

Two separate PCB boards, each with its own chip. —one exclusively for key input, the other for RGB lighting.

By physically isolating the lighting circuitry, it eliminates signal noise and thermal interference generated by RGB lighting components. This allows the main keyboard circuit board to focus entirely on high-speed, accurate key processing.

Engraved Feather Aluminum Case

Milled from a single block of aluminum. Only CNC machining can shape aluminum into a clean, solid block, never spliced.

The #200-grit anodized processing,creating a smooth, matte surface that resists scratches and fingerprints, with a subtle sheen and a reassuringly solid feel in hand.

The final signature detail is a laser-engraved feather pattern that subtly shifts with light and movement.

Dual RGB LEDs for brighter, more balanced RGB Effects. Larger keys — including Left Shift, Right Shift, Backspace, and Enter — 4 RGB LEDs each. Spacebar — 10 RGB LEDs for a fuller, smoother lighting.

Comes with 22 types of RGB Effects. You can control the RGB hue, saturation, brightness, effect speed, and different modes.

Fusion 61-Features

Unlike many keyboards built on generic third-party solutions, our hardware and firmware are developed entirely in-house for performance.

Frozen RGB Switch

Unlike traditional mechanical keyboards, Hall Effect keyboards use magnetic sensors to detect every keystroke with exceptional speed and accuracy. This technology delivers ultra-fast response times, precise input recognition, and fully adjustable actuation points, allowing you to customize each key to match your gaming or typing style.

Semi-Transparency Housing, Turn your keyboard into the undisputed visual centerpiece of your desk. The fully pure structure eliminates all light obstruction. RGB beams penetrate losslessly, creating a polished, ice-like radiant halo without any dark spots.

The Fusion 61 supports multiple types of magnetic switches, It is compatible with options such as Magnetic Jade, Magnetic White, Hornet Magnetic, and TTC KOM switches. Simply install your preferred switches and calibrate them through software for a custom fit.

With Rapid Trigger, every key resets and re-triggers the moment its travel direction changes — no need to return to the top position. This eliminates the fixed reset limitation of traditional switches, giving you faster repeated inputs, sharper movement, and tighter control when speed matters most.
