We Know Your Pain Points

01. A 75kWh Battery You Can’t Even Use

Your Tesla has a massive battery, but you can’t actually use it. The Tesla Model Y has around 75 kWh of capacity, while most portable power stations only offer 1–2 kWh — meaning your Tesla holds over 30× more energy 🔋.

Yet, in real situations like blackouts, camping, or outdoor work, that power remains locked inside your car and remains stranded when you need it most⛺️.

02. Ending the Tesla V2L "Horror Stories."

As a Kickstarter veteran, you’ve likely seen the dark side of the V2L category: backers paying for vaporware that never ships, leaving them with no product and no recourse. As a global leader in DC-to-AC conversion, we’re sorry it took this long to bring the NACS/CCS2 versions to the global stage. In this campaign, we’re skipping the flashy hype. We’re here with transparent engineering and an honest, professional approach to show you why WETOX D2 is the most reliable tool in your kit.

Purpose-Built Design 💎

We’re not here to force our aesthetic on you—though we believe the WETOX D2 is the most elegant, high-tech adapter on the market. Good design is about more than looks; it’s about solving two critical engineering problems.

Engineering-First Structure

Standard V2L adapters are often just communication boards and inverter modules crammed into off-the-shelf sheet metal boxes. It’s the cheapest way to build, requiring zero custom tooling.

We refused to do that. Every component in the WETOX D2 is precision-engineered. Our custom-molded internal structure ensures every part fits perfectly, providing superior stability, safety, and the tight seals required for our IPX5 waterproof rating.

Original Engineering. No Rebranding

Ever wonder why most Tesla V2L adapters look identical? Because they are "white-label" generic products sold by different trading companies. They use the same off-the-shelf inverter modules and aluminum shells, just with different logos.
