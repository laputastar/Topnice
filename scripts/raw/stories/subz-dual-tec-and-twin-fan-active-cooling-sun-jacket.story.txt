Why Kickstarter

Imagine standing under a relentless midday sun, where the air is thick and the heat feels like a physical weight. You reach for a traditional cooling vest, only to find that its fans are simply circulating the same sweltering air against your skin. You’re left drenched in sweat, with the "cooling" gear adding nothing but bulk. We believed there had to be a better way to reclaim the outdoors. Meet SubZ—not just another fan jacket, but a wearable climate control system designed to deliver an Arctic breeze whenever and wherever you need it.

Why choose SubZ

10-Second Instant

Chill It eliminates the wait for relief. Within just 10 seconds of activation, it delivers a powerful cooling sensation that can reduce your core body temperature by 15°C to 20°C (27°F to 36°F). Whether you are hiking a trail or working on-site, it provides an immediate escape from the heat.

Active Dual-TEC & Twin-Fan

It redefines cooling through its Dual-TEC Peltier modules, which actively convert electricity into cold energy. Unlike traditional jackets that rely solely on passive fans to move hot air, its Twin-Fan system works in tandem with the TEC modules to blast actual chilled air across your body.

Ergonomic 3D Airflow

It is engineered for comfort without compromise. The internal 3D airflow structure ensures that cold air is distributed uniformly across your upper body, preventing uncomfortable cold spots. Its lightweight, modular design feels natural and non-restrictive, making it perfect for active movement.

4-Speed Intelligent Control

It adapts to your specific environment with a single touch. You can cycle through 4 adjustable cooling levels to find your perfect balance. From a gentle Level 4 breeze to the maximum power of Level 1 for extreme heat, you are always in total control of your microclimate.

UPF 50+ & Moisture Wicking

It serves as your ultimate outdoor shield. The 100% nylon fabric provides a professional UPF 50+ rating, blocking harmful UVA and UVB rays. Simultaneously, its moisture-wicking technology actively regulates sweat, ensuring you remain dry and comfortable even in high humidity.

Multi-Scenario Modular Cooling

It seamlessly adapts to your every adventure. Whether you arehikingsteep trails,fishingunder a blazing sun,campingin the wild, or handlingprofessional outdoor labor, it delivers uncompromising relief. Itsdetachable modular coreeasily swaps between different compatible garments, ensuring you have the perfect active cooling companion for any scenario.

Easy Machine Washable

It simplifies maintenance for a busy lifestyle. Thanks to the two-step quick-release system, you can detach the electronic cooling modules in seconds. The jacket itself is fully machine washable, ensuring your gear stays fresh and hygienic after every adventure without the hassle of hand-washing.

What's in the box

We're a dedicated team of thermal engineers and technical apparel designers with a focus on active cooling solutions. Our lead engineer specializes in TEC Peltier module integration, while our product lead brings years of experience in ergonomic wearable tech and high-performance fabrics. Having successfully developed innovative temperature-control wear, we are thrilled to introduce SubZ—our first global launch that redefines active cooling and outdoor sun protection.

Why Kickstarter

We chose Kickstarter to validate demand before mass production. Your backing helps us secure larger orders for Dual-TEC modules and high-speed motors, ensuring better pricing without compromising hardware quality.
