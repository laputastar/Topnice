One Pot, Multiple Ways To Grow

How Does Self-Watering Work

Keeping plants alive shouldn’t feel like a guessing game.

Between remembering when to water, knowing when to repot and keeping up with a plant’s changing needs, plant care can quickly become confusing, messy and time-consuming.

Helix keeps plant care simple from the start.

Helix is a self-watering plant pot that expands with your plant, with accessories to help you grow from seed, propagate cuttings and support climbing plants.

Featured by leading voices in design, interiors and plant care.

The Sprout Plate creates a simple seed-starting setup inside your Helix pot, helping you grow from the very beginning, simply expanding the pot as your plant develops.

How To Grow From Seed with Helix

The Sprig Plate supports plant cuttings as they root, turning your Helix into a simple propagation station, from first cutting to established growth.

How To Grow From Cuttings With Helix

Trellis gives climbing plants flexible support as they grow taller, simply add another layer when your plant is ready for more height.

Space Saving Design

The folding structure reduces packaging and shipping space, while giving your plant more room when it needs it.

Plant care, made easier

Helix is designed to make plant care easier day to day, helping with watering, growth and support, while looking good enough to keep on display.

Designed to waste less

Helix combines recycled materials, flat-pack efficiency and long-lasting design to reduce waste at every stage, from production and shipping to everyday use at home.

We know how to make this happen

POTR has already taken award-winning self-watering products from concept to production. Now we’re applying that experience to Helix, from recycled materials and tooling to the final flat-pack assembly.

Production Process

Years in the making

Before Helix, there was our original self-watering origami pot — a £600 Kickstarter idea that helped us rethink what plant care could look like. Since then, POTR has grown through new products, awards, collaborations and years of material experimentation, all leading to our most advanced plant pot yet.

We’ve done this before

Helix is a new design, but manufacturing plant care products is not new to us. Our past products have already been produced using recycled plastics, precision tooling and flat-pack processes, giving us the experience to take Helix from prototype to production.
