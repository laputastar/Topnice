Why is multifunctionality environmentally friendly?

What do you think of the30 functions? It may sound funny, but they're real.

SCRUBBIO's multifunctionalismis justified by the quest to design new,sustainable objects. Your life doesn't have to adapt to the product, but your product must adapt to your life changes.

Multifunctionalismisessentialbecause:

It extends the product's lifespan: as soon as the product is worn out or useless, use it to meet another need.

Itavoids throwing away the productwhen you don't need it: better than throwing it away, reuse the product foranother purpose.

If you give theSCRUBBIO® as a gift, the person who receives itwill definitely find a usefor the 30 functions instead of throwing it away.

It's better to produce only one productthat fulfills30 functionsthan toproduce 30 products, each fulfilling a single function.This greatly reduces the pollutionassociated with the production of 30 products.
