Long-lasting design

VoltArk is built for repeated travel use with integrated US / EU / UK / AU plugs, a fire-resistant PC shell, GaN III power architecture, multiple protection systems, and a replaceable 250Vac 10A fuse. It helps reduce the need to buy and carry separate chargers and plug adapters for every trip.
