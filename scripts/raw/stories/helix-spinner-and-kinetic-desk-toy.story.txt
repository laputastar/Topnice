Introducing HELIX🧬

A unique and mesmerizing double-arm spinner.

Imagine taking the classic fidget spinner you know and love... anddoubling the fun.Spin them in perfect sync formesmerizing helical motion, or independently for wild combos and anoptical illusion experience. Everyday carry design meets addictive fidget engineering.

The Double Helix Spinnerisn't just another spinner –it's a game-changerfor fidget enthusiasts, desk warriors, and trick masters alike.

The strategically placed holes you see across the arms arenot random- they are the result of deliberate design and artistic vision. Perfectly aligned, these holes create a captivatingoptical illusionas the spinner rotates. When in motion, the pattern of precisely drilled voids interacts with light and shadow, producing amesmerizing “strobing” or “ghosting” effectthat makes the arms appear to blur, multiply, or even form temporary geometric patterns in the air.

It turns every spin into ahypnotic visual performance. Different speeds and directions (e.g. clockwise or counterclockwise) will produce different effects.

Dual-Action Innovation: Two high-performance spinner arms connected via a sturdy, low-friction bearing axle. Twist them together like a double helix for smooth, synchronized spins – or flick each end separately for complex tricks and hybrid moves no single spinner can match.

Stress Relief & Focus Boost: Like classic spinners, its fun, fidgetty, and provides satisfying tactile feedback. But with double the engagement – it's twice as awesome.

Epic Spin Times: Precision bearings + balanced weight distribution deliver spins that last 5+ minutes. Longer, smoother, and more satisfying than standard spinners.

Zinc: Anodized zinc body, steel axle, steel bearings. Durable, pocket-friendly, and feels confident in hand.

TheTitanium Silver with Coppergripoffers a crisp, contemporary look. Its matte silver body feels dense and solid in the hand, while the glowing rose copper center button adds a subtle pop of warmth and luxury.

TheAntique Brass with Black gripdelivers rich, golden depth with a classic, heirloom appeal. The warm brass finish pairs perfectly with the sleek, high-gloss black center, creating a bold, eye-catching contrast that turns heads every time it spins.

TheGritty Gold with Gold gripDelivers a sleek, monochromatic look with a clean, modern edge. The gritty gold finish across the body and grip creates a seamless, minimalist appearance that emphasizes the spinner’s unique double-arm geometry and smooth mechanical action.

My son, who is Autistic, immediately latched onto it. He couldn’t put it down. What started as curiosityquickly became one of his favorite toys— something he reaches for when he needs to play. Watching him find joy and calm in the smooth spin and flowing optical illusiongave me more reason to bring HELIX to life.
