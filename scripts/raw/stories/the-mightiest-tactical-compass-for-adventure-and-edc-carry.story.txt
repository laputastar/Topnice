Environmental commitments

When you're miles away from civilization—or simply navigating everyday challenges—the last thing you need is a pocket full of bulky tools.

Ti-Trailblazercombines navigation, survival, utility, and craftsmanship into a single titanium EDC companion that's always ready when you are.

The Ti-Trailblazeris precision-machined from aerospace-grade titanium, measuring just 60mm in diameter and 16mm thick, and integrates ten practical functions in a single compact body. Designed for explorers, makers, campers, and anyone who believes that preparedness should fit in the palm of your hand. Available in stainless steel, brass, and pure titanium.
