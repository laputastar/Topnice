Risks and challenges
Hardware development is a complex process, and we want to be transparent with our backers about the challenges ahead.

Manufacturing and Tooling:VoltGale is currently in the prototype stage. Moving from prototype testing to final tooling and mass production may involve small adjustments to the internal structure, button feel, surface finish, and accessory fit. We will continue testing and refining these details to ensure stable performance and consistent build quality.

Battery and Safety Testing:VoltGale uses a high-capacity 16.8V 10,000mAh battery system and supports 65W USB-C fast charging. Battery safety, thermal control, and charging stability are important parts of our development process. We will complete the necessary testing and safety checks before shipment.

Supply Chain and Fulfillment:Key components such as the motor, battery cells, aluminum body parts, and turbine components may be affected by supplier lead times or logistics changes. To reduce this risk, we will work closely with our suppliers and prepare backup plans when possible.
We will keep backers updated throughout production, testing, and fulfillment. If any delays or changes occur, we will communicate them clearly and work to solve them responsibly.

The Problem: Weak. Wasteful.

Canned Air?It’s wasteful, freezes your hands, sprays liquid residue, and loses pressure in seconds.

Generic Electric Dusters?They are just noisy toys with weak plastic fans that can't move sticky dust.

You need real power.You need raw power. You need consistent velocity. You need VoltGale.

Meet VoltGale. We packed 1000W turbo power into a compact cordless blower built for real-world cleaning, drying, and blasting tasks.

With up to 1300g of thrust, 55m/s high-speed airflow, a 65,000 RPM motor, and a massive 40,000mAh battery, VoltGale delivers serious air power anywhere you need it — from garage cleanup to outdoor gear, home spaces, and daily utility.

Engineered for Extreme Air Power

1300g Violent Thrust

Not just wind — real thrust.

1300g of thrust turns airflow into visible force.From blasting snow off surfaces to rippling water and spinning heavy objects, VoltGale delivers a concentrated air stream that doesn’t just blow — it pushes, moves, and proves its power.

1000W Turbo Airflow System

Powered by a1000W turbo motor system, VoltGale spins up to65,000 RPM to generate a focused 55m/s high-speed airflow— delivering fast acceleration, stable output, and serious blowing performance in a compact cordless body.

This incredible wind speed delivers powerful gusts that quickly blow awaywater droplets,snowandleavesfrom surfaces, ensuring efficient and thorough cleaning in no time.

4-Speed Control（Turbo Mode）

we optimized the airflow dynamics.The VoltGalefeatures 4 adjustable wind speeds, allowing you to select the most suitable setting for different scenarios and needs.

40,000mAh Power System

A large-capacity battery gives VoltGale the endurance to support high-power airflow without being tied to a wall outlet. From quick cleanup to longer outdoor tasks, it is built to keep serious air power ready in your hand.

Emergency Power Station

More than a turbo blower, VoltGale can also work as a backup power source for essential devices. When your phone, light, or small USB-powered gear runs low, itspower bank functionhelps keep you connected and prepared.

Intelligent AI Protection

Power means nothing without protection.

VoltGale is equipped with multiple intelligent safety systems, including overheat protection, overcurrent protection, short-circuit protection, and motor stall protection.These safeguards continuously monitor operation to help ensure stable performance, protect internal components, and extend the lifespan of the device even during demanding use.

Sleek & Stylish Design &Comfortable Grip

VoltGale boasts a sleek and stylish design like theHammer of Thor. Crafted with attention to detail, it not only delivers outstanding functionality but also adds a touch of sophistication to your cleaning routine.

Weighing just1.350kg(compared to traditional 3kg blowers) and measuring221 x78x88mm, the VoltGale is3x smallerand designed for convenient one-bag carrying. Carry it anywhere, store it effortlessly—cleaning has never been this convenient.
