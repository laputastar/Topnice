Risks and challenges
Lumysi’s design, ergonomics, and AI system are already validated. We’ve completed multiple titanium and polymer prototypes and refined every detail for comfort, precision, and style.

📋 | Specifications

We love health tracking. We just never loved what health trackers looked like.Most wearables feel great at the gym and completely out of place everywhere else. Sooner or later, they get taken off, and once that happens, they're often forgotten. We wanted something different.Lumysi is a wellness braceletdesigned to give youmeaningful health insightswhile looking and feeling like apiece of jewelry.It trackssleep, recovery, heart rate, HRV, activity, temperature, SpO2, and more, so you can stay connected to your health without wearing something that looks like a gadget.Wear it wherever life takes you.And as our early supporters,Kickstarter backersget alifetime subscription for FREE

🎥 | Walkthrough Video

Lumysi was designed around a simple idea: if you're going to wear something every day, it should be something you genuinely love having on your wrist.Comfortableenough to sleep in.Elegantenough for dinner.We obsessed overevery detailand partnered with thedesignerbehind luxury productsconnected to Rolls-Royceto create a health bracelet that feels more like jewelry than tech.But while we refined the design, we never compromised on what matters underneath:the sensors, the data, and the health insightsthat make Lumysi apowerful wellness companion.Because healthtechnology can be powerful and beautiful.

📊 | 30+ Health Metrics

While you work, sleep, train, and move through life, Lumysi is tracking everything that matters.Heart rate, HRV, sleep, recovery, body temperature, SpO2, activity, and cycle trackingandmore. All happening quietly in the background while you go about your day. You need to just wear it and admire it.Over time, the data builds up into something genuinely useful, patterns that tell you how your body is actuallyresponding to your sleep, your stress, your movement, and your lifestyle.

🧪| Beta Testers

🤖 | AI-Powered Insights

Most trackers give you a number and leave you to figure out the rest.Lumysi goes further. Instead of showing you a dashboard of isolated metrics, itconnects your sleep, activity, recovery, and wellness signalsintoone simple daily picture.For example: not just "HRV: 52", but "Your HRV is lower than your 7-day average. You had a shorter sleep window last night and high activity yesterday. Your body may benefit from a lighter day."You get context and direction. You get something you can act on.

🎯 | Precise Accuracy

Lumysi uses aPPG sensor with green, red, and infrared LEDs,the same technology trusted byApple Watch and WHOOP, to continuously read your heart rate, HRV, andblood oxygen levelswithgenuine precision.A dedicated skin temperature sensorquietly tracks yourbody's thermal baselinearound the clock, surfacing subtle shifts that connect torecovery, cycle patterns, and early signs of illness.Anaccelerometercaptures every movement:steps, activity, and the stillnessthat tells the story of your sleep.Every sensor refreshes your data every 30 seconds.This is real, continuous, precise monitoring that builds a picture of your health you can actually trust.

We made a deliberate choice with Lumysi.Lumysi iscompletely screen-free: silent, calm,anddesigned to stay out of your way.Because most wearables come with constant reminders, notifications, and things to check throughout the day.Lumysi tracks your health in the background.
