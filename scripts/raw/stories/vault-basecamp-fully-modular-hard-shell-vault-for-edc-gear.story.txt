Meet VAULT Basecamp

The hard-shell home base for your complete EDC loadout.

Built for tool collectors, modular gear builders, and everyday carry users who want every knife, light, tool, tech kit, and accessory visible, protected, and ready to move.

Modular Gear Page | ShapeSpace Divider | SafeSlot Insert

Swap pages. Shape space. Protect gear. Expand your loadout as your setup grows.

Modular Gear Page - Swap Pages. Build Any Loadout.

Turn loose gear into a clear, visible, and removable loadout.Organize your tools, lights, blades, tech, maps, cards, and repair parts by category, purpose, or mission.

When your setup changes,remove, replace, hang, or rebuild the page.Everything stays in place, visible, and ready to grab.

ShapeSpace Divider - Move the Dividers. Shape the Space.

Create custom space for bigger, boxed, or irregular gear.Move the divider strips, resize the layout, and build compartments around tech kits, chargers, camera accessories, tool boxes, emergency kits, fishing gear, outdoor gear, and more.

No fixed layout. No wasted space.Just the shape your loadout needs.

SafeSlot Insert - Separate Your Gear. Protect every piece.

Give every valuable tool its own protected slot.SafeSlot Insert keeps blades, flashlights, pens, coated tools, and precision gear separated, secured, and easier to access.

Built to reduce rubbing, scratches, bumps, and messy storage —so the gear you carry, collect, and rely on stays better protected.

Expandable from 13L to 18L

Start compact. Expand when the setup grows.VAULT Basecamp expands from 13L to 18L, giving you more room for extra pages, larger kits, tech gear, repair tools, emergency items, and full-loadout storage.

Keep it clean for daily organization, or expand it into a complete gear station. Mix Modular Gear Pages, ShapeSpace Divider, and SafeSlot Insert to build the layout your gear needs.

Hard-Shell Protection for Serious Gear

A serious gear system needs a serious shell.VAULT Basecamp uses a structured EVA hard shell wrapped in durable 300D fabric, giving your loadout a stronger outer layer while keeping the inside fully modular.

Soft pouches carry gear. VAULT Basecamp protects, organizes, and structures it — helping tools, lights, blades, tech, small kits, and precision gear stay better protected from pressure, bumps, and messy storage.

Turn the back of VAULT Basecamp into a stable work surface.Take out the Workbench Tray, place it onto the rear EVA groove, and create a quick setup for maintenance, sorting, and small parts.

Use it for knife care, flashlight maintenance, tiny screws, bits, spare parts, cleaning cloths, lubrication, and EDC tune-ups. No more rolling screws. No more lost parts. No more messy setup.

Built to Stack, Store, and Set Up Smarter

Create a clean gear system with multiple VAULT Basecamps.Stack dedicated setups for EDC, tools, tech, outdoor, emergency, or repair — then store them neatly and grab the one you need.

Grab It. Shoulder It. Take the Whole Setup.

Your gear home base is ready to move.Use the handle for quick carry, or attach the shoulder strap when you need to take the full setup farther — from shelf to vehicle, home to workshop, or gear station to campsite. When one pocket tool is not enough, carry the whole loadout.
