Risks and challenges
Risk: Technical Delivery & File Access
The biggest challenge with digital rewards is ensuring that all backers receive their files securely, quickly, and without technical issues.

Our Solution: The core 3D models, structural engineering, and tolerances for the HEX-SYNAPSE table are already fully developed, prototyped, and tested. There is no manufacturing risk. To ensure a smooth delivery, we will use established, secure digital distribution platforms to host the files. Backers will receive clean, organized STL files immediately after the campaign ends and funds are cleared.

Challenge: Slicing & Print Settings
Every 3D printer is tuned differently. Varied filament brands and machine tolerances can sometimes lead to tight fits on highly precise parts like our 25mm slim drawer.

Our Solution: To minimize printing errors, we will provide a comprehensive printing guide alongside the files. This includes optimal print settings, recommended wall thicknesses, and advice on choosing the right print bed (such as utilizing a smooth PEI plate for the ultimate high-gloss finish). Additionally, we will host an active Discord community where backers can share settings, troubleshoot prints, and get direct technical support from me.
