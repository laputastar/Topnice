XERA Event Horizon

The idea for Event Horizon began with a simple question:If the universe made a clock, what would it look like?

I've always been fascinated by time, not just as something we measure, but as something we experience. Over the years I found myself thinking about concepts such as time as a dimension and the idea of the block universe, where past, present, and future may all exist together.While exploring these ideas, one object kept standing out above all others.A black hole.Few things in the universe are more closely connected to our understanding of time. Around black holes, time itself behaves differently. The closer you get, the stranger it becomes.That led to another question:If the ultimate time object in the universe is a black hole, what would a watch inspired by one look like?The answer became Event Horizon.The watch is built around a simple concept: a black hole with hands.The deep black centre represents the darkness of a black hole, while the luminous ring surrounding it is inspired by the glowing accretion disc of matter spiralling around the event horizon. The result is a clean and distinctive design that carries a deeper story beneath the surface.With your support, Event Horizon can move from prototype to production and become the first watch released under the XERA name.

Event Horizon was designed to balance a distinctive concept with everyday practicality. While its inspiration comes from one of the most extreme objects in the universe, its purpose is simple: to be a watch that people genuinely enjoy wearing.

The 39mm case size was chosen to provide a comfortable fit on a wide range of wrists, offering enough presence to stand out without feeling oversized. A sapphire crystal protects the dial from everyday wear, while a screw-down crown and 100 metres of water resistance ensure the watch is built for daily use.

At the heart of Event Horizon is the Seiko VH31 movement. Unlike a traditional one-second quartz tick, the VH31 produces a smooth sweeping seconds hand, giving the watch a more refined and mechanical feel while retaining the reliability and accuracy of quartz.

The watch is paired with a durable black FKM rubber strap, chosen for its comfort, flexibility, and resistance to heat, sweat, and everyday wear.

Event Horizon was never intended to be a display piece that spends its life in a watch box. It was designed to be worn, used, and enjoyed every day.

By day, Event Horizon presents a clean, minimalist appearance centred around the deep black dial and subtle ring detail.

By night, the watch transforms.

The C3 X1 luminous ring and hands come to life, creating a striking halo effect inspired by the glowing accretion disc that surrounds a black hole.

Minimalist by day. Alive at night.

The result is not only highly legible in low-light conditions, but also reinforces the core idea behind Event Horizon: a black hole with hands.
